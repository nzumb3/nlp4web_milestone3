The Play
###BREAK###
A famous theatre group in Kolkata is ravaged when the members of the group gets killed one by one in a span of one night. Only three of the members survive Mainak(Indrasish Roy), Ishika(Mumtaz Sorcar) and Rishabh(Rajdeep Gupta). The task of finding out the truth falls on Mainak, but the answers he was looking for are not the answers he will get.
