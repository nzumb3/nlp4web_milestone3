Life After Mrs. de Nijs
###BREAK###
When Mrs. de Nijs passes away, she's relieved to be dead. To her surprise this is not the end. She meets Gabriel who asks her to join the Hereafter Agency for Volunteers. Mrs. de Nijs agrees and gets the case of Mercedes, a young taxidriver with some peculiar habits. She also carries a large box with her with a dead dog in it. It will be a long and important day for both women.
