Bike Cops of Haycraft City
###BREAK###
The film tells the story of young Bike Cop recruit Jerry Spacecraft (Nik Tidquist) and his mentor, veteran Bike Cop Anthony Nettling (Daniel Calwell). Things are complicated when Jerry falls in love with Toni Greene (Janna Drake), infuriating Chase Haycraft (Jeremy Drake), son of Haycraft City Mayor, Jay V. Haycraft (Dan Kehde).
