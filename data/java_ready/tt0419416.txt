Abo Ali
###BREAK###
Undermined by poverty the lead character, Hassan, resolves to theft in order to feed his family. While on the run from the authorities Hassan stumbles across a young fugitive named Salma who's escaping the sexually harassing grips of her step father. Unexpected twists and turns in events take place through out the story ranging from false murder accusations to intense love bonds between the characters.
