Graffiti In/Out
###BREAK###
Graffiti on buildings used to be the sign of a neglected neighborhood, however the Wynwood area in Miami has become an example of a neighborhood revitalized by graffiti art. The film shows the story of local and out of town artists working in Wynwood, their interactions with developers and business owners in the area. The Artists also discuss legal versus illegal ways of promoting their graffiti, the messages they convey to the public through graffiti and whether they can make a living off of their art.
