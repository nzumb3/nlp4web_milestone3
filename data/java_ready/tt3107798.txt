Rambo
###BREAK###
Rambo is a bizarre comedy that revolves around a trickster called Kitty.(Sharan) Living with his mother and employing his uncle in his car broker business causes much hilarity and with weird superstitions such as a vehicle can not dash against a pig nor a pig dash against a vehicle makes for riotous fun! Starring Sharan with Madurai and Sadhu Kokila.
