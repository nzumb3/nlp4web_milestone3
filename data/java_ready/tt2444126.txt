Partideiros
###BREAK###
Musician and producer Antonio Galante was so impressed the first time he met sambas 'Aniceto do Imperio' that 25 years later he promoted a samba improvisation session, called 'roda de samba de partido alto', which became a live CD and movie. This film directed by Luiz Guimaraes de Castro, has an important improvisation samba style.
