Komatose
###BREAK###
Nostalgia comes to vivid life in the cinematic fever dream of Komatose. When Perry is introduced to a powerful new substance called Komatic, he is given the opportunity to relive his most treasured memories. Delving further into his past, Perry is thrust into a nightmarish journey into his deepest and most startling obsessions. Komatose is a dark and hypnotic film-going experience you won't soon forget
