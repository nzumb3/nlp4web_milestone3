Ende der Schonzeit
###BREAK###
"Ende der Schonzeit", is set in 1942 and tells the story of Fritz and Emma who are hiding Albert, a Jewish refugee at their remote farm in the black forest. Since his marriage with Emma has remained childless, Fritz uses this opportunity to suggest an unorthodox deal: He asks Albert to sleep with his wife and conceive a child on his behalf. Against the background of war and escape an unpredictable drama of jealousy and sexuality unfolds that turns offenders into victims and vice versa.
