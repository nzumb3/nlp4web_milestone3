Prey - Stille meine Liebe
###BREAK###
The first documentary film about the operatic baritone Hermann Prey, with exclusive private photos and film footage never previously screened as well as unpublished concert recordings. The film offers a fascinating and emotionally charged view of the life of Hermann Prey on and off the international stage. Fellow musicians, friends and family members talk about the singer as an important artist, human being, husband and father who followed his calling and dedicated his entire life to music. A life of joy and drama, love and longing, success and obsession.
