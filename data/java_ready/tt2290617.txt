Resolution
###BREAK###
"Resolution"; a dorky comedy about one mans quest to fufill a New Years Resolution. Begining on 12/31/2010 and continuing through to 12/31/2011, this movie tracks the progress of Paul who has vowed to stop joking, clowning and horsing around. Come along for the hilarious 365 day journey. Made on a shoestring budget, this movie was shot on HD video and runs approx. 95 mins.
