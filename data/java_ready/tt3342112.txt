Uncementing My Heart
###BREAK###
Mayan Priest and Master Teacher, Miguel Angel Vergara leads Canadian surgeon, Dr. Virginia Calverley through three initiations in the Yucatan, Mexico. Dr. Calverley is tired of the hospital environment and feels like there is something different that she can be doing but before she can move forward, she must discover a block deep in herself that has been holding her back.
