Delhi Boom!
###BREAK###
An unusual tale of two brothers whose lives change dramatically following an encounter with a woman named Jennifer. Acting as catalysts, subsequent events unwittingly change each brother's destiny in irreparable ways. Set in one day on the streets of India's capital, what follows is a hard-hitting perspective of a world that comes crashing down on both brothers as consequences come full circle by 5pm.
