Albino United
###BREAK###
In Tanzania where albino's are killed because of traditional witchcraft beliefs, Albino United, an albino soccer team, come together and join the national league in an attempt to dispel the myths surrounding themselves. The film follows the amazing journey of the players as they travel to different epicenters of gruesome albino murders. Despite all expectations, the team finish in the top of the league and become a national phenomenon, proving to the masses that albinos are not only good footballers but that they are humans in their own right.
