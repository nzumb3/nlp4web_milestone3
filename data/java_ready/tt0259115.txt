It Will End Up in Tears
###BREAK###
Sharon is living with her fed-up partner, Ronit, who after finding some photo's Sharon took of their beautiful neighbor, throws her and her young son to the streets. She meets the same neighbor that night after working a double shift as a chef at a local restaurant. Michal offers her a place to stay and they get to know each other a bit better. Michal drops the affair she has been having with an insensitive married man and enters a regime of chemotherapy for a malignant tumour.
