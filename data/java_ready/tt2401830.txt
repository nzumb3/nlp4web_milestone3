The Hunt for Good Americans
###BREAK###
'The Hunt for Good Americans' is an experimental, absurdest fairy-tale, which follows two women, Abby and Tori, who leave the safety of their bubble to find the meaning of love through experience and human interaction. The film is a satirical portrait of American culture and the various stereotypes which make up the vast, American landscape. The protagonists navigate this journey using their imagination, and with fearless naivete, learn lessons from colorful characters along the way.
