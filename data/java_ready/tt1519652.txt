Path of Anna
###BREAK###
This film follows Anna, who contracted HIV from her ex-husband ten years ago. Anna marries Pom whom she met at a hospital. She makes her living by selling eggs at a marketplace every morning. During the day, she takes care of HIV-positive orphans with Pom. Looking ahead to their future, Anna completes many mother's duties for her daughter in her teens. Anna tries her best to be a mother, a wife and a member of her family.
