Masks 50 minutes
###BREAK###
An emotional story about dropping disguises, Masks tells of an American bachelor living on a remote island off Hong Kong to escape the privileged life he once knew. A secret passion helps to ease his loneliness. Then an unexpected phone call disrupts everything, leading to the unwanted arrival of a needy 12-year-old nephew once abandoned as a baby. With no other relative to care for the boy, the reclusive uncle must conquer powerful emotions and accept a big responsibility. Shared pains deeply rooted in the past may destroy or unite them.
