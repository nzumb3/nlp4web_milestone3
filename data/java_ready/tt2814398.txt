Coney Island: Dreams for Sale
###BREAK###
Coney Island, the world renowned and once magnificent amusement park, has survived many battles, but now faces its biggest enemy: corporate greed. An outraged community of local business owners and amusement activists set on a 3 -year roller coaster journey to protect the future of the park from a unscrupulous real estate developer and a group of misguided local politicians.
