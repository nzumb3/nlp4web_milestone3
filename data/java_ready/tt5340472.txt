Luz: Light
###BREAK###
LUZ is the name of a short film that tells the story of a conflictive relationship, of a 20 year old daughter, Luz, who is in search of her personal and professional growth, and her father, Hugo, who is a cocaine addict and an alcoholic, who was abandoned by his wife when their daughter was only four years old, by then a teenage dad, he decided to face the situation alone. It is a love story that cannot move on.
