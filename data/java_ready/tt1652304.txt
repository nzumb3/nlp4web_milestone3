Last Chance
###BREAK###
After spending the last six months trying to pull his life together, Nate decides he is ready to try again with his ex-girlfriend Kim. Unknown to him, she has moved on to another relationship and has no intentions of ever going back to him. After a brief confrontation between the two goes awry, Nate resorts to more extreme measures in order to convince Kim that they belong together.
