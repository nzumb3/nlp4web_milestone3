Badla Jatti Da
###BREAK###
Pregnant Gulabo Kaur is devastated when her entire family is slaughtered by Jaildhar Jung Singh and his goons, when the later finds out that her brother-in-law, Jasbinder, had dared to woo his daughter, Bebo Kaur. Gulabo swears to avenge this humiliation by forcing a marriage between her son and Jung's daughter. She spends the rest of her life training her son, Jagga, to overcome Jung and his goons, but will soon realize that her son will not only have to face-off against Jung, his goons, but also his three sons; a gun-toting daughter, Lalli Kaur, who would rather kill Jagga than wed him; as well as Choudhary Joravar Singh and his son, Shamsher, who wants to wed Lalli at any and all costs.
