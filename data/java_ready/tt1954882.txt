The Art of Killing Bigfoot: A Tragic Story of Love and Loss
###BREAK###
THE ART OF KILLING BIGFOOT exposes the candid world of Bigfoot hunters in central Texas on their passionate quest for the ever-elusive beast. Their seemingly bizarre obsession with the creature, as well as their unique, even disturbing, personal journeys, are chronicled in a series of interviews and live hunting footage by maverick documentary filmmaker JoJo Hoppelfefer.
