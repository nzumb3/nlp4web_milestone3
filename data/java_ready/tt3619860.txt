Her Summer
###BREAK###
Carefully shot details of an old woman's hands, a woman who spent eighty years doing housework, intercut with pictures of the eyes, folds on the clothes or things in the house jointly create a document of closeness, where the details of the household, often shot through various plants, and slight movements around them become mediators on the journey to the world of lonely aesthetic contemplation, where the sense of beauty equals human emotions similarly as in eastern philosophy.
