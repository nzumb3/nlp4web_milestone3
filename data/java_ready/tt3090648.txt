Their Last Weapon
###BREAK###
A caste/community from Western Rajasthan that owes its existence solely to music and Rajasthani folk music owes its existence solely to them. In these times of music mass produced, packaged, marketed and sold with short shelf lives, here are people who have had a torrid affair with music for generations. And like any passionate affair, there is a constant battle of love and hate but what never changes is the high respect for the art.
