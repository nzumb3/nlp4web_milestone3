God's Amazing Grace... Is Just A Prayer Away
###BREAK###
God's Amazing Grace... is just a prayer away tells the heart lifting true story of siblings growing up in 1960's Baltimore. Though the boys are raised in a God-fearing home, both are arrested for violent crimes and both face their respective forks in the road. The brothers each make choices leading to very different life outcomes; but, ultimately, they share the greatest and most sacred bond of all - God's grace and mercy.
