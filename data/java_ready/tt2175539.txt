All I Know
###BREAK###
Documentary about a project called Nordic Co-writers by Roar Amundsen of Denmark, in which, Roar chooses musicians from the Scandinavian countries to come to Engelsholm Castle to co-write songs together and to play those songs at SPOT Festival in Aarhus, Denmark. After a trip to Austin, Texas, to Troy Campbell's House of Songs in November of 2009, Roar decided to bring in American musicians to write with the Scandinavian musicians for the 2010 camp.
