Kameleon
###BREAK###
Two childhood friends that grew up in an orphanage want to save up enough money to buy a home together. They work at night as janitors to find information about lonely desperate woman. Then George, the handsome one, romances them, gets engaged, steals their money and disappears from their life. All is going well till George is intrigued and attracted to a beautiful ballerina. This is where things get interesting and complicated. Remember 'Perception is Reality'
