Micah and Noel Share an Apartment: An Origin Story
###BREAK###
A med student, Micah sets out to obtain independence from his parents when he moves in with his aloof best friend (Duo) only to find out it moving out wasn't what is was cracked up to be. Micah soon discovers that there is an additional roommate in the house (Noel) who he has nothing in common with. Sidetracked, old high school crushes, their eccentric landlord, and nagging parents the clock is ticking for these polar opposites to work and live together, and solve a mystery is this hilarious buddy comedy for the ages.
