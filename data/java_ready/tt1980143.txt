Human Interest
###BREAK###
A veteran Journalist and his young Cameraman hike into the isolated wilderness to film a routine 'human interest' story. Their subject is a young Biological Scientist with a point to prove. When her research Assistant disappears and her study animals turn up slaughtered, paranoia sets in. It soon becomes apparent that there is something else out there in the wilderness - something not human.
