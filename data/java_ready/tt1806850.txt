Controlled
###BREAK###
Anna Delgado had the perfect life; wealthy husband adored by all, charming home and beautiful daughter but behind closed doors lies a secret. A terrifying marriage surrounded by power, lust, deceit and control; her world filled with abuse, fear and threats against her daughter. Desperate to escape she turns to the only one she can trust but in a twisted game of cat and mouse, Gregory, relentless in his pursuit will stop at nothing to regain his control
