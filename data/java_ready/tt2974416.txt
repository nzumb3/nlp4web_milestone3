Four Drops of Poison
###BREAK###
Four Drops Of Poison is a feature film consisting of Four Vignettes. The first (Poison Ivy) explores the life of an inner city prostitute. The second (The Blister Sisters) is a documentary about two young sisters living in Hankins, NY.The third in the series (Snot-Groove) is a dark comedy about a Jewish young woman and her personal addictions, and the final (Li Ouanga) explores a modern day tribe of Vodouisants.
