Sonido Bestial
###BREAK###
Without a doubt, Ricardo Ray and Bobby Cruz Ware the most representative musicians of the Salsa sound next to Celia Cruz, Tito Puente, Johnny Pacheco and Hector Lavoe, among others. In the Sixties Richie and Bobby established a before and after in Latin music: Richie, a pianist born in Brooklyn and Bobby, a singer born in Puerto Rico. Together they began one of the most fruitful collaborations in the music history of the Caribbean.
