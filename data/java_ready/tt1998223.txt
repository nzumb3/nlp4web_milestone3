Frontier Texas!
###BREAK###
The story of the Old West in Texas from 1780 to 1880. This feature, narrated by Buck Taylor, tells the story of the people who lived and died on the Texas frontier in the region surrounding what is now Abilene, Texas. The story cover the lives of Indians, early settlers, frontier military, cattle drives, the Butterfield Stage Line, outlaws and lawmen.
