Status and Trends in Arctic Biodiversity
###BREAK###
The Arctic has entered into a cycle of intensive pressure and change involving a new set of challenges and stressors with climate change at the forefront. The magnitude of change will exert major influences on biological dynamics in the Arctic. To meet these challenges, the Arctic Council Working Group on the Conservation of Arctic Flora and Fauna (CAFF) set out to provide policymakers and conservation managers with the best available scientific and traditional knowledge on Arctic biodiversity. This video presentation will focus on highlighting the key issues that surfaced in this report.
