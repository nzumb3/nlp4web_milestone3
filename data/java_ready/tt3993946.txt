Meeting the Wickersons
###BREAK###
A dark comedy that paints an unusually captivating portrait of an intriguing interview, where a parapsychologist, David Harvey, uncovers the secrets of the extrasensory perception phenomenon, remote viewing, through a clairvoyant, Eva Wickerson, and unexpectedly becomes a player in a dark secret housed in the walls of the Wickerson home.
