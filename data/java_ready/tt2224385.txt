Origins III: Destiny
###BREAK###
Four days after the events of Star Wars: Origins II: The Rising, the forces of darkness are bent on finding the final Light and destroying the very essence of life in the galaxy. The Jedi are scattered. Jedi Master Kyle is now under the control of the mysterious beast and forced to do the bidding of Elroy and the Dark Lord Jack. He now heads off to conduct an interrogation of his old apprentice, Clark. Meanwhile, Jedi Knight Ross has been captured. His condition is unknown. If the forces of evil are to be stopped, Ross is going to need help from the wise old Bill and his daughter Julie, assuming Ross can escape his captures before its too late.
