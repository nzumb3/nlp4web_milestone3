Worst Case Scenario

Olga is a costume designer. Six weeks ago, she split up with Georg: film director, artist, dick head. Now they are both somewhere in Poland, trying to shoot a comedy. The script is awful, the producer has resigned, the sound man has run away, and now the lead actor wants to go. And, oh yes, Olga is pregnant. A film about a film that never really stood a chance.
