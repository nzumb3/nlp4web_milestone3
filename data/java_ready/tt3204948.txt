Puti
###BREAK###
Amir Luna is a successful counterfeit painter. He has made a lot of money selling his forgeries in the art black market. But his life changes when he gets into a car accident with his 8-year old son. Amir survives the accident but suffers a head injury that renders him color blind. His son is comatose. His world collapses as he tries to overcome this twin tragedy. While recuperating, strange things start happening to him.
