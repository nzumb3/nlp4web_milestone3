Mondo Montreal
###BREAK###
The hidden life of a big city conceals secrets such as Satanic masses, snake worshipers, massage parlors, women's lingerie direct sales, the therapeutic pyramid, extra-terrestrial visits, chiromancy, and strange cults: all these phenomena attract the filmmaker's attention. Exotic dancers are given top billing in this exploration of drug addicts, prostitutes and protagonists engaging in various unusual underground activities in Montreal and surrounding area.
