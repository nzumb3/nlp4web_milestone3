The Dark Side: Rise of Darkness
###BREAK###
An action packed, mind twisting cliff hanger exposing the grand influence of the media and money in today's world. What was once based on discipline, truth, Honour, and Quality, has changed to marketing and money making schemes for the sake of; power/money, fame, increased corruption and respect/reputation. Who do you go to when you need help? If there is no one to be trusted.
