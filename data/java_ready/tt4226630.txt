My Heart Is Not Broken Yet
###BREAK###
This film tells the story of the trial and struggle of Grandma Song Sin-do who has continually campaigned for redress in the issue of the comfort women drafted into sexual slavery for Japanese troops during the war.Although she lost the trial, her statement that My soul is undefeated reveals her desire to fight independently against the scars caused by the war and historical prejudice.
