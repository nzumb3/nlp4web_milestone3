Bonneville: Wide Open
###BREAK###
"Bonneville: Wide Open" explores the unique and enthusiastic community of Bonneville Salt Flat racers whose garage creations include everything from powered barstools to 250 mph diesel trucks. They tell their stories against the surreal backdrop of the barren sun-baked salt flats. The viewer will meet many of the creative, unusual characters who populate the pits during Speed Week and will enjoy the visceral sense of accomplishment through each team's struggle.
