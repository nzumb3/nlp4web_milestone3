ADHD. Rust, Reinheid en Ritalin
###BREAK###
A calm documentary about a hyper subject. Tiffany is 12 and almost incapable of concentrating. The parents of Yoram (8) think he is fidgety and incoherent. Both families decide to have their children tested at the ADHD clinic. The filmmakers followed the two families for almost 2 years.
