Night of a Thousand Screams
###BREAK###
A married couple celebrating 10 years of marriage and 4 college students on Spring Break stop at "Carol's Bed and Breakfast". Once there, they discover that their hostess isn't what she seems to be and soon they are attacked by zombies hungry for human flesh. The ones that get to survive the first attack must band together if they want to escape alive.
