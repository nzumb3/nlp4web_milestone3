Hands in the Mist
###BREAK###
A short documentary in collaboration with with the Latin American NGO, Un Techo para mi Pais and its Innovation Center. The film follows the Designmatters Safe Agua Peru project, in which Art Center students co-created innovative design solutions to overcome water poverty with families living in Cerro Verde, a 30,000-person slum [Asentamientos] perched on the hillsides surrounding Lima, Peru.
