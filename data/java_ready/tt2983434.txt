Naari Chakra
###BREAK###
The story revolves around the chakras of women trafficking and women empowerment in the society, with a message for all women to never give up and fight back. The film highlights a story of a girl being abducted and forcefully introduced into the prostitution racket. Her brother who is an army officer unravels her kidnapping, and gets her out of the whole underground plot. The story then moves on to the healing part of the protagonist, her intellectual fight against the society, and rehabilitation by instilling yogic values in Sandhya.
