Hurrah
###BREAK###
Ever since the horrific death of his fiancee, Raoul has turned his back on life. He lives in total isolation at the decrepit, derisively named homestead, Hurrah. Grief eats away at him relentlessly, just as the sun tortures the desolate land. And then a high-heeled apparition appears from the endless haze of the horizon. Julia. Who is she? What are her secrets? As passion ignites and love begins to heal, mysteries unravel to reveal the heart of this enigmatic love story.
