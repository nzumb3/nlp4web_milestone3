Like Me
###BREAK###
LIKE ME is a feature length documentary that follows NYC comedian and actor Micah Sherman on his often hilarious journey of self discovery as he tries to find his voice on social media. Along the way he gets advice and feedback from smart, talented folks like Jim Gaffigan and Janeane Garofalo who try to help him figure out if all the stress, sharing and work of maintaining an online presence is really worth it anyway.
