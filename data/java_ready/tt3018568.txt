Zoom! - Tucson's Late '50s Rock 'n' Roll Record Label
###BREAK###
The endearing story of two teenage boys in Tucson, Arizona who, in early 1959, created one of southern Arizona's first rock 'n' roll record labels, Zoom Records. Follow Burt Schneider and Ray Lindstrom as they recount the story of their youthful adventures as teenage record executives, recruiting talent, working in the studio with one of rock 'n' roll's most renowned recording engineers, and releasing their 45-rpm discs into the Tucson marketplace and beyond. A compelling testament to youthful entrepreneurship and the power of music to change lives in positive ways.
