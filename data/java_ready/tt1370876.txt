Right to Exit
###BREAK###
The first documentary produced about Dr. Jack Kevorkian since a Michigan court convicted him of a murder for helping a terminally ill patient end his life. A serious, well-researched study of the trial, the politics and euthanasia, featuring interviews with the attorneys, the prosecution and the witnesses. Includes an interview with CBS investigative reporter Mike Wallace (60 minutes) and an exclusive phone interview with Dr. Kevorkian from his jail cell.
