4 Young Barents Dox
###BREAK###
A four-documentary mini series produced by Filmpool Nord in the Barents region with four young directors from Norway, Sweden, Finland, and Russia each directing a 28-minute documentary of their choice. Includes Anton by Reuben Sletten, Trackback by Anette Niia, Before the Flood by Mika Ronkainen, and Jazz de Gas by Sergej Jigaltsov.
