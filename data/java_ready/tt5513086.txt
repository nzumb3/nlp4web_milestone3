Inferno and Road to Inferno

'Inferno'' is a serious yet at times funny coming of age story. It's about learning not just to cope with an abusive upbringing but turning those demons into muses so that Dante can inspire others and breathe hope into a world, a neighborhood, and a family that is sometimes lacking any.
