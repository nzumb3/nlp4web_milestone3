03:34 Terremoto en Chile
###BREAK###
Directed by Juan Pablo Ternicier, this moving film portrays three different but interconnected stories that take place in the wake of the devastating 8.8-magnitude earthquake that rocked Chile in February 2010. Filmed on location in affected zones, all profits from the film will go to school reconstruction projects in areas of need.
