Nurse shimai: Momoiro shinsatsu-shitsu
###BREAK###
NURSE SISTERS is a drama about relationships and commitment starring the sizzling Sasaki Sisters (yes! They are real-life sisters!) Both sisters star as young nurses who struggle to make ends meet. They both have relationship troubles and career woes. Problems start when one of the sisters starts a relationship with a doctor she works for, who happens to be married. A big hit with Cosplay fans!
