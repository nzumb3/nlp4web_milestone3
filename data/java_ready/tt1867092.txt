The Road Trip USA
###BREAK###
15,000 miles, 34 states, and plenty of adventure along the way... 3 friends from San Diego decide to restore a classic convertible and hit the open road. Their goal: to rediscover the American dream and spread the hope of Christ across the homeland. RTUSA takes viewers along for this life-changing journey with Chris, Troy and Kuno, 3 hilarious characters armed with little more than their faith and a few bucks for gas.
