What Is Hidden, May Be Found
###BREAK###
What Is Hidden, May Be Found takes its audience to South America, opening up a world of culture, antiquity, myth and adventure. Archaeologist Robert Farrugia delves into the lesser-known cultures and history of the region around Ecuador and Peru, seeking to explore off-the-beaten tracks and find that which may be hidden. Robert acquaints us with tales and legends of South American exploration. His adventures see him traveling along remote roads and rivers finding figurines, idols and other artifacts, following in the footsteps of the explorers of old. Robert finds a wealth of items, information, new sites and discusses how and why this journey could be made; how the 'little guy' can become an adventurer and that there really is much more waiting to be found.
