The Oracle of Del-Fi
###BREAK###
The legendary music producer Bob Keane of Del-Fi Records speaks about his tumultuous and iconic career spanning 50 years in the record business. Bob who was portrayed in the film La Bamba, tells all about the discovery and behind the scene stories of Ritchie Valens, Sam Cooke, Barry White, Frank Zappa, Bobby Fuller, and many other of his Rock'n Roll Hall of Fame discoveries. From the rise and fall of Ritchie Valens to the mysterious Hollywood murder of Bobby fuller, hear the stories that have never been told until now. To find out more information about Bob Keane visit www.Del-fi.com and keep checking the site for updates and clips from the film The Oracle of Del-Fi.
