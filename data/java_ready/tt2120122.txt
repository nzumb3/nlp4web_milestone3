Pour vous servir
###BREAK###
"At Your Service" takes you to the heart of the action, and most of all makes you feel how riders and cameramen share the same emotions. Like a collection of short stories, the movie tells how they live together and share the joys and the hassles of the everyday, the trips, the exhaustion, the laughter... And how one has to be able to see with the eyes of the other, to the point he becomes him, to catch this magic moment when the light shines. And thus give us, through the images, the sensation of being the one flying over the mountain...
