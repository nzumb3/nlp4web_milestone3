Gran Sueno a los 77 is a documentary about one mans 60 year quest to be world champion in the decathlon. Raul is 75. He lives modestly in Montevideo, Uruguay. He has never been a world champion in anything. He wants to be a world champion in the decathlon for his age. It was shot over a 3 year period from June 2009 to April 2012 in Uruguay, Finland, and the United States.
