Changing Sands
###BREAK###
Abu Dhabi, the capital of the UAE, has gone through an enormous transformation in the past 60 years. Changing Sands, the first feature documentary to be produced by PyraMovies, is a breathtaking film that will give viewers a glimpse into the amazing history and future of a part of the world that is destined to be on everyone's lips for many years to come.
