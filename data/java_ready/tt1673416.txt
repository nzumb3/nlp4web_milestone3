Linked: The Dream-Creativity Connection
###BREAK###
This documentary explores various perspectives concerning the phenomenon [the link between dreams and creativity] that has inspired the director's vision for a Dream-Arts Community Center. The SIRB approved research footage included this documentary takes place: at the Idyllwild Arts Academy, all over New York City, Los Angeles, and Ashland, Oregon. It can be described as a phenomenological urban ethnography, with a cinema verite style. It explores the link between dreams and creativity, through subjectively described experiences of 20 unique individuals. Director Angel K. Morgan narrates chapters of the film with a script and artistic style that she dreamed. There is recorded music in the film that was the product of her dreams and therefore, the documentary itself is some of her own dream-creativity in action.
