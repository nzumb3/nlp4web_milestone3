Murder in the Cassava Patch
###BREAK###
MURDER IN THE CASSAVA PATCH is a mystery about a young man GORTOKAI who's accused of killing his wife TENE. He's imprisoned but a mystery shrouds the circumstances of her death. Set in a village in post-war Liberia, the film is based on Liberia's most acclaimed novel.
