Midnight Sun
###BREAK###
Nick is a new Asst. Professor at MSU. To save money, he rents a room from Steven, only to find out later that Steven is a Vampire. With a series of gruesome murders striking campus, Nick has Steven arrested, only to find out that he's not the guilty party. Soon Steven, Nick, and Father Chris, a friend of Nick's, find themselves knee deep in blood and guts as they, with the aid of a Vampire Hunting Biker Chick, battle an army of vampires.
