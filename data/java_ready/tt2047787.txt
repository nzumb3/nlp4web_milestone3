Mayhem Behind Movies
###BREAK###
Mayhem Behind Movies is a fly on the wall mockumentary following Kevin Knight, a struggling low-budget filmmaker, on his mission to complete his first big-break movie Valentine before the opening premiere. Kevin soon finds himself in turmoil as he races against the clock in a make or break scenario packed with side-splitting and nail-biting moments. Will Kevin finish in enough time with all the odds stacked up against him?
