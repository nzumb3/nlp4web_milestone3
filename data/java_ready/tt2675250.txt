By portraying the deactivation of the buildings that once housed the old cinemas in the center of the city and neighborhoods Belo Horizonte , the documentary Metropolis intends to take the spectator to reflect on the need to recover the collective memory of a community, and the transience that govern the social-cultural processes and personal relations in society.
