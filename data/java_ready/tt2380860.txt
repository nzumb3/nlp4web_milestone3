A Year and a Day
###BREAK###
An unusual story of The Flills who are a successful, well respected working class Glaswegian family who founded and run the local community centre. Mikey Flills is the backbone of the family, who selflessly keeps the family together and happy with his enormous charm and huge personality. But Mikey has many secrets, which lead to him leaving his family behind without any explanation. He returns a year later to make amends. He visits his fathers' grave to speak to him before he returns to his broken family to give them answers. Will he atone? What devastation will he find? How will the family react
