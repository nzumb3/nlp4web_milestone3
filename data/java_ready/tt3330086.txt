Olive Green
###BREAK###
Running away from her past, Olive Green, a dashing professional art thief, takes on a job of stealing an impressionist masterpiece from the private house of a British collector. But the security systems are the least of her problems, as she will have to deal with dangerous, high-profile criminals, as well as her own ill-fated love. Will Olive succeed in fixing the mess she got herself into?
