The Merry Graingers
###BREAK###
SAMANTHA GRAINGER, comes home to her family's buffalo ranch after being MIA for seven years. Her plan - bury dad and get out of dodge like a lightening bolt. But, a surprise visit from her Cuban boyfriend, a flat tire and a missed plane forces her to spend Christmas with her icy family who only begin to de-thaw while rounding up buffalo. Can this family become friends?
