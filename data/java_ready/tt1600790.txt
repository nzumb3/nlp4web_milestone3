Multiplayer
###BREAK###
multiPlayer is a documentary on those computer gamers who insist that playing against other people is far superior to playing against the computer. All over the world as many as 500 young males bring their computers together for long days of gaming. International competitions are now running. Terrorists and Counter-terrorists are now the good guys/bad guys, cheating is rife and piracy is commonplace.
