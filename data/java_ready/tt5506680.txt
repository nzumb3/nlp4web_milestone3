Kaavala
###BREAK###
Incorporating various factual events sourced from reports in the media,the movie emphasizes on the issues and consequences of teenage aberrance. Feminist style of approach is used throughout the movie to potentially concentrate on the issues faced by teenage girls. The movie focuses on the need for the solemn guardianship by patrons and stresses on need for responsible conduct by teenage students . It also sheds light on the ill-effects of Endosulfan in the Kasaragod region.
