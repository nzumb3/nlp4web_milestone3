Fading Heritage
###BREAK###
In the island of Palawan, Philippines the leader of a tribe struggle to protect his traditions as his village is stuck in between a palm oil plantation and a nickel mine. They live in harmony with the nature but now their ressources are destroyed by these two industries. Fading heritage is a poignant dialogue between a father and his son they both share fear for the future of their tribe.
