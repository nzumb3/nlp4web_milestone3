Struggle for Existence
###BREAK###
This is the story of the last Wildlife Rescue Centre standing in London. Barry and June dedicated their life to the centre they founded more than 20 years ago, but due to the lack of funding every day becomes a battle. The film investigates the relationship Barry and June have with the animals they rescue, and looks at the struggle for survival of both the animals and the centre.
