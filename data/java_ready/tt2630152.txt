Mud Slinging
###BREAK###
Over 30 artists and activists participated in a mud stencil action on June 6th, 2009 in Chicago. The event was organized by the Tamms Year Ten coalition, the Milwaukee artist Jesse Graves, and Nicolas Lampert of the Justseeds Radical Artists Cooperative. The action was designed to bring more public attention to the human rights abuses at the Tamms supermax prison in southern Illinois.
