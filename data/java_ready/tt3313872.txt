Moda e Amore
###BREAK###
Rossella is a young top-model. She is in love with a young costume designer, Stefano, but is always stressed out by her manager, Filippo, that would impose the classic trade-offs in the world of fashion and entertainment. This causes confusion and discomfort, and brings the girl quickly to dependence on drugs and alcohol, and leads her to self-destruction.
