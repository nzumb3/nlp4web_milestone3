Sinus
###BREAK###
A black comedy from a group of friends living in Oslo, describing on hell of a week. The 7 friends are followed over 7 days, where 7 different actions changes their life radically. After the 7 days their relationship is over. Edgar, with an IQ of 180, tells the story, giving chaos theory and God the responsibility for him using drugs, smoking and drinking coffee, not doing something important. His gang of friends searches for a meaningful life.
