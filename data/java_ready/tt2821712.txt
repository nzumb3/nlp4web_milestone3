The Whale Sings
###BREAK###
This personal documentary tells an amazing story about how eight time serial entrepreneur from Silicon Valley, retired to the big Island of Hawaii, turned a hobby, recording whale songs, into a billion-dollar business building the first ocean going self-contained robot. He did this all because he couldn't get good quality whale songs recorded using his hydrophone. It is a movie that tells its story in a unique home movie style. It will find audiences of all ages interested in the environment, in entrepreneurs, in the oceans, and in robots, mesmerized.
