Walk Through the Valley
###BREAK###
A captivating story about love, infidelity, duplicity and lies! A man's double life with the two women he loves. One woman is of high respectability; his wife, suddenly faced with the fight of her life; the struggle of trying to save her name, reputation and her liberty. The other; his lady friend, a woman of great strength and weakness; living a life of ill repute according to the critics. Both are suffering from emotional grief that may haunt them for the rest of their lives. The one man who can get to the truth is faced with deceptions, diversions and confusion, while his lover, his opposition, risks losing him. This is a courtroom drama about crime of passion.
