Collateral Damages II
###BREAK###
'Collateral Damages II' revisits the lives of several characters first seen in 'Collateral Damages', a 2003 documentary chronicling the lives of firefighters from three New York City firehouses in the aftermath of September 11Th. Ten years after the attacks on the World Trade Center, After testing positive during a random drug screening, Fireman Al Sicigiano has been thrown into an unprecedented legal battle with the FDNY, a battle whose outcome will determine his future as a firefighter as well as a husband and father. Al's trials and tribulations over the past 10 years have strained nearly all his relationships, both personal and professional and left him in a state of limbo, with little knowledge of an impending decision that will change his life forever.
