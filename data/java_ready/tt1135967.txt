Deadmeat
###BREAK###
Ex-con Clarkie is betrayed by his best friend, he has no one to turn to as he tracks down his brothers killers. The war spills onto London's mean streets. If Clarkie makes one false move he is...DEADMEAT. Get ready for a thrilling ride as our hero finds out that his female lawyer has a crush on him and gangs have joined forces to kill him. What would you do if your best friend betrayed you?
