Azul Magia
###BREAK###
In a remote part of the island of Dominican Republic, away from all traces of technology and modernity, we have our hero Moises. He decides to embark on an adventure through the depths of the forest in search of a mystical blue fruit that has the healing powers to help his seriously ill mother. On his journey, Moises meets a strange blue haired girl named Bel, who is fleeing from a monster that haunts the forest and wants to eat her. Bel promises Moises to help him find the fruit in change of his protection from the monster.
