The Astronomer
###BREAK###
A group of misfits go to a vacation on the moon, where they discover that they were sent there by the government and cannot get back. Mr. Cavor, who invented the technology that brought them there, must learn to accept his insanity in order to save them all. Meanwhile, a war is brewing on earth. The film is a remake of the historic 1902 silent film Le voyage dans la lune.
