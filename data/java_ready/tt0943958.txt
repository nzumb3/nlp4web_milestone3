Driving with My Wife's Lover
###BREAK###
Tae-han(Park, Kwang-jung) runs a small stamp shop in a small town. One day finding out that his wife is having an affair, he decides to witness his wife's infidelity before his eyes. Knowing that his wife's lover, Joong-sik(Jeong, Bo-seok), is a taxi driver, he goes to Seoul, intentionally gets Joong-sik's cab and asks for a long-distance drive. They encounter various situations; sometimes fighting, sometimes sharing excitements.
