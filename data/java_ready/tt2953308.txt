Final Flight
###BREAK###
After 65 years on the ground, a WWII pilot returns to the sky. Gene Fowler is a 91 year old WWII pilot who started flying when he was 15. It became his passion. But after the trauma of flying 33 missions in the war, he never flew again. Until now. Fly along with Gene as he goes up in a small plane for one last flight, despite the danger he faces due to his advanced age. An uplifting and moving story.
