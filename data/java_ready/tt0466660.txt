After Roberto
###BREAK###
The story of an American man, pursued through northern Italy, confronting the demons outside and within. A mystery. A fantasy. A love story. Roberto, a displaced New Yorker living in Genoa, Italy, finds himself the subject of a mysterious stalker. Who is this man in the red hat? This is the story of one man's conflict between taking responsibility for his actions and holding onto his need for self-preservation. A psychological drama that takes you on a journey through undiscovered locales. We travel with Roberto through the transient streets of Genoa's historic port, complete with unsavory characters, suspense, intrigue and disillusionment. Who is it that is following "after" and, what indeed will follow "After Roberto"?
