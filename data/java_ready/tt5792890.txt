Long Film : old ASIA
###BREAK###
Two friends had a good but dull life without a lot of complaints in Japan. But one day, they decided to change their life forever. They decided to trade their life for the world. No matter what their journey threw at them, they were ready to take the world into their own hands. The journey took them to the furthest points of Asia, Europe and South America. The journey opened their eyes to the kindness of people around the world. The journey was nothing but full of excitement. This is the story of globetrotters discovering the world whilst in search of their purpose in life.
