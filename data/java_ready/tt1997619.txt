Innocent Blood
###BREAK###
James Park, a retired undercover detective, faces his worst nightmare when his young son Cody is kidnapped by a mysterious criminal connected to his dark past. As James begins to unravel the criminal's game of vengeance, he and his wife are forced to reconcile his past transgressions and keep their faith as they fight to bring their son home safely.
