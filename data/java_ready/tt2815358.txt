Vanguard
###BREAK###
Wade is a quiet loner who seemingly leads an unremarkable life. But come night he s haunted by strange nightmares that have begun crossing over into his waking hours. His fragile world explodes when a strange woman visits him. His memories and identity a sham, he rediscovers his true origin as a sleeper agent for an advance invasion force, the Vanguard. Not a man but a machine, he must choose between the woman he loves or fulfill his true destiny as an agent of mass destruction.
