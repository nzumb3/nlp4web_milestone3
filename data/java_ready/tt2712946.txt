Noshta na Mechtite
###BREAK###
Two angel girls - Annie and Lucia, are sent to Earth with a special mission. They have to do the impossible to bring together nine very special kids and discover their most cherished dreams. Normal human relations can hurt for real, as the two girls understand, but this shouldn't be enough to stop them. Unless one of the angels is not an angel anymore.
