David's Apartment
###BREAK###
David's Apartment is a feature length digital improv film about a Jewish man named David Morris, who is planning to sell his apartment and commit a violent action that will alter his life forever. David's plans get complicated when he ends up falling in love with a woman named Michelle. David's brother, Samuel, who is the only one that knows of David's violent intentions, meets Michelle and asks for her help in order to prevent David from doing violence.
