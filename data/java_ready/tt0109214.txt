No, Sir!
###BREAK###
Hilarious sequel to the movies 'Yes Sir' and 'Yes Sir 2,' about the Nationalist soldiers of the Taiwan Army. This is equivalent in the US as M*A*S*H 3. Only this time, instead of being obedient as they were in 'Yes Sir' and 'Yes Sir 2,' it's just the opposite.
