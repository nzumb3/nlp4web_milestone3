Story of a Wild Girl
###BREAK###
Two Bengal Tigers are born at Ankara ZOO in Turkey. They are rejected like the previous cubs by their mother. There was no new-born unit at the Zoo. So Young Veterinarians began to take care of them day and night. Without colostrum (mother milk), cubs survival rate are only 5%. The journey began searching for the right nutrition. The male Tiger has lost his life at the day of 5 th. The female Tigress was a fighter and she is named "Cocojambo".
