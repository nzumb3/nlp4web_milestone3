Drowning in Blood
###BREAK###
Adam, a vulnerable sociopath is reminded of his broken past, when he befriends an experienced serial murderer. Adam is unable to find pleasure in the mundane day to day. When Adam finds an opportunity to try something new, he takes it, even if that opportunity presents itself in the form of murder. Witness a fragile human mind crumple, as it is corrupted by unspeakable actions. Adam will soon discover that more than one person is destroyed by each act of violence.
