The Escape to Hollywood
###BREAK###
The Escape to Hollywood is a poetic documentary about a young storyboard artist who travels to Hollywood to pursue his dream. In an era of Mad Men craze and nostalgia, the film touches the heart of the escapism of our time. The film is a portrait of dreams, how they arise and develop how they drive us forward and stubbornly hang on.
