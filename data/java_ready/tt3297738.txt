Corey's asterisks
###BREAK###
They are the Bad News Bears of women's college basketball. A diabetic coach with a jerry-curl mullet and a potty mouth transforms a group of underdogs, including a 6 foot 6 transexual, a deaf player, a foster child and a gifted star with major attitude, into a team with one goal: to prove everyone who has ever doubted their heart, their talent or their right to play, wrong.
