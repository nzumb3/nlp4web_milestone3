Beyond Nevermore: The Ballad of Jefferson Waller
###BREAK###
Imagine if Jack Kerouac met R. Crumb. 'Beyond Nevermore,The Ballad of Jefferson Waller' takes the viewer inside the fascinating world of the outsider artist Jefferson Waller as he literally draws his way through life. The journey to create the world's longest piece of continuous art becomes a quest to find the heart and soul of America. Shot entirely on location in the majestic Texas Hill Country outside Austin the film features Willie Nelson, Freddy Powers, Danny Terry, The Troubadillos and many others and we join Jefferson going Beyond Nevermore.
