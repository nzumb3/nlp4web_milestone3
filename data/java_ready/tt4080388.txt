Sainte-Agathe-des-Monts, Une ville, des histoires
###BREAK###
Celebrating the 150 years of Sainte-Agathe-des-Monts, the film tells the story of the town with reenactment of key moments in its history, intercut with festivities of the 150th and interviews with historians and other colourful characters.
