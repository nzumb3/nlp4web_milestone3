A Secret Spot
###BREAK###
Secret Spot is the latest film of Nicolas and Loris Falquet. After 15 years traveling the world to produce their free ski movie, the 2 brothers are spending a complete season in THE valley were they learned to ski and film: The Valley of Trient. 15 years of archives and brand new footage will make you discover their career and this magic place!
