Fashion Tension
###BREAK###
New regulations upgrade a small island settlement in Dalmatia into a city and temperamental citizens have a difficult time accepting the new city rules. Things get even more complicated with the arrival of an Italian fashion designer and his favorite male model. Through the climax of utter misunderstanding among the main protagonists, realistic and surrealistic elements of cinematographic narration, the story ends in a great flood of Biblical proportions, leaving the fashion designer, the model, three sheep, two goats and a dozen hens, which happened to be on their ship, the only remaining inhabitants of the planet Earth.
