Los besos
###BREAK###
Geronimo and Lisa are reunited after a time without seeing each other. At that time they were dating and today it does not seem to be so. Both are in their hometown trying to discover that link, but they look like they were tourists learning a language. They share friends: Dante wants to do a movie and Lucile he wants to sing her song. Characters ranging in a summer of indecision, where nothing seems to hurry them.
