Somewhere in the Night
###BREAK###
A crack addicted homeless girl prostitutes herself for drugs and artificial stimulants. In the process she gets impregnated and takes the baby to term, but then abandons it. Years go by and she finally cleans up her act. She's constantly tormented by nightmares of a baby she once had. One day as she's walking home from work, Somewhere In The Night, she gets attacked by three gang members.
