Hollywood Unscripted: A Chaos Theory
###BREAK###
Max is a frustrated infomercial director whose dream is to make A Hollywood masterpiece. Needing a partner in crime, he contacts Sam, a producer in the adult film industry with 200 films under his belt. The only thing holding them back is a script. They try everything imaginable to find a story worth making. Under the guise of an infomercial audition, Max and Sam corral in a variety of actors, polish their egos and solicit ideas for the perfect movie. Watch Max and Sam in a hilarious battle with each other as they try to come up with the next big Hollywood Hit. "Hollywood Unscripted (a chaos theory)" is not only a great experiment, it's about the blues of everyday life in the city of "angels".
