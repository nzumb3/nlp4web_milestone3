The Item
###BREAK###
Texas executes convicts at the state prison in Huntsville, Texas. Tori Brock, a reporter from the local newspaper (The Huntsville Item), observes and reports on each execution. Barring a last minute reprieve from the governor of Texas or from the United States Supreme Court, Timothy Adams is scheduled to die on February 22, 2011 at six o'clock pm. This will be Tori's second scheduled execution within a week.
