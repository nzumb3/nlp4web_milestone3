Tori Nelson: Fighting for Her Future
###BREAK###
A documentary about Tori Nelson, a WBC and WIBA World Multi-Class boxing champion ranked #1 in the world, who struggles with raising two teenage children on three minimum paying jobs while grasping to maintain her professional boxing career. With only her trainer, she has fought to become the #1 USA and World Middleweight and Welterweight title holder. Tori Nelson and her manager hope to gain sponsorship so she can continue her career and help her children with a better life, a life she never had.
