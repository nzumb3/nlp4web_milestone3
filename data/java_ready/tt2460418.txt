Faith Under Fire
###BREAK###
The true story of a community's struggle with loss and devastation after a former school teacher opens fire during a church service in East Texas in 1980. 30 years after that tragic day, survivors share their journey of dealing with trauma that few experience. With candor and vulnerability they share about the shock to there faith, pain of loss, forgiveness, revenge and the memories they relive every time there is a public shooting today. They remember the moments four valiant men raced into the face of bullets with no thought of their own safety, making a way for 350 others to fulfill their lives. Their story journeys from the pit of heartbreak and despair to restoration, ultimately letting go of anger and hatred to find peace again in everyday living.
