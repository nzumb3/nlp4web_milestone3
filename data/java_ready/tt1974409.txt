There Is No Undo
###BREAK###
In a time when words mean little, the easy, thoughtless way they are spoken, sent by text, updated on blogs and social networks, bears no relation to the torment they may cause. Following the death of Emily, a girl she maligned with unbridled cruelty, Laila begins to realize that if you said it, you must have meant it. Increasingly aware of the angry, poisoned talk everywhere she goes, Laila dreads the onslaught of carnage that will surely follow and tries to warn her friends. Someone or something is listing. Just as things cant get worse as her sister vanishes. Was it something she said Now more than ever she realizes, you should be careful what you wish for There Is No Undo
