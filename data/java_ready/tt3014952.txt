An Abstract Mind
###BREAK###
An abstract mind is an alternative independent film based on new york city street art. The goal of this film is to dig back to the roots of what art truly is and what it stands for in today's mainstream media. This is a chance for artist of the underground to find their voice and empower others. YouTube page. We want this film to reach an audience of young artist and filmmakers.
