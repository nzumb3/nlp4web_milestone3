Sacred Rage: The Hellbent Vision of Richard Williams
###BREAK###
Follows the existential angst of a humble, wildly creative artist with Gothic sensibilities struggling to find his place in the modern world, while coping with the suicides of several of his closest friends. Ethereal beings, phantoms, and unsettling creatures inhabit Richard's inner vision, brought to life within the forests, haunted swamps, and supernatural landscapes of Cape Cod. Richard's compulsive need to make art has evolved into a sacred ritual giving his life meaning, even when all hope seems lost.
