A Sicilian Odyssey
###BREAK###
How far would you travel to find your way home? A successful young American writer whose trusted mentor and Editor-in-chief tells her that her writing is lifeless. Devstated, she reveals feeling blocked and left oft of something...but all that changes when she receives a curious key chain and a surprisingly tender birthday card from her mother challenging her to to go Sicily to search for a treasure. Reluctantly, she embarks on an odyssey, feeling alone and unsure of who she is. The Sons and Daughters of Sicily who befriend her share Sicily and their Sicilian way of life as they guide her to the treasure that unlocks her mother's deepest secret, revealing a destiny that changes her life forever.
