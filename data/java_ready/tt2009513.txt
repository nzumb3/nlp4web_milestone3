Life of a Cabbie
###BREAK###
In this short amusing documentary i will take you to a wonderful journey of two cab drivers, who are very excited to share their views on how their lives revolved around their passengers, and cops, who often don't treat them with respect, and politeness. Also they complain about how city authorities and politician are taking advantage of them and our nation. The first interviewer talks about his passion of becoming a nascar driver but ended up becoming a cab driver. He also shares his view on wars, and politicians. On the other hand the 2ND interviewer address issues related with how cops loves to gift them tickets, and often forgets what will happen if they decided to go on strike.
