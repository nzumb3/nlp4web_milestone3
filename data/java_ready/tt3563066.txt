Dristor
###BREAK###
In the middle of Dristor, an infamous neighborhoods in Bucharest, 4 characters are caught in some unusual events of their daily life. Alexandru manages to gets his girlfriend fired from her job, but she has her way to make her boss keep her. Mihai, Alexandru's little brother, decides it's time to make things straight in his relationship with his drug dealer and makes a confession to his mother.
