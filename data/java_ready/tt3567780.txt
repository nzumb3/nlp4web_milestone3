Stray Bullet
###BREAK###
Jack Warner is a shy introverted 28 year old with a broken past and a dead end job. His only friends in the world are Stacy a 27 year old drug counselor and her 7 year old daughter Riley. Jack's already crumbling life comes crashing down around him when a secret from his past puts Stacy and Riley in danger. Now the mob wants him dead and the FBI wants to bring him in. Forced to run with no where to go and a team of relentless killers tracking him every step of the way, Jack is fighting not only for his life but to protect the ones he loves the most.
