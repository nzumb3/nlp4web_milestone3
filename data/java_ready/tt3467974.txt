S.T.A.R.C.H Episode III: Jeff-Andy Alltogether
###BREAK###
S.T.A.R.C.H is an independently created documentary web-series in which young adults from around the country are interviewed on the basis of their possession of a talent and/or involvement in an initiative that the STARCH Team determines to be objectively beneficial to Humanity. Episode 3 featured the first official guest of the series, a very interesting young engineer/musician by the name of Jeff-Andy residing in Boston. So the STARCH team headed there and sat one on one with Jeff, learning of his story, his motivations, his beliefs and of the obstacles he faced along the way.
