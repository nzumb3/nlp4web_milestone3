Reflections: Art for an Oil-Free Coast
###BREAK###
On an expedition organized by the Raincoast Conservation Foundation, 50 artists band together and journey into the heart of Canada's Great Bear Rainforest - to protest a proposed oil-pipeline that threatens the region. By taking up paintbrushes and carving tools to create works depicting the fragility of a remote wilderness, these artists and the film Reflections captures an inspiring act of social and artistic protest.
