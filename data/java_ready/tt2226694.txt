Opposite Blood
###BREAK###
Two brothers, a prominent and respected doctor and an international gun for hire turned businessman, form an ambitious partnership. The two construct a proposal seeking $40 million in government funding for a community project. Soon after funding is approved, a secret alliance between one brother and an anonymous businessman leaves the other murdered and his children without a father. Years later, Jimmy and Tommy, raised apart after their father's murder, begin to question their respective past. Growing up on opposite sides of the law, the two young men battle forces determined to keep the truth of their father's death hidden.
