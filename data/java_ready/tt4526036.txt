Give Earth a Chance
###BREAK###
In this day and age human race has become an alien in its own home. humans have been unable to coexist in a positive manner with the planet. It is the time in which we most decide if we ought to destroy everything its left. Not only the climate will change, but the economy trough the pic of oil will change, society is fragile. This documentary is the critique of a civilization and the proposal of a new one.
