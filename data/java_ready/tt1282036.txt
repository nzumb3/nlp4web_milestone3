Dead Fury
###BREAK###
Led by the wisecracking Max, a group of hunters accidentally stumble into the forested realm of a family of demonic freaks. The freaks are hungry to possess anyone who trespasses on their sacred land, so Max and his friends must fend off the evil enemy with whatever weapons they have at hand. Shotguns, pick-axes, knives, rotary saws, weapons of every description are enlisted to dice, slice and sever.
