Halation
###BREAK###
Halation is a psychological thriller about a young photographer named Alex who moves into an old house in Seattle. It's the picture-perfect place for him, it looks and feels like an antique, a relic from out of the past. Alex finds himself comforted by the timeless nature of the rooms and some of the old family heirlooms that still populate the house. However, Alex begins to get the distinct feeling that he is being watched, and he starts to think that he is sharing his place with an unwanted housemate. The horrors of the past continue to stalk Alex as the real world and the world of his nightmares begin to collide.
