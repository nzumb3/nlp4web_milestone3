Ocean Aquarium
###BREAK###
Ocean Aquarium is comprised entirely of aquarium-style "lockdown" shots, filmed on location in Papua New Guinea with special footage from Bonaire and Fiji. Enjoy the breathtaking "top side" images captured just above the water, and experience a unique perspective. Ocean Aquarium was captured in High-Definition by Emmy Award winner Anthony S. Lenzo, one of the world's foremost underwater videographers and producer of HDScape's "Visions of the Sea: Explorations".
