Softballs
###BREAK###
A men's recreational slo-pitch baseball team are looking to capture their first league championship. After being led to the championship game the previous year and losing, Brody decides that he will have to step up as Team Captain/Manager, replacing his older brother Bret. With the help of his overly confident sidekick, Richard, Brody looks to guide a group of charismatic, quirky, and sometimes irresponsible young men to the championship they all dream of.
