Naan
###BREAK###
After leaving the government home where he grew up, Karthik (Vijay Antony) wants to begin life afresh. He decides to move to Chennai after his relatives refuse to take him in. After the bus in which he is traveling meets with an accident, Karthik takes on the identity of his dead co-passenger and joins a medical college, but the past plays catching up.
