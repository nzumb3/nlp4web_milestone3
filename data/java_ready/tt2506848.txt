PTSD: An American Tragedy
###BREAK###
A film that accurately depicts the tragedy of post traumatic stress syndrome. Soldier Victor Martinez (Carlos Lopez) returns home after serving a tour in Afghanistan. In denial and unwilling to seek help Victor's relationships with friends and family begin to fall apart. A story that will open your eyes and break your heart as to how war effects our veterans after returning home and begin to recover from the horrors of combat.
