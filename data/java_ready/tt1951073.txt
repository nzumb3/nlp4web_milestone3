Affected
###BREAK###
Natural resources are running out, and a mysterious man known as the Tempter is goading people to fight for the luxuries they want. Teenage documentary maker Jacob and his girlfriend Laura take their camera to the scene of his last crime on the violent Eden Estate. But as people to start to turn 'affected', possessed by greed and empty of empathy - how will our heroes survive?
