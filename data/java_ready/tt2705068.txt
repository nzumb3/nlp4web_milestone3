The Molers: Searching for the Mole People
###BREAK###
There is something lurking beneath the surface in this mockumentary that rips the lid off one of the great urban myths of our time. Talpidanthropy - the belief in creatures that are half mole (family Talpidae) and half man (anthropo) - a product of fevered imaginations, or alert attention to a closely guarded secret?
