When Yasmina was in Thailand she slept with Johan. One day she sees him in Stockholm and dodges him. Then one night at a party she meets Tariq and follows him home. The morning after Tariq founds out that Yasmina had unprotected sex in Thailand and freaks out. Malin, Johans girlfriend, discovers he's been cheating in Thailand and it breaks her heart. Johan tries to make it up to her, but she refuses to talk to him. Instead she seeks out her HIV-positive cousin Alex and together they talks about their longing and hopes for the future. They love, they lie, they cheat, but all they want is to find love.
