Bolivar Twilight
###BREAK###
A documentary regarding the small-scale gold miners in Colombia, who are facing a critical situation: the arrival of international mining corporations. The film mainly gives the voice to the miners themselves and explicitly describes 13 tactics that the government uses to eliminate them. From putting military pressure to changing legal requirements, everything is made possible to ensure that the small-scale miners leave the stage.
