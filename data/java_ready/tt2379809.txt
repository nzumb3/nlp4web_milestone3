Driving Nowhere
###BREAK###
Julia and Nikki set out on a road trip in hopes that taking along their friend, Eleanor, will allow her to experience things more worldly beyond the shallow, materialistic world in which she had been sheltered. One night, they happen upon a 24 Hour Tavern in the middle of nowhere where they meet a young man by the name of Rich. Little do they know that the encounter with Rich will change everything and life as they know it. Secrets behind each woman's past and current life will be unveiled.
