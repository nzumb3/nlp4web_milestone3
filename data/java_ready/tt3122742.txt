Headrush
###BREAK###
Lighting crashes! Kurt Schuler (Cameron Spann) hovers over a shrouded body. Who is underneath the sheet? Who has been killed and who is the killer? In HEADRUSH, a thrilling science-fiction murder mystery directed by Johnson Thomasson, Kurt is a biochemist who does cutting edge research into the nature of death but when he discovers a mysterious human body in his laboratory, he must race to find the killer or risk losing everything he has worked for... even his own life.
