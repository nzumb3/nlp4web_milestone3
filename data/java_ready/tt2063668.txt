Hidden in Vicinity
###BREAK###
A dark edged thriller that takes one man on a quest to save humanity from the most deadly virus ever known...and worst still, his wife...time is the essence as the virus kills its victims quickly and painfully and as a famous doctor struggles to find a cure to combat the deadly infection; he must face the many haunting truths about his own mortality and that of those he deeply loves....Sometimes to save a life, you must commit murder...!
