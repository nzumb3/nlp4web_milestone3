The Fireball Run: Southern Excursion
###BREAK###
An outrageous, unusual, epic, and very REAL road adventure rally. FIREBALL RUN: Southern Excursion follows driving teams from celebrities to dignitaries as they use any means (including deception) to solve clues, accomplish missions, and travel to the most interesting yet unheard of destinations in America. Starting in Melbourne, Florida and finishing in Gulfport Mississippi driving everything from a Prius to Delorean, Bentley to Suburban, and Charger to Camaro the adrenaline charged cast of characters discover winning isn't anything and finishing is everything. Filmed on location in; Melbourne Florida, Peachtree Georgia, Knoxville Tennessee, Clarksville Tennessee, Anniston Alabama, and Gulfcoast Mississippi.
