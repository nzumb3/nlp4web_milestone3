Mariano is engaged to Daniela, but after a dinner in her house, she says to him: " I leave you because I love you too much.". Mariano can't believe what he hears. What does it means? So he shuts himself up in his room for a time. Mariano lives with his father, who never speaks; with his mother, who speaks too much and with his grandmother who seems the only person of commonsense in the house. One night Mariano meets Ana Paula a Brazilian girl searching a Hotel. He likes her so he offers his room as 'Bed and Breakfast' and later falls for her. But he meets Daniela again. Which girl will he marry? Daniela? Ana Paula? Or neither of them?
