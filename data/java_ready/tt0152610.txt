Stretchmarks
###BREAK###
Over black and white images of New York City's dingy apartments, gray skies, snowy streets, and crowded subways, a voice tells a young woman's story of single parenthood. The story is of her inner life: the loneliness and fatigue, the judgemental stares of strangers who offer no kindness, and the occasional moments of joy her son gives her.
