Nanny Culture
###BREAK###
Ever wonder what happens in the private world of nannies and the families they work for. The documentary, partly mockumentary, presents an amusing look into the households and the many mishaps that arise along the whirl road towards social harmony. Follow a nanny from the UK and an Emirati family, in Nanny Culture the dunes will be alive with songs of Arabia.
