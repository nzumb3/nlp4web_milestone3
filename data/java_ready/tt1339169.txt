Slightly Used
###BREAK###
Slightly Used is a comedy that takes place on the battle ground of the used car lot. The story revolves around humorous events that ensue during a competition between two sales teams, fighting for a chance to be able to star in the company's new commercial, and be crowned the new "HOT GUN OF THE MONTH". The Story takes place over the course of one work day in one location, the car lot.
