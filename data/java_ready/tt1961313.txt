Faultline
###BREAK###
Anna Wilding returned to her home town of Christchurch New Zealand, from the USA, following the fatal earthquakes of 22nd Feb. This film examines the movements in the city's recovery and the bureaucracy that accompanies it. The city has now been through some 7500 quakes which show to signs of abating. This film shot over an extended period examines the state of shock and the aftermath and indeed if the town will be rebuilt. Interviews with NASA, the Mayor, American crisis experts, the Wiggles, are dotted throughout the film as well as the face of heroic Canterbrians.
