Dead Bite
###BREAK###
A Hiphop group called Gancore Club being led by Joey Boy will find themselves stuck on Mermaid Island after a video shoot has gone terribly wrong. After some fun in the sun and a few strange events everything will change. The group will find themselves chased by the local villagers, different monsters and zombies! Has a Blair Witch Project/Cloverfield feel to movie.Original is subtitled.
