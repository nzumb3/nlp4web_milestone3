BoTTom: The True Meaning of Love & Acceptance
###BREAK###
Errol A. Grant is a disrespected newspaper columnist who experiences an apparition that mysteriously appears and speaks to him from the pulpit during a church service. He then begins to attempt to uncover the identity of the "The Black Angel," finally landing the elusive front page of the newspaper, which sparks a national sensation. Drunk with fame, Errol then begins to sell his soul by continuing to uncover this story, leaving a trail of death behind.
