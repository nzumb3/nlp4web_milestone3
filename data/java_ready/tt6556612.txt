Bloody Bits: Shorts Compilation Vol. 1
###BREAK###
Bloody BITS - Shorts Compilation vol. 1 is a collection of the best short films to play Toronto's critically acclaimed Blood In The Snow Film Festival, The Bloody Bits Shorts Compilation features the genre shorts "Dead All Night," "Greater Than," "Lively," "One More For The Road," "Seiren," "Tasha And Friends," and "Uncommon Enemies."
