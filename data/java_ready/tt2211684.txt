If Not You
###BREAK###
Andrius and Tomas are roommates in a youth home for mentally handicapped. Andrius is strong and handsome, a ladies' man who enjoys organizing activities. Tomas is the opposite: wheelchair-bound, not so attractive, insecure. Still, they are good friends; Andrius teaches Tomas about life and looks after him. But a girl who lives in the same home sparks up a rivalry between the friends, and provokes a test of their friendship. A creative observational documentary challenging common perceptions on disability, humanity, and emotions.
