Mama's Boy
###BREAK###
At a very young age, Mobo Jeeba witnessed the rape and murder of his mother, Akushika. Many years passed and the traumatizing effect haunted Jeeba, molding him into a ruthless killer. But little does he know, that some strange twists of events will soon be leading him on a journey that will unveil the mystery surrounding his mother's death. And on this journey........there will be BLOODSHED.
