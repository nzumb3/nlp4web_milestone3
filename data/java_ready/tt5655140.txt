Science Fiction and Magick: Frater Thabion Aka Poke Runyon and Curtis Shumaker
###BREAK###
In this interview on Poke Runyon's Hermetic Hour, I discuss the history of esoteric and occult themes in the literature of the horror, fantasy, and science fiction genres. The general focus is on H.P. Lovecraft and writers he inspired, such as Fritz Leiber, Richard Matheson, Charles Beaumont, and Jack Williamson.
