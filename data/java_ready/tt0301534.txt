Learning to Surf with Surfer Joe: The Ocean, You & Your Board
###BREAK###
Join Surfer Joe on his journey from clueless to cool, and discover the fundamentals of surfing along the way. Respected professionals and longtime surfers, like Sean Collins (surfline.com) and Sam George (Surfer Magazine), give you the information you need to enjoy our incredible ocean. The Ocean, You and Your Board demystifies and demonstrates basic oceanography, surfing fitness, wetsuits and surfboards with examples, animation, and beautiful surfing footage on waves from Malibu to Indonesia. Whether you're a pro, or have yet to paddle out on your first board, you'll want to watch these 50 minutes again and again.
