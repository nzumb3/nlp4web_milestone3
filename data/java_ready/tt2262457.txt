Winter Swimming
###BREAK###
Have you ever taken a cold shower? Well imagine that cold shower and you are on the way to understanding the sensation you feel from plunging yourself into icy cold water. Since the time of ancient Greece cold water has been much revered and throughout the ages it has often been used in healing practices. Today winter swimming is both an adrenaline sport and a lifestyle choice. The winter water appeals to a broad range of people and even attracts expectant mothers. In this film we meet some of the characters from a winter swimming club and find out more about the body's ability to withstand one of natures harshest elements - cold water!
