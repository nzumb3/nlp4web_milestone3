Time Capsule: The Silent Generation
###BREAK###
Time Capsule follows five people of the American generation born between 1925 to 1945. The documentary concentrates on specific stories and experiences of their past to serve as examples of their lifestyle during the prime of their life 1950 to 1970. It will also follow their quirky interests of today to show how they are 'still kickin'. All in an attempt to capture their cultural significance to American history.
