The Meaning of Filo
###BREAK###
A highly symbolic look at alcoholism as told through the eyes of Bob, an alcoholic who is having nightmares about being murdered by a mysterious figure. Terrified and alone, Bob embarks on a journey to defeat this shadowy demon whose dark presence comes to represent the disease that shackles him. The way Bob seeks to destroy Filo is a powerful look at how addiction can grip an individual, as well as being a strong reminder to those of us who have been face to face with addiction ourselves.
