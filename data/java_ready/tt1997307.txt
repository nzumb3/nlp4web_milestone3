Checkout
###BREAK###
In a town in which the main industry is 'suicide tourism', an old family-owned Suicide Guesthouse is in financial trouble. When a peculiar guest finally checks in, just days before Laura is about to go bankrupt, she has to do anything to get him dead, fast. But as he postpones his final 'checkout', and even becomes friends with her chef and nephew, encouraging him to leave, Laura must become... creative...
