All I Need
###BREAK###
All I Need is an Independent Film based on the first book in the Trilogy All I Need, by self-published author Rebecca Carrigan. Rebecca also directed and casted the movie. It's filled with new and upcoming actors & musicians that fill the soundtrack. All I Need is a story of a young girl named Sophie and her Guardian Angel. It's about following your dreams, falling in love and the unknown supernatural world of angels. This film is sure to inspire people of all ages, and make you think about how to live out the rest of your life. This film is now live on YouTube.com/CarriganProductions look for "All I Need - The Independent Series" and watch it in 10 parts.
