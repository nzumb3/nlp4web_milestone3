One Cube in Numerals
###BREAK###
Is the purpose of life to acts its part in a greater scheme and discarded when it wears out? Three unrelated women from different cities in India go about their lives on an odd working day. Something invisible & powerful runs in in the backdrop like long shadows. Their fates mirror and are affected by those of the people and objects around them. After all are they truly unrelated?
