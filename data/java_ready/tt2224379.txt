November Lies
###BREAK###
NOVEMBER LIES is a film about Damon, a family man with a promising career, but with an equally dark secret. Damon hides behind a web of lies and cannot show his true self to his family, friends or co-workers without his whole world crashing down around him. Unknown to him, there is someone close to him who knows Damon's secrets and is pulling strings that will unravel his web and show the world what lies beneath.
