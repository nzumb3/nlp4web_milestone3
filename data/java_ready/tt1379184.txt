###BREAK###
At an old people's home in a small Taiwan town, Xiao Liu, a dashing male nurse who does things his own way, has his hands full taking charge of a group of quirky old folks. Until, a gorgeous but reserved woman, Ru Ping - the new dance teacher, arrives in town. The old people want to learn Tango from Ru Ping and enter a dance contest. Only problem is the home is being shut down. But that won't stop this colorful crew of young and old!
