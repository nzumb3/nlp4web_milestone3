Rabhasa
###BREAK###
Rabhasa is a 2014 Telugu film written & directed by Santosh Srinivas. The story revolves around a US return student Karthik who pledges to marry his uncle Dhananjay's daughter Indu which was his mother's wish. A small family feud ends up with both the families getting separated. The rest of the story is all about how he controls the relationship and fulfills the wish of his mother.
