Medium
###BREAK###
William and Jobe have come to visit a very different type of relative. A practicing MEDIUM, Aunt Agnes opens a door to her minds consciousness to communicate with those who have died. Helping their Aunt move William and Jobe are faced with the horrifying realization that while performing a reading Aunt Agnes has suffered a major heart attack leaving open the door to the "THE OTHER SIDE" and all who cross its threshold wide open. With the aid of a physic, the three must find each other and exit the house before what has entered its threshold now enters them.
