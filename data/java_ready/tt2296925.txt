Raging Gray
###BREAK###
Helen and Meeka are two women of different races thrown together in a whirlwind of of abuse by their husbands. They discover each others abuse and decide to confront their abusers but tragedy strikes. Now in the fight of their lives they band together in friendship, family and faith to heal the wounds that secrets have revealed. The perfect weapon was created when black and white came together. Raging Gray, because there is no calm between black and white.
