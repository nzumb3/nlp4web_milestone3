Ecila
###BREAK###
Once Upon a time in the city of Boceno... Up and coming writer, Ecila, finds her curiosity spiraling out of control plunging her into the midst of a unique visual world. Ignoring all advice to flee the city of Boceno, Ecila trails a peculiar pink moped down a twisted road creased with a slew of masquerading characters. Omnipresent danger inks Ecila's pages in this wonderland adventure
