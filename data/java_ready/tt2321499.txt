S-a Furat Mireasa
###BREAK###
Nina, an unemployed former winner of a beauty contest (ex Miss Barlad, county level), is tired of living the poor life with her husband, a policeman. She decides to kidnap Marinela who is just about to get married, with a mobster son. With the extortion money, so she hopes, all her troubles will be laid to rest. Little did she know that the bride hates her soon-to-be husband and welcomes her abduction.
