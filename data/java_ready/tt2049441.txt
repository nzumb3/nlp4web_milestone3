Fear in the Gorge
###BREAK###
While on a hiking/fishing trip, four friends discover what they believe is Bigfoot in the gorge forest. One of the guys decides to shoot the beast and drag him back to town as ultimate proof that Bigfoot really does exist. But he ends up only wounding Bigfoot and then they lose track of the beast in the gorge forest. During the night the tables are turned and the four friends become the hunted.
###BREAK###
