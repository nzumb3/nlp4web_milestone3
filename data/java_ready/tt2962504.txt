Stuck in Kathmandu
###BREAK###
A young American traveler (Briana Kai Louis) embarks on a epic adventure to put her grandfather's ashes to rest in the most beautiful valley in the World, "The Valley of Flowers" in India. During her journey Briana Kai gets stranded in the ancient city of Kathmandu, Nepal. Embraced by the locals, she discovers true beauty within herself, and through the kindness of strangers as she struggles to reach her destination.
