Murphy's Law
###BREAK###
Starring Intouchables OMAR SY. Murphy's Law is an adage that broadly states: "Anything that can go wrong will go wrong." After four years in prison, Elias is carrying out his social reintegration in a hospital, where he works as an orderly. In five hours, his probation will come to an end. He can set out on a new life and forget about his past. But he neglected to take into account Murphy's Law and its delightful raft of maximum problems...
