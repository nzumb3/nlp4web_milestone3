Common Threads: The Rasta/Shigley Project
###BREAK###
The collaboration between professional free surfer and environmentalist, Dave Rastovich and San Diegan based artist, Neil Shigley. In August of 2009, Neil was commissioned to do a portrait of Dave using his 'Invisible People' motif, which includes in no particular order, carving, painting, photography, drawing, and printing. This is the journey of a piece of art, from sketches on a pad to a signature in the corner, that also helps to shed light on Dave Rastovich's environmental causes along with Neil Shigley's helping the homeless endeavors.
