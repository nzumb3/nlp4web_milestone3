India inside Out
###BREAK###
INDIA INSIDE OUT is a sensitive journey throughout contemporary India, a country's portrait. This trip to the chore of the country's social, cultural and ritual diversity allows, through famillies' talks, to feel India from within. We are able to share their questioning confronted as they are to the many changings due to globalization and the issue of modernity and traditions. Through this deep immersion in intimate spaces, a specific window opens up onto contemporary Indian nation.
