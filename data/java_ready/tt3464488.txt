Baby Heavy
###BREAK###
Baby Heavy is a film documenting the life of a letterpress. Byfarr Design Studio recently welcomed an early 1900s Chandler and Price Letterpress into its fleet. The press is affectionately named Baby Heavy and the team is excited to tell its tale. This short documentary, filmed by Kickstand Studio, captures the history of letterpress printing, the work that goes into preserving these treasured machines and the beautiful work they create. Follow the documentary as it covers the history of printing, the significance of letterpress in today's world, interviews with local designers and antique experts and Baby Heavy's trip from Tennessee to Columbia.
