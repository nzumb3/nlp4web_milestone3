My Brother's Keeper
###BREAK###
'My Brother's Keeper' takes the audience on a roller coaster journey deep into the distorted mind of a Rogue soldier within the midst of his tragic downfall. Upon returning home to his family in Scotland, it does not take long before they realise that their John is not the same man and the true horror of the person that now lies beneath is gradually revealed. 'My Brother's Keeper' is the latest feature film from Scottish Filmmaker Lee Hutcheon, with compelling performances from a wealth of new British screen talent and a twisting plot that puts you on the edge of your seat and keeps you there.
