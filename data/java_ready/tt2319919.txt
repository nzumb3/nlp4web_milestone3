Lethal Commission
###BREAK###
A secret software created by the American government leaks into Mumbai, India and is at risk to going completely underground. An American spy is sent into India to recover the software. The agent is assigned an assistant by the name of Natasha who will lead him through the agency's Indian contacts. In effort to recover the software from the programmer Marcelo Sanchez who is planning to escape, the agent is assigned to play a poker game in order to corner the programmer. In the process of trying to recover the software, the agent falls in love with his assistant and suddenly the mission turns personal as the chase begins.
