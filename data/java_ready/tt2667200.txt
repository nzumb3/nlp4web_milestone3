Dr Smith
###BREAK###
This is a magical story, located in an enchanted castle where a psychologist tries to save a rebellious teenager from an imaginary world in which she lives in. Will Dr. Smith manage to conquer the girl's trust? A funny but also thought provoking movie in which the narrative is lead by psychological and creative duels... And if you discovered that reality isn't what it seems?
