WHSOC: Saddleback Island Trip
###BREAK###
The Windham High School Outing Club (WHSOC) supported by Teens to Trails (T3) by hosting the 1st School Premier of the Maine Outdoor Film Festival (MOFF). Thanks to Teen to Trails and their partners, the Maine Coast Heritage Trust and Old Quarry Ocean Adventures. The Windham High School Outing Club won a 3 day Sea Kayaking trip to Saddleback Island. This film documents that amazing once in a lifetime trip.
