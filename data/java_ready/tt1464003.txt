Blue Sheet Blues
###BREAK###
Ryohei, a documentary film maker, is trying to find out what is missing in his life. His girlfriend Rui who is working at an 'adult entertainment saloon' shares this feeling of anxiety. One day Ryohei meets Gen, a homeless person living in a makeshift tent made of the 'Blue Sheets' that is typical for the homeless in Japan. Ryohei gets obsessed with the idea of doing a documentary about Gen's life. Bribed with a bottle of Sake, Gen reluctantly agrees to this idea. While taking shots of his daily life, Gen's past slowly but intensely reveals itself to Ryhoei. But can he do something to help him? People sometimes cause misery without meaning to. When this happens, how can they go on living with their sensation of guilt?
