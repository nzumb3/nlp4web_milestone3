Chameleon
###BREAK###
Jessica and Natasha, co-workers at a men's clothing store, are getting ready to close when they notice the headline of the daily newspaper; It's another report regarding a string of unsolved cannibalistic serial killings in the city. Natasha stays behind to lock up shop, while Jessica now can't help but feel a little nervous about her eerie walk alone to her bus stop. Her imagination runs wild as every sound and shadow is surely something dangerous in the darkness. Is this just another case of frightful paranoia? Or is there really something out there that Jessica has every right to fear?
