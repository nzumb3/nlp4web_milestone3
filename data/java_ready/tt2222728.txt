Just Ate
###BREAK###
Just Ate tells the story of Eve, a brilliant, young and bulimic chef. Eve spends her time closed off in her room, distracting herself from life with food, or working at the restaurant she manages with her tyrant boss, Martin. She ignores her family, despite living with them, and her only friend is her perverted co-worker, Benji. But when she meets an enigmatic homeless man at the soup kitchen she volunteers at, Eve finds herself wanting to live again. But can a girl with so many secrets ever really let anyone in?
