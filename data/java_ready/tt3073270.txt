###BREAK###
London ... Azra is newly arrived from Lebanon and is finding her feet in a new city. She has found a job, a flat and a friend called Ade. But then everything begins to change. Azra starts having dreams of accidents, hospitals and lights? Is her new friend in danger? Are the threats of racist, white power fanatic Mark real? Is he the one who will be the cause of Ade's injuries? A tightly written thriller, focuses on the experiences of a young girl and her relationship with those around her as she struggles to unravel the mystery of her dreams.
