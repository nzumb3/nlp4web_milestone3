Jason - A Triumph of the Human Spirit
###BREAK###
This is my life story, my trials and tribulations in dealing with life and my disabilities. It starts with my birth and growing up. It deals with moving out on my own, college life, making new friends and long-lasting relationships. It also talks about my relationships with people and women, my struggles finding a job, and the misunderstandings of others. But through it all, I've overcome. Life has dealt me several blows, but I've gotten back up on my feet and face the world.
