Minas
###BREAK###
A film about the life, death and eternity of the artist Minas. Who is the artist? Is it a profession? Calling? State of mind? The strokes of his brush are his life, his philosophy, his childhood, his love. His art is his very soul, where he does not dominate. But who does? Artist Minas Avetisyan reaching fame and recognition was killed under mysterious circumstances in an accident, in the prime of his life, at 47.
