Last Days: Hype or Hope?
###BREAK###
A shocking investigative report filmed on 3 continents that examines world events to see just how closely they are lining up with the predictions of the prophets. Hosted by Dr. Jack Van Impe, LAST DAYS examines the cultural landscape for signs of the coming apocalypse. The investigation looks into current events on three continents and compares the various modern-day happenings to the predictions of Biblical prophets.
