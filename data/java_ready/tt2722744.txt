Bogd Khaan
###BREAK###
Before the democratic changes in 1990, socialist ideology influenced every field of art such as film and literature. The activities of eminent Buddhist monks, mainly the personality and efforts of head lama, (1870-1924) become distorted, he appeared as the main enemy of the Mongolian people's revolution and poisoner of leader of the red revolution. These perverted views still exist in people's mind. In 2012 we are celebrated 100Th anniversary of the Mongolian National Independence. This monumental film is to present the personality and historic activity of the religious leader correctly, helping the future generation to understand properly his role in Mongolian history.
