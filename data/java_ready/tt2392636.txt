Scrapbookers
###BREAK###
Once a solitary and frugal pastime, scrapbooking has exploded into a multi-billion dollar industry supported by a culture of camera-wielding, sticker-hoarding, late-night cropping women. Scrapbookers is an feature-length documentary about the women of scrapbooking, their supporters and critics, and the psychology behind the need to preserve one's memories.
