The Dirt Bowl: The Tradition The Journey. It Lives in Me.
###BREAK###
The film The Dirt Bowl The tradition the journey, 'it lives in me' is a full length documentary about the Dirt Bowl. A summer basketball league that has been a sports tradition in the City of Louisville Ky since 1969.In this documentary the history, tradition, its journey and challenges are told by basketball legends such as Darrell Griffith of the 1980 NCAA champions the Louisville Cardinals along with the University of Louisville coach and basketball Hall of Fame inductee Denny Crum, University of Kentucky and NBA greats Derek Anderson and Winston Bennett and Louisville very own Harlem Globetrotter Mr Dallas Thornton and many more.
