Tom Wolfe Gets Back to Blood
###BREAK###
Tom Wolfe spent several years researching his new novel, Back to Blood, in Miami. This documentary goes behind the iconic white suit to show what makes this master author and journalist tick. The man behind Bonfire of the Vanities, the Electric Kool Aid Acid Test and the Right Stuff shows that even in his 80s, he hasn't lost his touch.
