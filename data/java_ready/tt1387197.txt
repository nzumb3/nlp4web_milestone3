Karma Rider: We Together with Ralph
###BREAK###
Haunted by his past karma and many surrealistic nightmares, a Vietnam Veteran treks across the country on his motorcycle. The Vet is befriended by a 'groovy' guru, Mennonite family, and a 'groovy' Miss America, but is haunted by skeletal figures and demons! His journey takes him from The Rockies, to New England and...into the astral realms wherein lie his enemies... and salvation.
