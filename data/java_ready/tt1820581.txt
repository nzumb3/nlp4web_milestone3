Out on a Limb
###BREAK###
David 'Squirrelman' Csaky received international attention and became the face of homelessness when Seattle officials evicted him from his elaborate treehouse where he'd been squatting on city property for nearly two years. 'Out On A Limb' chronicles the construction and destruction of David's make-shift home in the trees and follows his subsequent journey to officially and permanently crawl out of homelessness once back on solid ground.
