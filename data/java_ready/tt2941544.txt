I Love Mali
###BREAK###
Since the beginning of 2012, the war in northern Mali has provoked the displacement of 250,000 people. More than 75,000 are now refugees in Mauritania, living in the Mberra camp. Most of them are women and children. These are their stories. The stories of people who have been forced to leave their home country, of women who have been raped, of men who survived massacres. The tragedy of Tuareg people from northern Mali. With an exclusive interview to the only woman who is a leader of the main Tuareg rebel group.
