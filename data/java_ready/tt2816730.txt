Pale Horses
###BREAK###
When Patti returns to Astoria, New York, the last person she expects to see is her old college friend, Janis. But after they run into each other one morning, they soon find themselves bonding over dinner about the mistakes they hope they've left behind. Soon, Janis's friend Keith is on the scene, testing the fragile relationship being held by these three.
