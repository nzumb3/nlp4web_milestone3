Three Days Happiness
###BREAK###
In Athens, a city that decays, three young women struggle to find their way out. Irina is ready to leave for Canada, to escape from the "family" that forces her to work as a prostitute. Anna is getting married to banish the ghost of her dissolved family. Vera, in the beginning of her new life, is confronted with the secret reality of her family. In three days their paths cross unexpectedly driving them to a violent adulthood. How far they can go, fighting for their own happiness?. After the promising Athens of millennium in '2000+1 Shots' (2000), the euphoria of Olympics in 'Planet Athens' (2004), here is the decaying city in epilogue of a trilogy on Athens, by Athanitis.
