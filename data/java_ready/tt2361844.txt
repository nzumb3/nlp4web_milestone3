Last Floor
###BREAK###
When an army captain discovers a dark secret about his unit, he's soon fired. Jobless and depressed, having strong nationalist and homophobic views, he can see enemies all around him. To protect his family from the evil of the outside world, he decides to get rid of the TV set, keep the children away from school and shut all the doors to their apartment on the last floor of a tall building.
