Sam
###BREAK###
SAM is a character study film about a man who believes his life is like television so he makes a movie in order to discover who he really is. Jonathan his best friend sticks by his side, however Jonathan's girlfriend Rosella, an atheist who out of frustration with Jonathan's often lewd behavior is starting to find feelings for Sam. Sam is conflicted and torn between God, religion, himself, who he is and what he is doing.
