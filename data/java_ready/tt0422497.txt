Irene's Last Call
###BREAK###
In the 1930's, a teenager became the first woman in Detroit to get a liquor license. During her 65-year reign, Irene called the shots at The Kress Lounge, the legendary bar that defied every rule. Accompanied by an original score from Emmy- award (Evil Dead, Zena, Brotherhood of the Wolf) composer Joseph LoDuca.
