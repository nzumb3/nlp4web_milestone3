Fatalis
###BREAK###
FATALIS is a story about Milo, a young man living in New York City, who finds himself at a crossroads. Working as a late night cab driver in 1990, Milo silently plays the hand that society deals him and drives the empty streets alone. While dealing with prejudices due to his Croatian roots, Milo struggles to improve his surroundings and avoid a life of crime. One night, Milo meets Alina, an all around American girl turned prostitute, who takes a liking to Milo. As Milo and Alina form an unexpected romance, Milo's idealistic views fog his opinion to Alina's way of life. Milo is faced with the choice of either following his true self or his true love.
