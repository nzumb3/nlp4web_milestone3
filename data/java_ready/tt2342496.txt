Redemption
###BREAK###
A middle school physical education teacher with a perfect amateur mixed martial arts record of 5-0 finds himself falling down a bumpy road when he turns professional. His first fight ends with him getting knocked out in only 39 seconds and soon after he learns that he is laid off from his job. This is the story of his year long odyssey finding himself back in the cage exactly one year later on the same night, looking for redemption.
