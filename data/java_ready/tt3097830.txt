James Dean's Lost Slideshow
###BREAK###
James Dean returns from the dead as a 78 year old man who has been living as a recluse in NYC. Returning to his home town and family farm in Fairmount, Indiana, James Dean retrieves a hidden suitcase from his barn, containing film negatives. He then creates a slide show and tells his own story...
