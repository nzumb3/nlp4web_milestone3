Chocolate
###BREAK###
It a ROM-COM Thriller movie. With a twist in the end. The story Chocolate revolves around the life of a COP, a CONMAN and their life threatening chase for their common targets - THE RING, A CHOCOLATE FLAVORED BOX and something else that cannot be explained. This deal takes a Topsy-Turvy twist and becomes the fight for their LIVES and COMMITMENTS and at last, it turns into A SATIRE where, with many human characters, these non living objects - a chocolate flavored box and a diamond ring, also play a very dominant role in this film.
