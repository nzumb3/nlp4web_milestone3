Le Sequel
###BREAK###
The film is a indie Comedy Horror, Sci Fi spoof mash up mockumentary style movie following the journey of hapless 22 times film director Carlos Revalos who embarks on his biggest feature film to date Le Fear II: Le Sequel, with a $10 million budget $500000 of his own cash what can go wrong?, Efi Womonbongo a Nigerian faux film producer is what can and does go wrong, bringing horror and sfx from a playground, Nollywood meets Hollywood in a clash of styles, personality and impacts into a chaotic movie, seeing is believing we have: Explosions,aliens,vampire lesbians,witch doctors and much more.
