Adventures in Spying
###BREAK###
Brian McNichols and his mate, Shoup are folding newspapers in their Toyota ute, for their paper round, when they spot a notorious criminal who is supposedly dead. They know there is a reward for his capture, so the two friends go to the local policeman who does not believe a single word they say. So they decide, by themselves, to prove that the criminal is alive... and in their town!
