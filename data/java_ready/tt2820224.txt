Morning Mary Lou
###BREAK###
A touching look into the lives of a couple that have been together for over sixty years, 'Morning Mary Lou' emphasizes the fact that life isn't over once you've hit your twilight - even when the unimaginable happens. The story of love and perseverance in the face of one of life's most difficult trials is absolutely proof that beauty is often born of hardship, and that bond of family and community is more powerful than anything that can befall you.
