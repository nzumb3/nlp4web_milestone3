La mort du Dieu serpent
###BREAK###
After a street fight goes wrong, Koumba, age 20, is expelled to Senegal. Having arrived in France in her early childhood, in 48 hours the restless teenager is sent to a village in the middle of the bush, far from her family and parisian life. The destiny of a young parisian from the tenements during her five year exile.
