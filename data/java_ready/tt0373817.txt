Elvis in East Peoria
###BREAK###
Meet Donna: an ambitious woman struggling with her marriage, her life and her explosive daughter, Lisa. And Jimmy: a struggling Elvis Impersonator with no money, no job and three failed marriages. However, tragedy and comedy are about to collide. When Donna meets Jimmy, nothing will ever be the same again. When Donna brings Jimmy home to live with her and her husband, Jerry, sparks fly and dreams are born as Donna gambles their entire life savings and family stability promoting Jimmy in her own lavish Elvis production in East Peoria.
