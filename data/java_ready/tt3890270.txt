Brake Mode
###BREAK###
This is the sixth co-production project between Tokyo University of the Arts and Korean Academy of Film Arts. It describes meeting and parting on business of Japanese and Korean young workers. A young employee of a Japanese automobile automaker goes to Busan for a business trip. His company has announced joint project with Korean largest automaker and a new-model car will be released in two days. His job is to do nothing but attend a release ceremony in Busan, until a mysterious old man steals the new-model car in front of his eyes. Getting a great kick by a female colleague from a rival company, he becomes aware of craziness inside himself and start dashing in Busan searching for the stolen car.
