The Bridge
###BREAK###
Evan Sears of the Traveling Network show 'Haunted America' travels to the great north woods to explore paranormal activity. While there, Evan learns of the haunting of Allison Antonich. A woman who was raped and then committed suicide after killing her children off the Aerial lift bridge in Duluth, MN. As Evan learns more of this tragic event he starts to change and not for the better.
