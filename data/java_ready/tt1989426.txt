18 jours au coeur de la révolution égyptienne
###BREAK###
This documentary is a chronological historic testimony of the 18 days of the Egyptian revolution counted by 6 participants among whom: Five Egyptians who followed the remote revolution through the Egyptian and foreign media. and the exclusive interview of a Korean cameraman which was imprisoned for the simple fact of being a journalist. From the beginning of the revolution of the Egyptian people till the fall of Mubarak (from January 25Th till February 11th, 2001), this movie tries to draw, above all, the narrative of the lived and the felt of an historic event, that of the peaceful Egyptian revolution, the revolution ' Facebook '
