The Congressman's Dinner
###BREAK###
An American Adventure of Peace, Love and Politics. A congressman invites a select few people to a dinner party for his eighteen year old autistic son. With his campaign manager at his side the plan is to use this occasion to better ascertain the pulse of the people in preparation for his forthcoming reelection bid. Through the course of the discussion and the dinner we see revealed the nature of both the host and his guests where the bigger story is everyone's looking to love and be loved and to live in peace. The movie describes the political paradigm shift experienced by people of the 21st century and outlines a participatory plan for America's involvement including a proven pathway to the actualized creation of world peace.
