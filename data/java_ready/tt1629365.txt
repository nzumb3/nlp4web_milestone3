Raggio di sole
###BREAK###
Renzo and Roberto Rossellini - Two Brothers on their Journey towards the Unknown. Two brothers go through life together. The older one finds a new way to represent reality in film. The younger one expresses this film reality in music. At a mature age both brothers become aware of the shortcomings that limited their arts. From this they draw a conclusion - each one in his own way.
