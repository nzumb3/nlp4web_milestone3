Mud Pies & Kites: Death & Resurrection in Haiti
###BREAK###
Set in Haiti, Mud Pies & Kites is a visual meditation on compassion; essentially, it consists of a series of reflections on the nature and importance of compassion. The film captures the dignity and courage of the poor, as well as the vibrantly colorful culture of Haiti, including its music, art and stunning natural beauty. In the midst of the agony of extreme poverty and deprivation, the film celebrate the possibility of a better future through mutual cooperation and genuine compassion.
