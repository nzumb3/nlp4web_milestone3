La Festividad de los Muertos. Lanii Xtee Tugul
###BREAK###
Since pre-Hispanic times, death has played a fundamental role in Zapotec life. Heirs to an ancient tradition, the Zapotecs in Oaxaca still live with their dead. The certainty of the dead's presence in the daily life of the community is reaffirmed once a year when these souls are honored in a special way.
