Patricia Gennice
###BREAK###
In a night like any other, the lonely Lucas casually meet Patricia Gennice, the most gorgeous girl in town, and she invites him to a date in her house in an hour. From then on, everything will go wrong for Lucas in his way to Patricia's home: he is robbed, kidnapped, threatened, tortured, see angels and demons, and worse: he finds himself without condoms!
