Some Day I'll Find You
###BREAK###
In an industrial part of Britain known as the Black Country, strange goings on are afoot. Weird drawings of dead opera star Mario Lanza are being left around in Banksy style secret drops. But it's all been going on for fifty years. In this magical realist art detective story we follow retired cop Mick Pearson as he tries to find out the truth behind the mystery.
