Jongens, jongens wat een vent
###BREAK###
The (in)famous Dutch actor/producer/director Pim de la Parra tells about his life-lasting film career. Once a millionaire due to the success of 'Blue Movie', he went bankrupt after the financial failure of 'Wan Pipel'. Nevertheless he kept going making films and introduced the so called 'minimal movie'.
