True Story
###BREAK###
Hakim, a young single father in New York, is forced to deal with his past ghosts while maintaining his friendship from childhood. Wanting more out of life, he meets Nia, a woman that blows his mind. The love affair is magical, but when his past issues haunt him and an unexpected betrayal occurs, Hakim must decide at what cost is he willing to fight for Nia and a chance at happiness.
