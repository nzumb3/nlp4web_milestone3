Divine Souls
###BREAK###
In the hallways of this AIDS clinic, there are many untold stories. Through Kerri's eyes, a caring and compassionate nurse, we will see a few of them. We will meet David, a nine year old boy who holds the key to Kerri's heart and Lisa, a new patient with a dark secret. Through the good times and the bad times, DIVINE SOULS is a touching story of love and hope over adversity, and humanity at its best.
