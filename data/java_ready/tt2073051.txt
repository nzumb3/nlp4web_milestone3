Pariahs
###BREAK###
A new school year has begun and Tyler Hall would give anything to get through it without a hassle. With his best yet weirdest friend, Paul, he returns to school where he becomes captivated by newcomer Mary Williams. Ryce Anderson picks up bullying Tyler right where he left off last year. But now it's fueled by jealousy over Tyler and Mary's new relationship. As Ryce haunts his life, Tyler loses faith in himself and embarks upon a sickening downward spiral. His only questions are...when will he it and how hard?
