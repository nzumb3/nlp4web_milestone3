Misty
###BREAK###
People were disappearing one after another. Hideyuki, the detective was investigating this disappearance. Meanwhile, Eri, who is the wife of Hideyuki gave a birth. However, their baby has been kidnapped by someone. Hideyuki chases the suspect to get the baby back and tries to repair the damaged relationship with Eri. While Hideyuki was struggling to solve the problem, the aurora was just up in the sky looking down on him.
