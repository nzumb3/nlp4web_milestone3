The Round Table
###BREAK###
Lucky James Sanderson had it all: talent, an athletic scholarship and a lot of community support. His journey to play college basketball came with a big price. Lucky soon finds out that life is more than just fame and glory. Detective Mark Thompson, Lucky's cousin, wants to make sure Lucky stays on track, however a neighborhood gang has other plans for the young star. Lucky finds himself evading peer pressure but a routine errand to the store places Lucky at the wrong place and time.
