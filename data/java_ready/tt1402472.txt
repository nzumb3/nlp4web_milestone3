Españolas de portada
###BREAK###
Spanish cover girls' is the intimate portrait of four famous Spanish models: Martina Klein, Vanesa Lorenzo, Eugenia Silva and Davinia Pelegrí. Because of their youth and the brevity of their careers, they are the most fragile element in a whimsy and very requiring business. Being a model means living in a glamorous world, but also full of stress, disappointments and solitude. New York is the aim. A giant photograph in the Fifth Avenue.
