Amparo
###BREAK###
Coming from the countryside to work as maid, Maria do Amparo, 15 years, gets pregnant. She could hide the pregnancy from her mistress, but she then founds out and asks her to get rid of the baby. Desperate, the young girl throws herself with the baby in a river. The film tells Amparo's history and her rescue trial, narrated by testimonials of who helped or seem the case.
