Politics Unveiled
###BREAK###
For 3 months, a filming crew dove into the unexplored scenario of an Electoral Campaign period in the heart of Brazil's Northeastern countryside. They captured the images of passion stirring political practices which have also become a means to survive for many poor communities. Brazil politic uncovered, the movie, plunges into these private and particular stories, thus presenting to us, two periods of a same town, two periods of each one's political life.
