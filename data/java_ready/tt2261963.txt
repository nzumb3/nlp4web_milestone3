Phantom of the Ghetto
###BREAK###
Dynamic spy film in which Johnny Danger and a host of daring new characters are sent on their deadliest challenge. Revealing fighting skills as lethal as his legendary intellect, aided by his beautiful and lethal spy mistresses belly dancer to ex-Navy Seal, Danger battles as never before to defeat and destroy a deadly nemesis and unravel a sinister plot that could destroy the economies of both the U.S.A. and Mexico.
