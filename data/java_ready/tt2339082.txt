Grand Theft Matrimony
###BREAK###
While playing wing-man to his friend Dutch, Jordan happens to meet DJ who is also playing dating support for her friend Mary. It just so happens that Dutch and Mary are on a blind date that fails horribly. However, Jordan and DJ hit it off famously.A passing joke is made about how easy it would be to steal a wedding. Hilarity ensues as Jordan, DJ, Dutch and Mary attempt to pull off the caper of a lifetime in order to enter into wedded bliss.
