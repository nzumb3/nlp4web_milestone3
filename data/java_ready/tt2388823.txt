Zombie Mutation
###BREAK###
The movie follows 6 people including low life junkies and strippers as they battle for survival in the midst of a zombie outbreak in a run down dump of a town on the outskirts of a city. As well as showing you the zombie carnage from the 6 low lifes and strippers point of view it also gives you a glimpse into whats left of the undead creatures minds themselves.
