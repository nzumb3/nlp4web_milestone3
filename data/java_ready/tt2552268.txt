Subway Song
###BREAK###
A tale of intersecting lives in the New York City subways. Gordon: a homeless drug addict on his last legs, Neil: a privileged therapist having a midlife crisis, Malik: a volatile ex-con and Valentina: a precocious teenage runaway, along with other denizens of the subway wander in and out of each others lives producing unforeseen consequences.
