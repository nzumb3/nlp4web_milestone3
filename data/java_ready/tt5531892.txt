Sanperè!: Venisse il fulmine
###BREAK###
A year in the shelter of Caramagna (in Piedmont) where Diarra, a young man from Côte d'Ivoire, and his companions of misfortune lives. They arrived from Libya, and they are waiting to get an immigrant visa and political asylum. Some of them fled from war, others are seeking a new future, for which they paid handsomely.
