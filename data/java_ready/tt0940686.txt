Dodo
###BREAK###
Bob Golubs, Dodo, is a sports themed autobiographical portrait of the artist as a member of a dysfunctional family. The film, like the one-man show that its adapted from,alcoholic, beloved father. Golub, a stand-up comedian from Western Pennsylvania, has constructed his film out of the fragments of a life-long effort to come to terms with his upbringing.
