Master of Inventions
###BREAK###
Jeff is the idea man for Blam Co's line of useless but enormously popular As-Seen-On-TV products, but he has a nagging feeling that his ingenuity is being compromised for a cash grab. Can he overcome soulless coworkers, awkward romantic entanglements, manic fans, and his own sense of self-importance to avoid going down in history as the world's worst inventor?
