Black Theater Today: 2005
###BREAK###
Perky Idea's innovative educational feature documentary reveals a creative montage of interviews from noted professional artist in the African-American Community. This fresh yet poignant tale reaches into the past to carve an exciting future, Thea-Marie Perkins' 70-minute feature documentary exposes where theater is today, shares it's past, and offers a serious look at crafting the future for a younger generation.
