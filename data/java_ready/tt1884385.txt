Pigskin & Magnolias: 12 Days of Fandom
###BREAK###
'Pigskin & Magnolias' is a love letter to college football fans of the Southeastern Conference. Fans all over the south make Saturday pilgrimages to concrete cathedrals to watch football. They graze through parking lots, and grassy fields to tailgate. Hard-core fans forge a deep connection to their teams, intertwining their fandom with their personal identity through meaningful experiences. 'Pigskin & Magnolias' is a fan's journey and a cultural anthropology of the southern college football.
