The Cramp
###BREAK###
Julien is a French depressed loner who wanders in a fishermen community located in the Mexican Pacific coast. After some days in this place of an astonishing natural beauty, the young man doesn't seem to feel better. On the contrary, he gets worse and worse. Nothing or no one seems to draw his attention. It's through Pablo, a fisherman who shows him the place and his way of life, that Julien slowly begins to feel better. Nonetheless, Julien will have to affront his solitude to reconcile with his self.
