Blood Reunion
###BREAK###
Fifteen years ago young Janeth discovered her mother's body. Her mother, Winona, had fallen victim to a vampire. She was imprisoned in her grave by the local priest, Father Rivers. Now Janeth, unaware of her mother's fate, has returned to her home town to come to terms with the grisly discovery made that night. Unwittingly she releases her mother from her grave prison. Now Winona is out to satisfy a fifteen-year-old thirst for blood-- and REVENGE!
