Romancing Sydney
###BREAK###
Elisa is young and sweet. Sachin is dreamy and naive. A disaster for both becomes love at first sight. Will fate keep them together? Zac and Alex are tight. Their relationship blossomed amidst dance and gymnastics. But can someone truly be that sure? George works in an antique shop run by Lilli. George also has a secret that's consumed many years of his life, a secret that would shock everyone. Through a saga intertwined with dance pieces, six individuals embrace their emotions in Sydney.
