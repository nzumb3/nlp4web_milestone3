The Angel of My Life
###BREAK###
In 1986, China is collapsed after Maos regime is losing control over China the country splits into a communist agenda. That is why a father is separated from his daughter, but the daughter refuse to give up to find her father again. But first she must survive on her own. In the dirty streets of Beijing. The story of "the Angel of My Life" is a sad tragedy of the darkest time in China during the 80s and a fathers love for his daughter.
