I Am Not a Rock Star
###BREAK###
Shot over 8 years, I AM NOT A ROCK STAR follows the tumultuous coming-of-age story of Juilliard trained concert pianist Marika Bournaki (age 12 to 20). Intimately exploring universal themes of dreams, sacrifice, love, family, loss and determination, the film ultimately reveals the elusive alchemy of finding one's purpose in life.
