Rivers of Australia: A Journey Along the Murray
###BREAK###
Join James and Albany, along with their faithful companions 'Onyx' and 'Rocco', on an awe-inspiring kayaking journey along Australia's longest river - the Murray. From Corryong, Victoria to The Coorong, South Australia, this unique television documentary series follows the ambitious journey of two southern adventurers who will kayak the entire length of the river.
