My Name Is Eric
###BREAK###
My Name is Eric is the story of Eric Sanders, a unique and interesting character living in Northern Alabama. Taking place over the course of 4 years, Eric explains his life and where he's been. This is an intimate look into the life of a very unusual person. Everyone may have a story, but this one should be seen to be believed. From chicken farming, to funeral embalming, to band manager, Eric's stories really give you some insight into his world. This documentary also features exclusive live footage of the bands ANTISEEN and Hammerlock. All used with permission.
