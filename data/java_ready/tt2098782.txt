Pilla Zamindar
###BREAK###
PJ (Nani) is the grandson of a wealthy zamindar. PJ is an irresponsible youngster who is spoiled by the pampering and excessive money. His grandfather writes a will with several conditions before he dies. He writes the will that PJ will inherit his entire wealth only if he completes his graduation as a common man without any luxuries. The rest of the story is all about how PJ learns about values and life as he achieves his goal.
