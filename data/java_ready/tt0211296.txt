Chasing the Dragon
###BREAK###
Life is a drug induced haze for Claire Oberon; living in her remote mansion, addicted to heroin, surrounding herself with her drug addicted friends, letting her life slowly fade away. Attempting one last chance, Claire's mother, Judith Oberon, contacts Cary Gable, an ex-boyfriend of Claire's, to help clean her up. Cary, at a stand still in his own life, sees this as an opportunity to move on. And through his efforts to get Claire back on track, the life of those involved is changed forever.
