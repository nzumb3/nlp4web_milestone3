Drink Tea Outside
###BREAK###
Sasha Polikov is a street performer from Russia, he is free spirited, and unconventional. When he arrived in Canada he discovered that the cultural clash is much greater than he could have ever foreseen. While falling in love with Maria, Sasha attempts to find balance between what he must do, and what he wants to do to survive in his new environment.
