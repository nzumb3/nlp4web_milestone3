Njan Kodiswaran
###BREAK###
Gopi (Jagadish) get's a reading from a fortune teller that he will become very rich soon. While returning from work one day, a suitcase discarded by some criminals who are being chased by cops comes into Gopi's possession by accident. The suitcase turns out to be filled with gold bars. Gopi thinks this new found wealth will change his life for the better, but it leads to bad luck for him and his family.
