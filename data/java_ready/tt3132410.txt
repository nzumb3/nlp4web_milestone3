Tras los pasos de Edith Stein
###BREAK###
It is a documentary that tries to explore the real essence of a woman who transcended herself on her way towards Faith and Truth. In this search we will travel through Breslau, her place of birth, up to coming to Auschwitz where she dies in 1942. Philosopher, feminist and humanist Edtih Stein has left a several written works where that does an anthropologic and philosophical study of the human being. Documentary about her life and work in that we will see a woman who fights to follow his way, a difficult way where she had to be faithful to herself and a life full of contrast where atheism and rationalism prevailed in her first years until both are replaced by spirituality and love and faith.
