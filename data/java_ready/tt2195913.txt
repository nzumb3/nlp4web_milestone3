Crop Circles the Enigma
###BREAK###
Steve Mitchell explores the enigma of crop circles, gaining insights as to the meaning of the enigmatic patterns and symbols found across the world. Crop Circle researcher, Charles Mallet, explains that crop circle design has been a constant evolution since 1978. One particularly interesting day was 7/7/2007 when a complex circle formed as people watched on.
