Sioux County Agriculture
###BREAK###
Follow host Andy Schuttinga as he visits a variety of farms across the Sioux County. Walk through herds of goats. Hear the rustling of the corn. Watch as hundreds of eggs go by your eyes in seconds. The rich soils and the abundant rains of Sioux County yield some of the most valuable farmland in the Midwest. From farmer to farmer, Sioux County is a supportive community of people striving for agricultural excellence.
