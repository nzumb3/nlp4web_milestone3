Balhay e zendegi
###BREAK###
This film is the true story of MahMonir, an unconventional Iranian woman. MahMonir was a successful pilot and parachuter before the revolution; she became an unknown farmer after the revolution. During her life she experienced terror and fear, sorrow and sadness. This story is about MahMonir with all the aspects of her soul, her attempt to be accepted in a paternal society , her love for humankind, her childhood. More, all the forces and limitations that religion and society have imposed on her makes her who she is today. Her passion for life has helped her to get through many hardships,giving us a picture of a truly amazing,woman.
