What Am I
###BREAK###
'What Am I' is a film about a man who after a string of heart breaks and failed relationships decides he will shut himself off emotionally from women. To fill the void, however, that being in a relationship fulfills, he decides to become a photographer of women, adapting an alter ego, a project hidden from his family and friends. But one of his biggest fans soon complicates his world when she joins him on a few shoots, and he finds that he must ultimately choose who exactly he wants to be, lest the decision is made for him.
