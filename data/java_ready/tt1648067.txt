DiMaggio: The Last Don
###BREAK###
Welcome to the life and business of Jamaica music executive Josef Bogdanovich and his company Downsound Records (Kingston, Jamaica). the viewer is taken directly into a seldom seen world from an insiders perspective- revealing layers of the business and the ups and downs associated with the process. Along the way, the viewer is permitted into the lives of the typical Jamaican studio and the characters who dream of making it.
