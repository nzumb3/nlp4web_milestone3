Sleep Debt
###BREAK###
When disturbed outsider Jack Fremont starts to see through another man's eyes in his dreams, it quickly becomes apparent the visions are much more significant than his usual nightmares. Realizing the dreams connect to the real world, Jack is forced to investigate and decipher their true meaning, each night revealing another piece to the puzzle. With lives at stake and time running out for both him and the mysterious stranger, Jack must depend on his friends and his own resolve as they race to solve the mystery. Filled with tangled relationships and a twist of fate, Sleep Debt is a clever supernatural drama that will pull you into another world.
