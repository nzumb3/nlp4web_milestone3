Mid Life Gangster
###BREAK###
Evan 'Bullet' James and his father are very close. After a disastrous new show pitch to a television network, the father tragically dies, leaving 'Bullet' alone in the world. 'Bullet' wants justice and seeks the help from the gang of his youth to exact revenge on the business world of Hollywood that he believes killed his father.
