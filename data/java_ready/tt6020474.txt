Ek Kulti Ek
###BREAK###
A well known singer Arun Deshpande separated from his wife Nandini and daughter Swara 18 years ago to pursue a career in singing he now lives a lonely life with just two people around his secretary Mehta and a good friend Madhura,One day he gets surprise visit in his house from his daughter Swara,Arun is not happy with her visit but is convinced by his secretary Mehta to allow her yo stay in the house, while living together Arun and Swara start knowing each other and discover the lost love of father and daughter in their relationship.
