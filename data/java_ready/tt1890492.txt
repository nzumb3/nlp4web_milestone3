Onnig's Inferno: The Element of Fire
###BREAK###
Onnig's Inferno: The Element of Fire is a feature-length documentary about sculptor Kardash Onnig and his Gaza Rebirth project. During the project, Onnig collaborates with other artists in an attempt to raise money for children in the Gaza Strip. When these efforts fail, Onnig takes matters into his own hands.
