5 Seasons: The Movie
###BREAK###
A seasonal story about a young woman's struggle (Jennifer) to find her true path in life. Along the way she goes through many wild and sometimes dangerous situations. Set in modern day South Jamaica Queens. In a young world without boundaries, sexual images, alcohol, drugs and Hip Hop, everyone is having the time of their lives, but things eventually spiral out of control. 5 Seasons speaks directly to people coming of age in this tech era, where love is a commodity and everything is disposable.
