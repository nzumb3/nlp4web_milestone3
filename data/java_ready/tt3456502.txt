Auf der Suche nach Jacqueline Bush
###BREAK###
A team of video bloggers is looking for Jacqueline Bush, a singer-songwriter from Graz, who seems to have disappeared without a trace back in 2010. Numerous interviews with members of the Austrian music scene the bloggers reveal memories, film and sound recordings. Bit by bit they unveil the singer's tragic story.
