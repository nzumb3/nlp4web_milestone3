Marble Hill: Unsafe at Any Price
###BREAK###
Marble Hill: Unsafe at Any Price is a documentary movie about the people who organized to successfully oppose the construction of a nuclear power plant downriver from Madison Indiana. Marble Hill: Unsafe at Any Price, is a people's history of the anti-nuclear movement in Indiana and Kentucky that was most active during the 1970s-1990s. It also tells of the story of the political and economic debacle that bankrupted what was one of the most well run electric utilities in the country. Marble Hill: Unsafe at Any Price, uses archival materials and features interviews with participants in the main environmental groups, whistle blowers and consumer advocates who worked to stop the power plant.
