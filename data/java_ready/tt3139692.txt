And Then Something Happened
###BREAK###
A romantic triangle between three outsiders in Tel Aviv. With Dina Limon, Iyar Wolpe, Avigdor Weil. 'After working for several grueling years as a night guard in a high-rise in Tel Aviv, Avi Kurtzweil found work at a camera store belonging to a friend of his.' In the camera store Avi meets Dita, and they become friends. Dita introduces Avi to her mother Zelda, Which furthermore complicates things.
