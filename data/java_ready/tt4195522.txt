Pilla Nuvvu Leni Jeevitham
###BREAK###
Seenu(Sai Dharam Tej) falls in love with Shailu(Regina) and proposes to her but gets rejected every time.Twist in the tale arises when Seenu comes to know that Shailu is in danger posed by dreaded goon and someone more powerful behind him and tale is how he protects her and wins her love
###BREAK###
Drama, Romance,
