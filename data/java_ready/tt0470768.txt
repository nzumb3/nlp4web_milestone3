Gangster
###BREAK###
Rosli is a stall owner who, in his quest to get rich, borrows money from illegal moneylenders. However he loses all his money in a get rich quick scheme and now owes the moneylenders a significant amount. Unable to pay, his wife and son are kidnapped by the moneylenders to ensure his repayment. Meanwhile a young man, Rizal, is involved with the illegal racing scene. He crosses paths with a notorious racer Legau and in their attempts to beat each other, they cause the deaths of innocent people. Ria, on the other hand, is involved in the sale of designer drugs headed by her boyfriend Nala. In a period of rebelliousness she meets Sufi, a nightclub waiter. Sufi is an addict but his relationship with Ria becomes so strong that he decides to take her away from Nala.
