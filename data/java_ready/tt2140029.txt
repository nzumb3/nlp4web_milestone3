Isola, where Bacchus' language is spoken
###BREAK###
Ethnopoetical point of view on 'Cantine Isola' legendary wine bar located in the heart of the Milan Chinatown, the film depicts the daily life of this place where artisans, known people, adventurers of the first hour meet there in order to taste subtle potions, but also to just be... measuring their fleeting existence with vintage wine.
