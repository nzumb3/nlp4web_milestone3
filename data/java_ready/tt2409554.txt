The Sunset Pavilion
###BREAK###
An epic voyage is set into motion when a Colorado street musician mysteriously comes across an ancient Indian manuscript. Compelled to discover the meaning behind the manuscript, he pawns is cello and travels to India. His search narrows as he is guided by the perennial wisdom of a mysterious 'voice'. The search comes to fruition once the musician emerges from a tragic event. This leads him to fulfilling a prophesy where his fate intersects with a street-bound Indian shoe shiner.
