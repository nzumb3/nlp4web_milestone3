Shadows
###BREAK###
Two young women are invited to a strange party in a large country house. There they join in a game where they play undead creatures hunting for the mortal victims in their midst - but things become sidetracked as the proceedings grow more disjointed and surreal, and they are less and less certain that the whole thing is just a creepy show... and more and more certain that there really is an evil lurking in the house.
