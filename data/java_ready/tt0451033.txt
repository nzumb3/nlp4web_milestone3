Death Ride
###BREAK###
The photographer Greg Ross has an argument with his wife Amanda about his affair with the Japanese model Yumi and he accidentally kills her. He decides to get rid off Amanda's body dumping it in the Black Lake. While driving in a road during the night, Greg recalls moments of his relationship with Amanda and Yumi and is haunted by their ghosts, driving him insane with a tragic conclusion.
