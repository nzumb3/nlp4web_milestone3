Ready for Love

Four women, all with different personalities struggle with real issues, men, and relationships. Jacqueline feels reluctant about men and relationships. But despite her overly independent and obnoxious attitude, she decides to take a chance at love and finds herself in a a whirlwind attempt to find "Mr. Right" Keisha, Christy, and Rene also find themselves battling issues of promiscuity, insecurity, and issues with trying to get a man. Real Life, Real Issues, and Hilariously Funny.
