De Rochemont: The Language of News
###BREAK###
Before there was television, Louis deRochemont's film series March of Time brought news of the world to American movie houses. He was the most-watched film producer of the 1930's and '40's as he reset the terms of the American documentary. Larger than life, Louie championed the dispossessed and did as much as any elected official to end US isolationism and propel America into WW II.
