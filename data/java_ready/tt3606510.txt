Trophy Pike Tactics
###BREAK###
Learn the secrets to catching trophy northern pike from a guide whose service has boated more than 1,000 pike over 40 inches! Watch the strategies and techniques to catching trophy predators as Predator Guide Service's own Steve Scepaniak reveals them. Explore fishing pike habitat from weed beds to rock piles, using a variety of baits including: Live Bait Rigging Crank Jerk Glide Plastics Spoons Bucktails Spinner And after you learn the techniques, see them in action as Steve and crew fish the U.S. and Canada boating one trophy after another. All the secrets to wall-mounting behemoths will be yours on Trophy Pike Tactics!
