Sin Rumbo Fijo
###BREAK###
This documentary narrates the journey of Jose, a man who decided launch a trip without a definite destination. His only occupation is to give love to whoever comes near him. His domicile is the street or wherever he can protect himself from the elements. Jose is full of memories and experiences , knowledge, he spends his time with his lovely dog which he calls Sahori. Everything he owns is wheelchair that helps him move around the streets of Santurce and a peculiar ATM machine that doesnt work. His past experiences, the situations that he had to face, the severe emotional stress that he endured, and the social-cultural shock helped him pick the streets as his home sweet home.
