I Want (No) Reality: Needcompany on Life and Art
###BREAK###
A personal story from a young Polish filmmaker Ana Brzezinska about the necessity of art, the price you have to pay for it and the search for the long forgotten secret of having a good life. A sensual and entertaining glimpse at a 5-year relationship of the director with a unique international theater ensemble Needcompany - a group of people dedicated to following their own vision of reality and willing to take all the risks and consequences it's going to take.
