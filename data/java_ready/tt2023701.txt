Sotiria
###BREAK###
Inexplicable reasons drive an ordinary city couple to choose a remote mountain village as their holiday destination. Through a series of paranormal events they come face to face with the impasse of their relationship as well as their actual being. The quest for a way out leads the protagonists on the verge of borderline situations and extreme choices. As they approach the edges of reason, reality fades into the subconscious, while they attempt to find Salvation.
