Norwegian Woods
###BREAK###
Joong-Rae (In-su Park) and Chang-Wook (Kyeong-ho Jeong) come to the deep forest to bury a body. When they almost finished digging a pit, they find out the body vanished. Myung-Sook (Seo-yun Ji) and Kyung-Soo are having an affair. Suddenly Myung-Sook sees something outside the car and asks Kyung-Soo to find out but he never returns. In the meantime, 3 high school students sniff on gas and glue.
