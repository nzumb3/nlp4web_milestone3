Boogie
###BREAK###
Boogie, a six year old boy steps out of the slums braving the dark with a failing glow stick. Like every other night, he sits patiently by the sidewalk and waits for his 20-year old mother to return from work. The sight of his mother walking down the street always make the boy's heart leap. Seeing his mother back has always been the sweet reward for his waiting. Boogie is a pimp who peddles his own mother in the street of Manila.
