Though the Heavens Fall: 100 Years of the BC Court of Appeal
###BREAK###
Set against the backdrop of a century of tumultuous social and political changes in BC, a complex documentary portrait of the highest court in the province- the British Columbia Court of Appeal. Featuring lawyers, justices, analysts, historians and plaintiffs from historic cases, this is a must-see for anyone who is interested in law, society and the making of the Canadian West Coast.
