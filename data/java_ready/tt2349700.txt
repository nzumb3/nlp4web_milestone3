Bhagaude
###BREAK###
'Bhagaude' in English mean a person who runs away from facing difficulties. Frustrated with their life 2 men facing mid life crisis and global slowdown run away from regular life in search of 'Nowhere'. They bike to unknown places with the determination of finding 'Nowhere'. The unplanned journey is filled with passion, danger, excitement, emotion, anger, friendship and shows different characters of a human being. The search leads them to an unknown place away from urban world where they find a beautiful temple with a lonely Priest. Will they be able to find the 'Nowhere'? According to Priest 'Moksha' can be achieved by following this words and advices But life is unpredictable, what happens to these 3 men? Will they get to 'Nowhere'?
