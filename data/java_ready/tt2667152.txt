Twisted Fates
###BREAK###
A trio of suspenseful horror stories, each with twisted plots and heaping' helpings of babes & blood! Two sorority sisters are jogging, then attacked by hooded figures. Just when you think you know what's happening, the story takes a shocking turn! A stunning bikini-clad woman meets a mild mannered trailer court resident at the pool. Someone is watching her, but she knows it and has plans to teach him a lesson. As the evening wears on, events unfold in shocking ways! Heartbroken Kelsey takes a house sitting job in the remote countryside. soon after the homeowners leave, strange and disturbing things start happening. Kelsey makes startling discoveries as nightmarish visions appear. Darkness falls as shadows of dread grow. Will Kelsey survive the night?
