Sinister Heaven
###BREAK###
College Student Tyler Wilkes is a Christian losing his faith. All of his prayers have left him unconvinced about the legitimacy of the Bible and the existence of God. Fueled by his spiritual obsession religious doubts, Tyler along with his Fiancee and friends delve into the dark side of spirituality. Sinister Heaven tells the story of a journey into the occult that will threaten their lives forever.
