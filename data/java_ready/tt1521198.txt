DeLorean - Living the Dream
###BREAK###
When the DeLorean was originally sold in 1981, the advertising slogan was "Live the Dream." Now, more than thirty years later, thousands of DeLorean owners from across the globe continue to keep their dream cars on the road. "DeLorean - Living the Dream" is the story of the iconic DeLorean automobile, from the rise and fall of legendary automaker John Z. DeLorean, to to the international phenomenon of loyal owners and devoted fans who've kept their dream alive for more than three decades.
