Children of the Wall
###BREAK###
Children of the Wall explores how the fall of the Berlin wall created a fertile ground for Berlin to rise as a creative world capital. The film focuses on the hopes, dreams, and struggles of 20 artists. Visual artists, musicians, street performers, photographers, filmmakers, singers, writers, a raw food artist, dancers, and arts administrators tell their personal stories about living and working in a city struggling for an identity after 30 years of division.
