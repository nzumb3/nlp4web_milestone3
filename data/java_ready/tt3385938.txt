On the Night: Robert Madge in Concert
###BREAK###
British singing sensation and London West End theatre star ROBERT MADGE performs live in concert during an engagement in America. The young lad from the West Midlands who co-starred in the Les Miserables 25th Anniversary Concert at London's famed O2 Arena, and other West End musicals including Mary Poppins and Oliver! is at the starting gate of what is sure to be a long and prosperous career as a multifaceted performer. You are invited to join this talented actor On the Night he performed his very first solo concert to a standing-room-only crowd. But more than a document of a fantastic musical evening, this film introduces you to Robert Madge personally through home videos and family photographs.
