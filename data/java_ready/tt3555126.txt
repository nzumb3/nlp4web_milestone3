P.U.L: Project Urban Legend
###BREAK###
In the fall of 2012 five college students enter a local state park to film and investigate a documentary. Several mutilated bodies have been discovered in the park. Authorities assume the deceased were victims of an animal attack. Believing there to be something more sinister at work, the five students launched an investigation to discover the truth behind the attacks. They were never seen again
