The Hour
###BREAK###
It is the last daylight savings time switch... ever. Jake is a struggling suburban teen who in an attempt to win the heart of the girl next door accidentally returns home with one of her father's most valuable possessions; a pocket watch that once belonged to Ben Franklin. When Jake awakes the next morning he finds himself trapped in time. Everyone he knows is gone. They are an hour ahead of him. As Jake struggles to get back home, he becomes entangled with a renegade group of strange people that are being hunted by the United States Government.
