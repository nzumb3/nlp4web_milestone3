Z HUNTERS: Bring on the DEAD!
###BREAK###
After the 1955 outbreak, the undead merely merged into our ecosystem (Without destroying the world in costly FX kinda way ). However, much like the common Bear or Wildcat, the undead will venture into populated areas when their food supply grows scarce. In order to prevent further outbreaks, the ZHAA (Zombie Hunting Association of America) has placed rapid response teams in key areas all over the country. This is the story about some of those guys.
