Kids from Khukhiin River
###BREAK###
The Documentary tells a story of school kids living in Munkhkhairkhan village in Hovd province. the remote western area of Mongolia. Children of herders from remote provincial areas leave their families and move to the village in order to go to school. away from parents care, the children learn to live and study independently from their early age. the children live together throughout three seasons in a year studying, playing and taking care of each other. They become a 'BIG FAMILY'. Being far away from their homes, the life at the dorm has taught these children to be strong, patient and independent.
