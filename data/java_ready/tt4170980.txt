Into the Void
###BREAK###
In the mist of incense smoke and firecrackers lay rituals that have been forgotten in modern Macao. These are people who have learned the arts of the occult and are only sought out when conventional medicine or practices don't work. They live among us, hidden in a city underneath clouds of incense fume and firecracker smoke, lost in time, slowly going closer...."Into The Void".
