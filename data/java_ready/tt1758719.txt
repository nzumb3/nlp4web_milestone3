Mexico: The Bad, the Great and the Ugly
###BREAK###
Looking for Adventure! Drama! Great Destinations? Then, come along as filmmaker, adventurer and pilot, Bill Behrenbruch, sets course for the GREAT and very remote Sierra Madre Mountains of Mexico. This is a territory largely inaccessible by road, rail or commercial air carrier, but if you gain access, this land offers endless canyon systems, incredible vistas, isolated cultures, unknown cliff dwellings, legends of lost treasure and solitude. But this is not paradise! There are those guys with guns, and there are a few other BAD things to consider. And, then there is the UGLY...!
