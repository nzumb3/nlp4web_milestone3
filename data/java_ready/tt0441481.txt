Cop Unbowed
###BREAK###
Ten years after being drummed out of the police force for killing a friend and colleague who was corrupt, ex-cop Lam (Alex Fong) and his girlfriend Kelly (Yoyo Mung) have settled down to a quiet life running a restaurant in New Zealand - a quiet life about to be ripped apart when mob boss Mr. Dick (Eddy Ko) returns to settle old scores.
