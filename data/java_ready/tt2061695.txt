How I Killed My Roommate... And Got Away with It
###BREAK###
This totally over the top korky comedy is about a Kwirky Killer/mob hit man 'ANGELO VETRANO' who wants to retire from the mob. BEFORE THE DON will grant Angelo his walking papers and 6.3 million pension he must meet 2 conditions: knock off the famed actor BRYCE FLAVOR, refrain from violence for the following 6 months while co-habitating with VLAD, the roommate from hell.
