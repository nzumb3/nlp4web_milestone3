Black Coffee
###BREAK###
Sometimes you wake up with the wrong person. Black Coffee deals with the hangover from the night before. John Dick wakes up, content with being unemployed. Lynn, an insecure rich bitch, finds herself falling in love with John, the "journalist for the local paper". Meanwhile, after years of marriage, Pat has had enough of Eve's too many late nights, leaving their teenage son, Ben, with the biggest hangover of all.
