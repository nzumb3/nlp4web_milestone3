Innimey Ippadithaan
###BREAK###
Seenu (Santhanam) is a happy go lucky guy. Everything goes well in his life until an astrologer predicts that he should get married within three months. By the time Seenu's parents scout a girl for him, he falls in love with Maha (Ashna Zaveri) but she keeps on ignoring him. At one point of time, Seenu says yes to an arranged marriage with Akila (Akila Kishore) due to the family circumstances and pressure mounted by his cunning uncle (Thambi Ramaiah.
