The U Reloaded: The Rise for 5
###BREAK###
The U Reloaded: The Rise for 5 is a documentary on the 2001 University of Miami football team. The documentary retells the team's journey on winning the national football title through the personal accounts of Ken Dorsey, Santana Moss, Joaquin Gonzalez, Reggie Wayne, Bryant McKinnie and many others.
