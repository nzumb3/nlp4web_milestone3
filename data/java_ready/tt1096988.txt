The Anatolian
###BREAK###
With her father Efendi's forceful-wish, Bahar, an Eastern Anatolian girl, at age of 16, gets married to an older man Hoca, a carpet store-owner in Los Angeles. After nine years, when his letter are returns back from USA, Efendi, her father, decides to go to Los Angeles and search for his long lost daughter Bahar.
