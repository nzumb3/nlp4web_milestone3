The Murderer
###BREAK###
Nickolas Allen, a man loved by some but hated by many, has everything. Will having everything cost him his life? It seems that everyone has a reason to want him dead. His wife, Ebony has finally had enough of many years filled with abusiveness and cruel treatment. Mylo Haywood, his best friend, has been burning secretly with resentment as he watches Nick steal everything he has and claim for his own. With so many unknown enemies with secret agendas, clear opportunities, and malicious motivates, one thing is clear, everyone is a SUSPECT. Nickolas has become a target for what many would call a forgivable crime, but who would dare? This short suspenseful piece is sure to keep you guessing.
