The Art of Moto
###BREAK###
Feel the experience of motocross. See the view from inside the helmet of a professional racer with this spectacular, artistic presentation of the toughest sport on earth. Featuring greatest of all time Ricky Carmichael, world icon Roger De Coster, current AMA champions, Ryan Villopoto, Ryan Dungey, world champions Ben Townley and Steve Ramon, WMA champion Ashley Fiolek, Sara Price and many more. Get the true feeling as a field of 40 racers take off side by side and then funnel into a turn that allows just one or two riders. See the techniques used by the riders to negotiate the ever changing surface of the dirt they race on. See the down side and perils of the sport and how these obstacles are overcome.
