Mattheus en Ik
###BREAK###
In Matthew and Me filmmaker Victor Vroegindeweij explores what he, as an atheist, can learn from his friend, the controversial evangelist Matthew van der Steen. This is the first time that Van der Steen allows the media into his church and personal life. Matthew van der Steen (37) is a rising star in the international Pentecostal movement. Matthew and Me is a gripping and very personal search into the meaning of life. In addition, the film offers a revealing and unique view into the life of a controversial evangelist and his organization.
