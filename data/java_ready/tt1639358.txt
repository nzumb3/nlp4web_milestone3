Blicke in die Leere
###BREAK###
A documentary about art of survivors of the Holocaust (poems from Batsheva Dagan and paintings from Egon Marc Loevith) and the memorial sites (Dachau and Ravensbrueck) as places of the survivors. Also Joel ben Izzy, author and storyteller from Berkeley CA, contributes a story leading into a discussion about memory and memorial sites.
