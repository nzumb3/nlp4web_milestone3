Freelance
###BREAK###
Jack is a young tired freelance hit-man with a code, he does not kill kids. When jack is about to call it quits for a white, he gets called back for one last job by his old boss Max, desperate for a vacation and some time off, he reluctantly takes the job by a young rich kid, Kyle who hires Jack to kill his girlfriend, Barbara. When Jack arrives to kill Barbara, he finds out that she is pregnant. It is up to Jack to find who is lying and who is being played, all the while trying to come to a decision on weather to kill Barbara or not, Jack may finally have to realize that killing has never been easy.
