Nandi
###BREAK###
Nandi is the name of a fictitious women's magazine that caters to the African-American woman. A freelance journalist, Frankie Reynolds, feels that Nandi is her only option for exposing some damaging information about a major pharmaceutical company. The story revolves around Frankie's life threatening adventure, investigating and subsequently publishing a story that not only sends shockwaves throughout the community but puts Nandi magazine in the middle of the controversy.
