Bao Zou Ma Ma
###BREAK###
The wedding ceremony is bustling with noise and excitement. In the school auditorium, students are busy with rehearsal. Suddenly the daughter falls down in the crowd, her face extremely white. Chen Guixian rushes to the hospital from the wedding. When Chen Guixian arrives at the hospital, she gets shocking news - her daughter has congenital hepatic insufficiency and needs liver transplantation immediately. The mother gives up the wedding resolutely without any explanation to Lao Huo. The mother, who has always been timid and afraid of pains, decides to donate her liver to her daughter. However, after a comprehensive check-up at the hospital, she is given an ironic answer-she has a severe fatty liver due to years of driving and hot temperature.
