Manyaa the Wonder Boy
###BREAK###
Manyaa The Wonder Boy. This is a Marathi film that beautifully presents the story of a champion who hails from a remote village in Maharashtra. It begins with his childhood and meanders around the difficulties that he overcomes and finally shines bright in glory, when he does his country proud by winning Gold in 100M at the most prestigious international event that is London Olympics.
