Broken Faith
###BREAK###
A compelling story of corruption, greed, guilt and salvation. Michael Joiner (The Grace Card) stars as Dan Zebrowski, a suicidal ex special operative so tormented by his past that he shuns everything he once held dear - his faith and family. But when corrupt ATF agent, Anson Toller (Rodney Wiseman) attempts to kill Calli, his eldest daughter, (Vanessa Ore) he has to face the personal demons that are slowly destroying his life, before Toller can kill her. This gritty tale follows one man's quest to rid himself of his past and find salvation in the gift of grace, power of faith and the love of family.
