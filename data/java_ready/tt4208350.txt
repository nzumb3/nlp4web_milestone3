The Haunting of Alice Rogue
###BREAK###
The haunting of Alice Rogue is a feature length documentary which follows 'Alice' who claims she has a malign force attached to her. 'Alice' explores the paranormal with presenter & producer Nick Stoppani meeting people heavily involved in the paranormal community to fully understand her physical attacks. The Haunting of Alice Rogue is a British Paranormal documentary.
