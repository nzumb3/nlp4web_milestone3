U.S. of D.I.Y.
###BREAK###
A document of the DIY punk scene in the U.S. from 2010 to 2011. Filmmakers toured 26 cities around the country capturing interviews, footage of shows, punk houses, record stores, info-shops, radical bookstores, art galleries and other places where urban, country-side and indigenous punks spread their passionate words about community, solidarity and lifestyle. Showing many sides of and sub-genres inside of this scene the film explore the passion for the punk life.
