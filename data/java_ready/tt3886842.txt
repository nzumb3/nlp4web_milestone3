Build It Play It Sell It
###BREAK###
This is the story of Paul David, aka Bandaloni. Paul is the one man band from Kitchener, Ontario that travels all over North America playing fairs and busking. This documentary was filmed over a two week period in July of 2013. It includes clips of him performing and Paul discusses how he came to be 'Bandaloni' and life on the road.
