Magische Plätze
###BREAK###
Carlo (Carlo Krebs) from Berlin meets Sofia (Tatiana Temerlin) from Buenos Aires on an internet platform. Carlo and Sofia chat intensively, exchanging photos and videos. At last they decide to produce a film together, a film that shows their two different lifes on two different places on the globe. Lugares Mágicos, a tribute to the 20th anniversary of the city partnership of Berlin and Buenos Aires, tells episodes and stories about Carlo and Sofia, focussed on a single day. Lugares Mágicos is the story of a digital and far away friendship, embedded in magical places of Berlin and Buenos Aires.
