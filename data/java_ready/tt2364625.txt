Collector's Waltz
###BREAK###
This documentary film features two Houston art collectors, reflecting upon their passion for collecting art and the close relationships they have developed with various artists and how this has impacted on their lives. The film documents interviews with the collectors, a local gallery owner and six Texas based emerging and mid-career artists. The film's themes will resonate with emerging collectors and artists, covering being a patron of the arts, sponsoring and mentoring emerging artists and enabling a network of art lovers, collectors and artists. Kristine Mills, an award winning Houston vocalist, wrote the original soundtrack. 'Collector's Waltz' has won an award at the 2012 Houston WorldFest.
