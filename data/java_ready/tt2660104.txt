Motel: A or How I Crashed My Plane on Isla Sorna and Fell in Love with Sandra
###BREAK###
An artistic motel bellhop on a lonely dinosaur island falls in love with five plane-crashed girls. He works for his boss, Mr. Clandestini, an aging drug lord and pimp De luxe, who rules the island and lives in his Clandestini residence in the middle of the jungle with his five exotic girls. Main question: Why reduce myself to one woman?
