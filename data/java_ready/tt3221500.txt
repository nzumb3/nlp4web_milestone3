Jaimeo Brown's Transcendence
###BREAK###
Beauty. Goodness. Truth. Some journeys start in the middle. For Jazz percussionist, Jaimeo Brown, this journey finds him searching for the roots of the spirituals that inspired his 1st solo album. This documentary portrait of an artist follows the pains of the past, the trials of the present and the hopefulness of the future. It is here, where everyone will take part in Jaimeo Brown's Transcendence.
