Theology of Human Suffering: Understanding the Redemptive Nature of Human Suffering
###BREAK###
A documentary on the theology of human suffering that describes the epidemic of obesity and sexually transmitted infections (STIs) in American society, and their link to a moral, physical, and spiritual suffering in the American populace. It draws the viewer into trying to comprehend the societal and individual consequences and to embrace a possible solution to this devastation.
