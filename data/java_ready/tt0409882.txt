Deseta zapoved
###BREAK###
Born in 1978, in Jesenice, Slovenia. Film started to interest him in his secondary school years. He is a student at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering. In his spare time he makes amateur short films. After a seminar organized by the Republic of Slovenia Public Fund for Cultural Activities, he drafted the outline for his first feature film project (Deseta zapoved, 2003).
