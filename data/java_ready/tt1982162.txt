Sun Devil Tough
###BREAK###
The Arizona State University Wrestling Team was dropped due to budget cuts then reinstated after the demands of the community. The Sun Devils returned to the practice room with the intention of putting the program back on the map at the 2011 NCAA Tournament. The 2011 Sun Devils included seniors Anthony Robles who is now a motivational speaker, nominated for and ESPY Award and due to receive the prestigious Jimmy V award at the 2011 ESPYs; and Bubba Jenkins who now competes in professional mixed martial arts. The way you train is the way you will compete.
