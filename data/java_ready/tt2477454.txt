Hooked on Comix
###BREAK###
In Hooked on Comix, Dame Darcy, a self-proclaimed witch and creator of the comic book Meatcake, contemplates a career that crosses over into music and the supernatural worlds. Tony Millionaire, creator of the comic strip Maakies, spins strange yarns with his hilariously rendered characters, Drinky Crow and Uncle Gabby, who like Tony, drink heavily and confront a hostile world with dark humor.
