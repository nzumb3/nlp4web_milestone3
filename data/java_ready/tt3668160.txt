Case at Midnight
###BREAK###
Charles Rainer is about to be sentenced for his crimes; however, his group of all female cult followers have other plans when they siege the building massacring almost everyone. A deadly cat and mouse game erupts when a group of security guards take Rainer hostage and try to use him in order to survive the night against the cult girls trying to break him out of the courthouse. With no power and little hope a night of justice turns into a night of death!
