Holding Lucid
###BREAK###
Set in the desert of southwest Nevada, 'Holding Lucid' is a post-apocalyptic, dramatic thriller. After a series of horrifying events, a tracker pursues and captures a cannibal, then sets about to change his way of thinking. Representing good and evil, they are both very intelligent men who each have solid, intellectual reasoning to justify their approaches to life.
