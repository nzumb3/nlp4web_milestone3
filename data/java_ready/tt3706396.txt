Forever Changed: 9/11 in Remembrance
###BREAK###
The day America was Forever Changed - 9.11.2001. Jay Sekulow & ACLJ Films brings you a compelling story of the most deadly attack on American soil - told from the perspective of those who sacrificed the most - the heroes who survived 9/11 & the families who lost loved ones. Featuring Former US Attorney General John Ashcroft & many 9/11 survivors and family members of those who lost their lives.
