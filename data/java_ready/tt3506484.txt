Voice of Spring
###BREAK###
A student interacts with different people at home, at school and around the neighborhood. He wants to buy a bicycle and make a kite, but his father won't let him. His friends and classmates reject him and tease him. The continuous shouting from teachers, father, and friends in his head drives him crazy. His anxiety is increased due to the pressure of school exams. Pushed to the edge, he unleashes his wrath.
