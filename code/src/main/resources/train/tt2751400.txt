Hundreds Tens and Units
###BREAK###
Hundreds Tens And Units is an independent film made for less than 3000gbp and shot in five days. It has an onscreen cast of two, and had a crew of four. It is 93 minutes. Set in only one room, the film follows Ran and Mal- trapped inside by an outbreak of H5N1 flu. With only a radio for information, and as the situation deteriorates, the boundaries between reality and imagination blur, the two struggle to come to terms with each other, and the horror outside.
