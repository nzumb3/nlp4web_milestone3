Nóirín Ní Riain, Voice on the Edge
###BREAK###
This one hour documentary explores the extraordinary life of Nóirín Ní Riain, an internationally acclaimed Irish singer of spiritual songs from many traditions. Nóirin has devoted her life to music, chant, family and spirituality. We will learn about Nóirin's spiritual development, her varied musical collaborations and her personal journey as mother and wife - which includes a difficult parting from her husband, the renowned musician Micheal O'Suilleabhain.
