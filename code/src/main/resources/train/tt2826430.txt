Hipster! The Musical
###BREAK###
Dave is a shy guy who can't bring himself to confess the love he has for his best friend Lily. But when she joins a cult of hipsters, Dave must embrace his inner irony for one last shot at Lily's love. Dave finds himself at odds with the maniacally-sarcastic Salinger. Worse than that, Dave is at odds with himself. And only through song and dance will he muster the courage it takes for a happy ending.
