Keshu
###BREAK###
Keshu is a deaf; mute child who stays with his maternal uncle.Being quite mischievous, there are never ending complaints from the villagers.Shalini, a teacher transferred to that village stared living with the family.She encourages him to take up drawing; convinces him to go to school.When he wins an international award for his painting he becomes the darling of the whole village.
