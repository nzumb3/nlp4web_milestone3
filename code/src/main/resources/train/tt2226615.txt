###BREAK###
The rise and fall of the largest orchestra and production company in the Netherlands: Holland Symfonia. From its origin in 1813 to the ending in 2013, this documentary film displays a truly unique organization with a broad range of activities. With severe budget cuts up to 73% the Dutch government ended the existence of HS, leaving the organization, its environment, fans and stakeholders in shock and agony and disbelief.
