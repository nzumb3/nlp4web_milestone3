Pelada, Favela's Soccer
###BREAK###
"Pelada, Favela's soccer" portrays the passion of the boys with the ball at his feet, the passion of the fans on the field and the universe that surrounds the everyday life of this kind of soccer. Striking images of the "pelada" moves in a unique movie, with remarkable testimonials of champions who came from communities, discovered their talent playing "pelada" and conquered the world - a world of challenge surrounded by the twist of samba, the rap funk and the identity rap.
