Come Morning
###BREAK###
Just before dusk, Frank and his grandson track a recent kill and find that, instead of a deer, they've shot their trespassing neighbor. With a history of land disputes with the family fresh on Frank's mind, he decides their only option is to hide the body. As the night draws on, they find themselves only deeper in darkness and soon begin to wonder if everything will really be okay come morning.
