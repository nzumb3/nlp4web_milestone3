Z.A.N.
###BREAK###
Adam Manish tells his psychologist about his psychic gifts. He is convinced that someone is trying to kill him because of that. Besides his psychologist, Zaid Hoder, thinks that everything is part of a big hallucination, indeed there are some mysterious people chasing him. In order to scape from the same odd dream and compulsive drawing, he takes a lot of medicine capable of killing any regular person. He finds out he is more than just a human.
