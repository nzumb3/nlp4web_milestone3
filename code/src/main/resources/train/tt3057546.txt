To Kill a Cow
###BREAK###
Pedro a vasc Cheff wents into a trip across all Europe, up to Norway where his next job is waiting, running away from his senseless routine of alcohol and decadence. On the way he attacks all the society meaning that he is not the only one responsible of his actual bad situation. In parallel he awakes his interest for the world through the gastronomy of the differents countries he passes through. He ended founding places and people that gave sense to his life.
