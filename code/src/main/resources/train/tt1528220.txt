Invitation to Darkness
###BREAK###
Invitation to Darkness is a story about Casey. Casey is the lovable rich girl who everyone has an ulterior motive for being her friend, and she is having a party. All her friends from school are invited and also her cousin Vanessa and Vanessa's boyfriend. At the same time, the party of the year is happening at Reklaw Woods. Vanessa, her crew, and a few others are torn between which invite to accept. The wrong choice could be deadly.
