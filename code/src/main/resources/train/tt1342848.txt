Dark Journey
###BREAK###
A lonely confused beautiful young woman from a small town in the U.S moves to London and is tormented in her flat by voices which she has no control over. There is a demonic force in the flat she has recently moved into and her landlord is very strange. She befriends local people in the area, but doesn't know who is genuine and who is not. She ends up changing from a sweet naive girl to a sexy vamp with attitude in order to survive in the remorseless city which is 21st century London.
