Gutbai, Charly
###BREAK###
The devastating consequences of the oil pollution in the coast of Rias Baixas force Xaquin, a widowed fisherman, to work for a local drug dealer. What at first became a choice to support both his son and father, soon becomes a constant duel with himself, his everyday life and the people surrounding him. After a series of unexpected events take place, Xaquin will en up in a critical situation he wasn't ready for.
