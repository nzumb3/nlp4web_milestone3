The Second Act of Elliott Murphy
###BREAK###
Elliott Murphy, born in New York in 1949 and living in Paris since 1989, has published over thirty albums and plays almost one hundred shows a year. The films follows him during one of his Spanish tours, in the company of French guitarist Olivier Durand; and it shows their routines of travels, hotels, sound checks... while the interviews reveal the personal side of this veteran singer/songwriter.
