One Window Will Suffice
###BREAK###
Narges and her mother live in an apartment in Tehran. Despite her mother who is a traditional woman, Narges wants a modern lifestyle and this issue fires a lot of conflicts and problems between them which ultimately creates distance. Narges who is suffering from depression and obsession, hides her relationship with her boyfriend from her mother. Her emotional crises entwined with feelings of guilt and depression, leads her into a dangerous situation.
