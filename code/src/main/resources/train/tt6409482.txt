Dead Ring
###BREAK###
Afraid go out or engage in life, Emily (Savanah McMahon) seeks an unorthodox therapist who gives her a spell that will rid her of her phobia by summoning a Dead Ring. A doppelganger she can create embodying the worst traits she sees in herself. After Emily's twin emerges from her closet, a strange friendship develops. Soon dark forces require Emily to make a hard choice.
