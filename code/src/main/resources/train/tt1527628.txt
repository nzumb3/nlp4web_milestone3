Hell Is Other People
###BREAK###
Morty can't seem to make heads or tails of anyone: himself, least of all. And to make matters worse, all he's got in his pockets is a collection of heads and tails. What if he were to go into business for himself? He's got a Jeep, and there are plenty of empty parking lots in the seedier areas of Chattanooga. Anything to make a buck, right?
