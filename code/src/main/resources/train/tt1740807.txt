Lonely Planet
###BREAK###
A film crew sets out on an unusual, adventurous quest throughout Siberia in search of an elusive Holocaust survivor who was a small boy during WWII. He hid out from the Germans for 3 years while living among a pack of wolves in the forest. Truth, reality and fantasy are combined in this film and create a fresh, new combination, never seen before.
