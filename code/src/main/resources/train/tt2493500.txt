Bits of Bob
###BREAK###
Steve is spending a nice summer's afternoon in the park when he discovers a dismembered corpse. He persuades his friend Dan, who is supposed to be meeting his girlfriend, to help him find the rest of the body and dispose of it. Meanwhile Murray, a depraved detective-wannabe-serial-killer fanatic thinks he is on the trail of two twisted murderers...
