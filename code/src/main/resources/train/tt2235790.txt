V-Day 11.11.11
###BREAK###
V-DAY 11.11.11 portrays the collective experience of being a veteran in our country. Cameras followed these individuals on November 11, 2011 and beyond for a glimpse into the lives of those who protect and defend our country. Weaving together these profiles and stories from all across the US, the audience witnesses what it means to serve in both peace time and at war. This film represents men and women from all wars, all ranks of service, and from all regions of the country, tied together with the commonality of being a US veteran.
