The Other Side of Bollywood
###BREAK###
A documentary about the making of films in different regions of India and the social forces, industry forces, and censor board that shape moviemaking there. Several actors, actresses, and directors are interviewed, such as Amrita Arora, Dev Benegal, Rahul Bose, Vasundhara Das, Zayed Khan, and Deepa Mehta.
