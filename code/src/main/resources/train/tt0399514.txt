Problemas personales
###BREAK###
Antonio, Jorge and Geovany are Ecuadorians and have come to Madrid looking for a job. While Antonio opens a lucrative weekend business at a local park, Jorge awaits for the release of his work permit and Geovany wanders around the city looking for a job. As time passes by their problems arise. Antonio does not want to go back to his native country which has become a land of no hope; Jorge will not return until he has achieved his goals and Geovany is obsesed with the idea of going back.
