Liquor Store Cactus
###BREAK###
Kohl Perry is a high school slacker with a life that is leading towards nothing. An unexpected opportunity arises as he finally "meets" Sarah Kim, a girl he's attended school with since the third grade. Just as Kohl's life seems like it's headed down the right path, a fork in the road will lead Kohl towards his bittersweet fate.
