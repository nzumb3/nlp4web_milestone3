The Biggest Thing That Ever Hit Broadway
###BREAK###
A musical show is a chance for the untrained, untried, untested Richard Howell, son of one of the misguided backers, to get into show business. But a fateful encounter between Howell and an irascible Genie jeopardises the show and shatters Howell's life. The bigger part he wished for becomes too much for him to handle...
