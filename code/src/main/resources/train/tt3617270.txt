Memoir of a Cannibal
###BREAK###
All started as a bizarre, macabre, cannibalistic obsession.Now 25 years later the man decides to take it a few steps further. He gets the idea to place an internet advertisement in search of a man to consume. He shockingly yields a willing victim to serve up his own flesh in this bizarre, macabre; fantasy horror film.
