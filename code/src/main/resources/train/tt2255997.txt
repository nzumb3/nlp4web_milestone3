The Lion of Judah
###BREAK###
The Lion of Judah tells the story of Holocaust survivor Leo Zisman, who leads a group of young adults on a journey back to Poland and through humanity's darkest period. Inspired by the strength of Leo, The Lion of Judah's powerful message encourages viewers - Jews and non-Jews - to remember the past and use it to change the world for the better.
