Darkslide
###BREAK###
DARKSLIDE is the story of Damen Krow, a young musician with Asperger's syndrome. He works at a sawmill part time, but his true passion is playing guitar. He desperately wants to reach people with his music. He spends most of his time alone, and is haunted by past traumas. Through unique circumstances he is introduced to a young artist named Naomi Sinclair. Naomi is his first real connection and introduces him to the possibilities of social interaction and the chance to embark on his dream of being a guitarist in a band. Will Damen and his band P.F.C. rise out of obscurity or will life forever slide down deeper into the dark... ?
