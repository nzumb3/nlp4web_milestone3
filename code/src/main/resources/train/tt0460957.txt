Texxas Jam '78
###BREAK###
The first real look at an event that crammed 100,000 people into the Cotton Bowl in Dallas, Texas to celebrate Rock n' Roll in 1978. The all day concert featured Aerosmith, Heart, Journey, Eddie Money, Head East, Frank Marino and the Mahagony Rush, Tedd Nugent, and others!!!
