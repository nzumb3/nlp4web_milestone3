Factory of One
###BREAK###
A documentary filmed in Portland, OR about the architect, designer, builder and self-contained factory of Emerald City - a theme camp at Burning Man 2010. The film follows his progress as he builds the entire camp and structure practically all by himself over four months and continues to track his adventures at Burning Man itself. Throughout his journey he maintains a strong work ethic and almost maniacal drive despite numerous setbacks and the grinding progress of time.
