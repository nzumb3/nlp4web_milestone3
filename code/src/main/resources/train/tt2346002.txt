Halfway There
###BREAK###
Nick Bruno (Rocco Chierichella) leads the wild party life of a star athlete at his small-time college, but when the party ends, Nick finds himself on the wrong side of the law. Facing a prison sentence for buying drugs from an undercover police officer, Nick sacrifices his promising baseball career for time in rehab. Enrolling at the Summerville Rehabilitation Center, Nick quickly discover the long road to recovery requires more than just saying no. Based on the long-running and award-winning play seen by over 2,000,000 students, inmates and rehab residents, Halfway There takes you inside an addicts process of self-discovery.
