Waterfront Nightmare
###BREAK###
"A Waterfront Nightmare" is a psychological thriller with twists and turns. Seven college friends spend their spring break in a remote waterfront cabin for a week of partying. When they arrive, they discover something is not right with their cabin owner and the other locals. With a mysterious man filming their every move and a group of Goths showing up at their cabin, they discover that their lives are in jeopardy. The partying stops when they each start disappearing one by one.
