A Stand Up Paddle Movie
###BREAK###
'A Stand up Paddle Movie' is a documentary about the beginning of Stand Up Paddle Sports. This documentary profiles the top athletes in three disciplines of Stand Up Paddle, which include surfing, open water racing, and circuit races. Shot on location in Hawaii and California, high definition event footage includes the Sunset World Pro, The Battle of Paddle Hawaii and California, The Catalina to Dana Point Crossing, The Tahoe Nalu, Hennessey's, and Utah.
