Cosimo and Nicole
###BREAK###
2011. Genoa. G8. Cosimo, an Italian boy, helps Nicole, French. A look: it is love. Then the two boys return to Genoa; here they work for a friend, Paul, concert organizer. This goes on until Alioune, a young illegal immigrant, falls from a scaffold, working for a stage with Cosimo. The boy remains there, abandoned by Paul, Cosmas and Nicole.
