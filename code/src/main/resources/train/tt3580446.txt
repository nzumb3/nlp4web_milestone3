Winnerman
###BREAK###
Trent Wienerman is the star quarterback of Tom Brady High School. Trent, along with the rest of the football team, relentlessly bullies the rest of the school, but when he is asked by the coach to bully his best friend Ned, he can't. After realizing the error of his ways, Trent dons a costume and, with the help of Ned, decides to take down the football team as the superhero Winnerman.
