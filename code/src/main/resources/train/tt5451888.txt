Keep Shut
###BREAK###
When Mark's troubled friend Christopher moves in, David's life begins to unravel. With Christopher's hostility towards David growing by the day, David braves one last attempt to salvage their relationship. But if things were bad before, they can only get worse, as David is forced to deal with the consequences of his actions if he wants to preserve his friendship with Mark - and his sanity.
