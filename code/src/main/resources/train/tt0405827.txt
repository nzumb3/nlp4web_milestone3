PaPa Loves You
###BREAK###
Ying is a single parent who's having a tough time disciplining his wild teenage daughter Ellen. When Ying is mistaken to be the famous gangster hero "Shadowless Eagle", he gains both recognition and popularity among Ellen's gangster classmates, and she begins to gain new respect for her father. But Ying's legend becomes more than he can handle when some real triad members come seeking revenge.
