The Children of Terra Firma
###BREAK###
The story of a mysterious UFO cult, and their connection to a series of disappearances in the woods outside Eugene, Oregon. A family went missing in 1982, a heavy metal band in 1992, and a duo of documentary filmmakers in 2002, but the answers are finally revealed in 2012 when the host of paranormal reality show discovers the truth.
