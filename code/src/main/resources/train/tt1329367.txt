The Democratic party's takeover of the House of Representatives in 2006 was more than just a good year. As HOUSEQUAKE illustrates, it was the result of an unprecedented, high-risk strategy and a relentless effort led by the colorful, iconoclastic congressman Rahm Emanuel. This historic campaign for change paved the way for Barack Obama's presidential victory and transformed forever the way political races are run in America.
