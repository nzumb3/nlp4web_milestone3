A Night in Bodrum
###BREAK###
Ejder Kalender, a young man in his 25s, has a lifelong aspiration of being a well known actor. Despite his relentless attempts, he is far from his dreams and currently works as a technician in a cable TV company in Bodrum. Upon learning about the Bodrum weekend trip of one of the top producers Baris Savas and a well-known actor Metin Leylek, Ejder drafts a plan make a house visit. His plan and the night goes way off track. If Ejder can achieve his dream after the messy night remains a critical question.
