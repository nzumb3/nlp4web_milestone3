Purple Haze
###BREAK###
Purple Haze is a movie about a talented musician from Haiti. The CEO of Colossal Records got wind of this raw talented musician and signed him to a deal. Sam Lucci the owner of Colossal Records is not the most honest business man. Damian Ship aka Purple Haze is not that familiar with the record business. When Sam decide to swindle Damian of his money, he get a lesson in true voodoo economics.
