In the Lines of My Hand
###BREAK###
In The Lines of My Hand is a conversation piece with 19 world renowned artists. The piece includes many people that have worked and lived in Venice, California starting in the early 60's and with many of them remaining there today. 'In The Lines of My Hand' explores influences, loves, feelings about one's self and one's work in an intimate discussion offered in each artist's own voice.
