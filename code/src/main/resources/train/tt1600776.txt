Memory of September
###BREAK###
Aya lives alone in a city, where she cannot really find her place to live. One day, she goes up to the mountain, where she meets an old man with his old wife in wheelchair at a shrine. Invited by him to his deserted home, the studio of Masks, this dreaming girl goes deeper into the mountain beyond the point of no return...
