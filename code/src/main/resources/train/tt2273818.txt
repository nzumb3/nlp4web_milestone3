Going Deep for Vancouver
###BREAK###
Since her childhood Margriet de Schutter has had a dream: to compete at the Olympics in Vancouver as a short track speed skater. Years of vigorous training bring her success, but also a battle with an increasing number of injuries. When she loses her place in the National Team, Margriet suffers a mental crisis. How will she cope without her sport, will she still know who she really is? Her mental coach advises her to find her true self again. The same passion that drove Margriet to 'go deep' as an athlete enables her to go deep in this very personal and revealing documentary.
