Inevitable
###BREAK###
George and Gina own a funeral parlor. Gina is a social gambler and drinker who likes the flashy things that George provides her. Fueled by alcohol, Gina's gambling habit leads to an awkward relationship with Micky the bookie. As Micky entices Gina deeper into his world, Gina finds her life in serious jeopardy while Micky is stricken by the consequences falling deeply in love. As Gina is tempted by the economic potential of Micky's world, love and greed collide with unintended results.
