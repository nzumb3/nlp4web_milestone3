Executives Minds
###BREAK###
This is based upon a true life scenario of lives places around all parts of the United States. Their are four individuals (2) Males "Roy" an Business Owner "Security Consultant" & " Stefon" an Owner of A "Construction Corporation" 2 Females "Joyce" Own of an Attorney Firm & "Kerri" Owner of A Banking Industry. They grew up in the streets of Baltimore, Maryland. Having life tragic moments, and traumatizing experiences that will cause them a shift and change of lifestyles. As they become successful CEOs and Entreprenuers years has pass, they decided to come back to the neighborhood they grew up in to help restructure the lives of people they once knew. As 4 executive minds come together to create and rebirth a new future.
