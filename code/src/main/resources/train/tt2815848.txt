Owalini
###BREAK###
Since their father doesn't have enough money to educate him, Laxmi and her husband have paid for Shiva's fees till his graduation. Shiva and Laxmi share an extremely loving relationship and they would do anything for each other. Thanks to the efforts of Laxmi and her husband, Shiva grows up to be a fine young lad, he earns fame as a wrestler and soon, joins the Indian army as a Captain. Meanwhile, there is a nexus of a money-lender, land-lord, a local MLA and a police inspector, who have an eye on the land owned by Laxmi's husband. The goons attack him and in the incident, he loses his arm. On the other hand, news comes that Shiva has been killed in action at the Indo-Pak border.
