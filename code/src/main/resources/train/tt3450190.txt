Carl and Sandy Say Goodbye
###BREAK###
The brother-sister duo Carl and Sandy have been at the news reporting business for years. After a short break from their show, however, their director has found out that bad ratings has put the fate of future newscasts at risk. Facing cancellation and a desire to move on in life, the three decide to band together to produce one final episode. As time goes on, they discover a deep conspiracy to end their show... five years in the making! Filled with pulse pounding music and an entertaining story line, this heartwarming film tells the story of two kids who find out the hard way that saying goodbye is the most powerful message of all.
