Honorable Retribution
###BREAK###
Johnny Lespin is a man on the verge of changing his profession to take on a more practical way of life. He is the voice and muscle for Don Ferero, the head of an underground crime syndicate known as the Cannach. Johnny becomes intertwined in a conspiracy involving a young woman who shows him a new way to view life. Now, Johnny is forced to confront the organization he worked to uphold, deciding between staying loyal to Don Ferero or seeking retribution for a woman he barely knew.
