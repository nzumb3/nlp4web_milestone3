Father's Day
###BREAK###
Thomas, a successful lawyer, is happily married with two children, but has an affair. His mistress wants a child from him - without any obligations - and although he agrees to "do it", it doesn't work out. After having a test, he is told that he can't have children - ever. But what about the kids he already has?? Thomas seeks advice from his brother, who happens to be a minister.
