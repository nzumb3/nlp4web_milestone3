March!
###BREAK###
Jennifer, an overzealous tenant, takes on her landlord after discovering that he is kicking everyone out of her Lower East Side building. A road trip from lower Manhattan to Brooklyn, Jennifer and her cohorts travel to confront their tyrant landlord and attempt to pick up supporters along the way. Based on a true story, MARCH! follows one ridiculously passionate woman's battle to "fight the man" and keep her home while rallying the city to her noble cause.
