Nug
###BREAK###
Nug is a film about a young women, Eva from Sweden. She is on path where learning about herself and the ways of life is in focus. Seeking the gold of life is a metaphor for a balance or concept of how one can live life with enjoyment and peaks of high highs and except that it comes with low lows. To be present in the moment but still have a plan for a long and substantial life. Eva is writing a essay for the university and document her learning's from a long travel that take start in Hamburg and then land in deeper consciousness throughout the long country roads of the Yukon territories up the most northern part of Canada. She learns that fortune favor the brave. This is Eva's adventure.
