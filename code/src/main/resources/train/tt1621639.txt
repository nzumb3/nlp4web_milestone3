Is That It?
###BREAK###
A Northern comedy romance It's the last thing a girl loses and the first thing a woman gives away. A moment in their life they will remember for the rest of their life. From the early eighties when computers and mobile phones were just a thing of the future. Redcar a seaside town on the tip of North Yorkshire reveals the true-life stories of five Northern lasses, now women, who learned the real meaning of "is that it?" With funny, sad and emotional consequences.
