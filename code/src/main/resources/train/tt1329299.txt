32 Hours 7 minutes
###BREAK###
My desire to document a close family friend's 23 year old speed record in the infamous cross country race, the Cannonball, ends up raising questions and suspicions I can't answer with interviews alone. In order to vindicate his effort I end up in the back seat of Alex's Roy's BMW M5, racing across the country to prove that 32 hours and 7 minutes is possible.
