I Am Not My Body
###BREAK###
'I Am Not My Body' is the story of a young 9-year-old Romanian boy who was severely burned on more than 75% of his body because of a house fire. Even though he lost his nose, eyelids, all 10 fingers, and his parents as a result of the fire, he still chooses to see the positive things that happen in life despite his personal circumstances.
