A Riddle of Steel
###BREAK###
The underground knife culture is going mainstream. The ancient art of maiming and killing with the knife is back. In a bid for self-defense, power and identity men young and old gather at the Riddle of Steel training camp. 'A Riddle of Steel' makes the journey, encountering difficult questions and disturbing ideas about human nature and the future of society.
