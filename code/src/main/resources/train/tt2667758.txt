PiCKLES: The Film
###BREAK###
A Motley crew of 5 embarks on a roller coaster journey of producing a feature film. With equipment going missing, a nagging wife, lack of funds, a disoriented costume designer, a love triangle, a haunted office, threats from the Malaysian mafia, a star crossed childhood love would Arvind, the metro sexual chief, be able to overcome the odds and make his dream movie or would it all just be his ultimate nightmare?
