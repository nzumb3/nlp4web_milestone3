Crack Willow
###BREAK###
An affectionate portrait of one man's struggle with death and loneliness. After the passing of his father Mark Walker embarks upon a nihilistic journey which, eventually leads him to confront his pain and loss. Crack Willow is an exploration of emotions captured through the use of vivid, powerful and imaginative imagery, a compelling and evocative film that treads new ground.
