A Break in the Clouds
###BREAK###
A post-apocalyptic thriller made by two guys with a passion for film and an out of pocket budget of $3000. Set in Montana, Maine, a man trapped in by the weather sets out after spotting smoke on the horizon. Upon coming to the smoke he meets a young boy. The two are immediately confronted by two other survivors who give them a chance at hope by opening their home, but first Maine and the boy must take a risk. Soon they are plunged into and long a dark adventure leading them into 'A Break in the Clouds'.
