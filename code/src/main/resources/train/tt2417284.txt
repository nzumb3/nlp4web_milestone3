Raising Wolves
###BREAK###
Two factions, the Tender Trios, a dangerous female gang and the Wolf Pack, teenage street hustlers get caught up in a deadly web of deception, masterminded by Ox, a drug kingpin, after he plans a fake kidnapping to extort money from the Tender Trios. Things go awry when they refused to pay without a fight.
