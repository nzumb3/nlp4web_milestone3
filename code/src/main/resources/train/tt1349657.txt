The Yin of Gary Fischer's Life
###BREAK###
*Alternate Music Edit A comedic drama about what comes after the 'coming-of-age' movie. Gary Fischer is a middling freelance writer living in an average small town. While his two best friends appear to be happy with their daily routine of coffee shop discussions, afternoon croquet, and late-night card games, Gary is increasingly discontent.
