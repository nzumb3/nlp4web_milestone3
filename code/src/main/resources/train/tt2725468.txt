The Tale of An Phuc House
###BREAK###
'The Tale of An Phuc House' is a powerful glimpse into the everyday lives of twenty disabled children - third generation victims of the Agent Orange warfare that occurred during the Vietnam War (1963-1973). An inspirational video tale about compassion, dignity and love; a story of a man who dedicate his live for the well-being of his adapted family; a story of twenty exceptional young adults, whose disabilities brought them together under one roof. A heartbreaking story about an ordinary people living an extraordinary life in spite of their misfortunate physical conditions.
