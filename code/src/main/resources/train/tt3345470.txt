An Even Worse Joke
###BREAK###
Comedy is an illusive art form. For comedy to work, context must be understood, timing must be impeccable, delivery must not be stunted, not to mention material must be bursting with quality and depth. Well, none of those apply to Ivan Lovegren, the world's first HumorisNt. 'An Even Worse Joke' follows his pursuit in 2013 to tell one of his classic bad jokes every day for the entire year, with the promise that each joke is 'Even Worse' than the previous one. Did he 'succeed'? We'll let you be the judge of that...
