The RuneScape Documentary: 15 Years of Adventure
###BREAK###
The RuneScape Documentary is here. Find out how one of the most successful MMORPGs of all time came to being - from humble beginnings in the Gower Brothers' family home to reaching our 250 millionth account in 2016, and all the thrills and spills in between. We give you '15 Years of Adventure' - a History of RuneScape. Featuring interviews with some of the most famous RuneScape players of all time (including the legendary Zezima), as well as Jagex staff past and present, it's essential viewing for gaming fans the world over. We hope you enjoy.
