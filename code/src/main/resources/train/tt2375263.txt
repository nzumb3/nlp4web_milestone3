Long on the Island
###BREAK###
Long on the Island is a psychological thriller in the tradition of HP Lovecraft. With an original screenplay, Long on the Island launches a fertility goddess into a haunting place where the damage she strikes is real and lasting. The movie opens with the arrival of Oscar, a dangerous personality, to the island and his immediate befriending of Misty Muffin, a local teenager with too much time on her hands. Throw in Crazy Pete, a local madman with intimate knowledge of the forces controlling Oscar and Long on the Island reveals its mythic, eternal proportions. The secret horror is just outside your door and it will get us all!!
