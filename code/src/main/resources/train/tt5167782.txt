Weekend
###BREAK###
All Miki wanted was the chance to turn her blog into a career in the cosmetics industry. When invited to model a new brand at a secluded mountain lodge, she never imagines that her hosts - three seemingly normal men - might be setting a trap. Forced into submission for their kinky snuff website, Miki becomes her captors dog, kept alive only to perform. But Miki's kidnappers aren't the only ones with a talent for brutality, because now this young beauty wants only one thing: vengeance!
