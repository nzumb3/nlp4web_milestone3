The Winter of My Discontent
###BREAK###
Bryan Thompson's impending 30th birthday and arrival of his first child has sent him spiraling into existential angst. He meets a beautiful bohemian gas station attendant, 23 year old Andi Parker. She is the opposite of everything mundane that comprises Bryan's 'real life'. With his girlfriend just weeks away from giving birth, Bryan embarks on a discreet friendship with Andi. Unsurprisingly, Bryan's emotional fidelity is compromised and he finds himself struggling to keep a life he's indifferent towards from slipping away.
