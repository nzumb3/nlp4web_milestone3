Paul Bowles: The Cage Door is Always Open
###BREAK###
'Paul Bowles: The Cage Door is Always Open' tells the story of writer and composer Paul Bowles and his wife Jane. They married in New York in 1937, and although they both had homosexual affairs, they maintained an intense devotion to each other that led them on a spiritual journey to Tangier, Moroco--the point of no return.
