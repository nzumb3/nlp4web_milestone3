The Ultimate Rally
###BREAK###
A unique documentary style adventure film about the real truth about racing and putting everything on the line to make things happen. In search for the Ultimate Rally Adventure, a group of minority racers decide to organize the first true outlaw race in the Americas racing through the most treacherous tarmac and off road terrain. They experience numerous epic adventures and a twist develops that makes them head back to Norway. Adapted from the real life adventures of Hotrod Ponce, the Minority Racer.
