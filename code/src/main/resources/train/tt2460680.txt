Bud
###BREAK###
Bud travels from Utah to try to save his failing farm. He looks up his Uncle Jim in Los Angeles to see if he can help save his mother's farm. Uncle Jim has his own troubles, however, and is not much help. Bud meets Henry, the apartment handy girl, which changes his outlook on the world. Jim's troubles begin to catch up with him, however, and Bud and Henry are pulled into his mess.
