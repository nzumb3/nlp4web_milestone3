¡Pega Martín pega!
###BREAK###
A biographic film based on the athletic and personal life of Martin Vargas, the greatest Chilean boxing idol. The film follows him from his humble and spirited beginnings to his glorious peak, when blinded by success and money he ends up losing everything. Ten years after his retirement from boxing, in a controversial comeback, he will have the last opportunity to recover what he has lost.
