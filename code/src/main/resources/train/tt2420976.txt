The Woman at the End of the World
###BREAK###
A young British Jew travels half a continent searching for an isolated old lady rumored to be a Holocaust survivor. From Buenos Aires, Argentina to a far-flung Patagonian island, he meets colorful characters that provide clues and a sample of life in the region. Several buses, pick-up trucks and fishing boats later, this gentle documentary brings a personal discovery at the end of the world.
