The Us Festival 1982: The US Generation Documentary
###BREAK###
Referred to as "Woodstock of the 80s", the Us Festival was iconic with over 2 million attendees. Created by Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak and legendary concert promoter Bill Graham, the Us Festival assembled the best bands in the world including The Police, Tom Petty, Fleetwood Mac, Santana, Jackson Browne, The Cars, Talking Heads, Ramones, Grateful Dead, Pat Benatar & The B52s.
