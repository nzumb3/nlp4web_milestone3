He...
###BREAK###
He...' is a story of a 13 year rag picker boy Hari from the Slums of Mumbai who gets a chance to act in a short movie. Hereafter his friends call him Hero. Under the name 'Hero' he starts a journey, a journey which is all waiting and observing. Under his observation he confronts the dark side of the society but practically he finds himself helpless. The name Hero gives him a new window to look at his harsh reality .
