Blind Spot
###BREAK###
Danny, a handsome 17-year-old prep school student and swimmer head over heels in love with an older man, Darcy. When Darcy mysteriously vanishes before a planned trip, Danny searches for him with the help of two strangers who are also connected to his lover. An unexpected bond develops between these three as they trace Darcy's steps and uncover the dangerous secrets he has kept.
