Lo Indecible
###BREAK###
In November, 1974, Gabriela Goycolea de Vos, Principal at Melrose School, was kidnapped and taken to a detention center. Her account, more than a memory of her stay in a clandestine detention center, it is focused on the impossibility of reporting this kind of experience. Men of different trades and creeds in current Santiago DE Chile can read statements of women prisoners under the Chilean dictatorship, reporting the different ways of torture and sexual violence exerted specifically against women.
