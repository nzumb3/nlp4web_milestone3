G: A Dark Tale of Desires
###BREAK###
Life is fun for Virender, Sikander and Keku even if it involves occasionally molesting a cousin, considered highly incestuous in the traditional Indian society or for some fast cash, at times attempting a car jack. The nightlife for them is full of adventure, sex, booze, fisticuffs, and fun. However, their real world is entirely different where Virender's cousin, Kiran having an affair with a guy Dheer from the same village is a matter of a morbid scandal. Add to that a MMS outrage, an Honor-killing and a bloody climax- that for you is 'G- a dark tale of desires'.
