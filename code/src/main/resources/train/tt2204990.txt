Gimme Guitar
###BREAK###
Gimme Guitar is an independent music documentary about the passion of guitar music, as told through interviews of 30 guitar players. It features some great original music from all genres of guitar music. Produced, directed, shot & edited by music lover, Glenn Michael Schneider. Most of the players are from NYC, NJ, Philly and Montauk, but one interesting addition is Gunter Schneider from Austria. Guitar shredder, Marc Rizzo & Rusty Paul, Les Paul Junior are added treats. Chad Smith from the Red Hot Chili Peppers, Blondie Chaplin & Carl Harvey also make an appearance. Gimme Guitar will entertain you, leave you wanting more & might inspire you to go buy a guitar & take some lessons. Run time 2 hours.
