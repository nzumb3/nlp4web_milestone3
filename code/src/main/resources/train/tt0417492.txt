###BREAK###
Gina (Jeanette Joaquin) Lilla (Gladys Reyes) Alice (Yda Manzano) and Lorraine (Rita Avila) are four waitresses who work in a canteen along a highway. The owner of the canteen is Lucas (Daniel Fernando) a temperamental boss who treats them like prisoners. Although all the girls aspire for a better life none of them have the courage to go against Lucas.Walking alongside the highway where the canteen is located is Mildred (Barbara Milano) a prostitute. Like the four waitresses she has her own journey. Experience a different trip and take time to watch a different odyssey. Fasten your seat belt for the ride of your life.
