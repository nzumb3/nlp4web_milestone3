51 Fragments of a Wandering Mind
###BREAK###
51 Fragments of a Wandering Mind is a feature-length experimental documentary-film which is both existential self-portrait and gritty travelogue. Shot over six months, in 13 countries and completely on a toy camera, 51 Fragments presents a first person account of a man trying to find meaning in a world becoming increasingly meaningless.
