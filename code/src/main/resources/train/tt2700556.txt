Iraade Maum Ke
###BREAK###
This movie is a story of the difficulty in fulfillment of ambition. Achieving difficult dreams requires continued persistence and perseverance. The ambitious will fail if they give up too soon. The film revolves around two characters. One is Shobhana, who is ambitious. She wants to start a restaurant. The second is Parth, who wants to enjoy life. He wants to make the most of the moments that pass him by. In Parth's company, Shobhana finds happiness. They fall in love and decide to marry. This film is about their life after marriage. What happens to Shobhana's dreams? Does she achieve them?
