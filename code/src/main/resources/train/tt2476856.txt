Con quella faccia da straniera - Il viaggio di Maria Occhipinti
###BREAK###
A voyage in the past of Southern Italy and Europe, searching for Maria Occhipinti's roots and political experience. With the help of her sister Rosina, with the reconstruction of historians, and an interview of Maria Occhipinti herself, the movie will offer the insight towards understanding the risks she took and the dangerous choices she made.
