Landlord: Time to Pay the Rent
###BREAK###
Louis Fay [Rob Ho] returns from Hong Kong to honor his Father's wish of looking after the Family Business, which is the target of a Criminal Kingpin [Warwick]. Warwick also focuses on another, owned by Kay Jones [Jessica Jay]. Kay reaches breaking point and calls upon her sister Lauren [Zara Phythian] for help. Both Louis and Lauren share the common language of violence as they are forced to confront Warwick and his army for one last showdown.
