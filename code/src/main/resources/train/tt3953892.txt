Francisco de Assis: Uma Lição de Vida
###BREAK###
Francisco de Assis - A Life Lesson conveys the story of Francisco de Assis Domingos, who becomes blind at age 25, after being accidentally shot by a friend. Francisco learns how to deal with visual impairment, but unlike other people, blind or not, he turns his life around in a spectacular and unexpected way. More than an example of overcoming, Francisco de Assis - A Life Lesson is a powerful story of love and forgiveness.
