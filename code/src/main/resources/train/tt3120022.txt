Caruso's Crew
###BREAK###
Gangsters and their crew - Andy Caruso is in charge of protecting his crew's daily criminal operations. His crew, Eldon and Nigel, both are longtime friends, works for drug lord Kurt Stuttgart. Respect and trust for each other keep this crew above water in a sea of violent and hardened gangsters. A contract killing on one crew member will set of a chain reaction that turns Kurt proficient enterprise into chaos. Friendship and loyalties will be tested. Lines will be crossed, Betrayal, deceit and greed will play its part as this drama unfolds with brutal and deadly consequences.
