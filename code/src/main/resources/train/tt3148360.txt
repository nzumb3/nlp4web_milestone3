Learning How to Read to Teach My Fellows
###BREAK###
The film accompanies two Angolan musicians who travel to Bahia, Brazil, on a journey in search of vestiges of lost ancient roots. Besides participating in a major concert, they meet other musicians and investigate elements of their own identity, coming across interesting discoveries. This musical documentary addresses an African inheritance out of Africa, which is found thanks to music.
