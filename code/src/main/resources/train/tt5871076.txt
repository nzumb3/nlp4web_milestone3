A Story Behind Mountain
###BREAK###
After a public school become dismissed from a local village in the mountains, a young man volunteer to re open the school and teach children while taking care of his sick father. However he is not popular by the towns people due to his young age and the ladies affections for him. The story reflects on the hard life of the small village of China that has difficulties to come to big city to educate.
