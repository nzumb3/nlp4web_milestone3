Murder Book
###BREAK###
Murder Book is a provocative crime thriller about Detective Rich Rodriquez who is on his last leg with internal affairs, and the police force. He was once a Hot Shot detective for solving two different serial killer cases. Rich gets called to a Murder scene for what is the third young lady murdered in a similar manner. The main coincidence the victims have in common is that they where all members of a new social media site.
