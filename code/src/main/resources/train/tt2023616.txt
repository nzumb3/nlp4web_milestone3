My Little Brother
###BREAK###
Friendship story spoken by Dina. This story is about her best friend Mourad (US Marine) who perished in a tragic helicopter crash in Iraq on January 26, 2005 along with the other 30 US servicemen on board. This interview was recorded on the day of the funeral in the house where was gathering taking place (after the funeral).
