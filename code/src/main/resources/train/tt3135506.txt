Wapato Rising 877

The story of the underdog's. Told by the players themselves. Not too many believed in this group of 11 first time varsity players when the season started. Only themselves. See the amazing season unfold, set to a Hip Hop soundtrack, but still a family friendly film, Wapato Rising 877 is a great film for everybody.
