Warpaint
###BREAK###
The story of Maxxe Sternbaum, a New Yorker who enters a post-grad quarter-life-crisis after the passing of her father and impulsively moves to Oklahoma City, OK to try her hand at investing in Warpaint: a one stop boutique, print shop, gallery, and vintage business run by Derek Knowlton and Travis Himself, its fun loving, free-spirited creators.
