Expats
###BREAK###
For the last ten years a group of EXPATS in Iquitos, Peru have been squabbling over which one of them invented the (poorly run) Amazon River Raft Race, which requires an international field of contestants to build their own raft out of logs and rope and then, over the course of three days, paddle 116 miles down the perilous Amazon River - I decided to investigate, and this film is the result.
