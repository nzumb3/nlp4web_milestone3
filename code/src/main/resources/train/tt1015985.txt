Futbol fanatico
###BREAK###
The passion of football supporters all over the world has different pronouncements. The most fanatic supporters can be meet in South America. Especially Argentina is presenting an visual event in colorful flags, banderols, paper streamers an confetti, completed by singing as tribute to the club. The action on the field will be pushed in the background not only for a short time. The priority of the supporters' life will be given to the football club. Accordingly the emotions on match days are erupting. This fanatic passion was known in Europe from hearsay only until now. (including Superclassico Boca Juniors - River Plate)
