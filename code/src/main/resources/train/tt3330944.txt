The Takeover
###BREAK###
Boy meets girl. Rather, Jadin meets Ashley, the ultimate con artist. This good, clean, Christian story uses the best comedic gaffs, including nosy neighbors, hearing voices in your head, and the worst nightmare of having one's house and life taken over by con artists. It is rich in humor and self-inflicted misery that ends happily ever after.
