Comic 8: Casino Kings - Part 1
###BREAK###
Eight secret agents under the leadership of Indro Warkop assigned to undercover as stand up comedians in search of a comedian who becomes a liaison to the master criminal named The King, the most spectacular gambling casino owner in Asia. To search for the casino and catch The King, the eight comedians must go through many processes and obstacles: avoiding pursuit by Interpol and the police for bank robbery in previous mission, and has to face the death squads, a group of thugs and criminals known with ax mark.
