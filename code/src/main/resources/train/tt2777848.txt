Kampen mot vekten
###BREAK###
There will be soon 1 million overweight people in Norway, how many will there be in 2020? The documentary film is based on interviews of persons suffering from being overweight, and about the people who have taken a grip on their situation. How does it feel to be bullied because you look different? Why choose an operation instead of a work out? What happens to us when we become overweight? What can we do differently to get the number of overweight to reduce in the future.
