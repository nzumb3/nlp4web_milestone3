The Jinn
###BREAK###
Actress, Juliet Howard has been taken hostage by a gang of rebels lead by outcast, Puck Victorious. Holed up in an abandoned ranch their plans are thwarted when the caretaker of the land, Ahmad, returns home at dusk. Soon Ahmad fills their heads with tales of the jinn that inhabit the land, slaughtering those that wander after dark.
