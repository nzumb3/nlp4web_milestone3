Marked for Life
###BREAK###
An English teacher, James, leaves Rome for the peace of the Arabian desert only to find himself working at a language school run by a tyrant. The film traces the fortunes of our hapless hero as he encounters an oddball collection of expatriates in the desert town and tries to cope with the heat, teaching with a hangover and the attentions of the boss's wife.
