Art Connect: Liverpool UK
###BREAK###
The artist is developing an in studio body work that takes us into his never ending world of fantasy and the dark silence of the human condition. Ghostly animations, moving sketchbooks and charcoal drawings fills the studio spaces at the Bluecoat as a religious exercise to his finds from working within communities. Three days out of the week you can find the artist working within the neighborhoods on a focused series of dynamic art workshops; Animation, Carnival Mask Making, Drawing to name a few.
