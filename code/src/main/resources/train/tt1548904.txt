Barka! Burkina
###BREAK###
BARKA! BURKINA is a love letter to the people of Burkina Faso, West Africa. It traces the journey of Ina & Esu, co-founders of The BARKA Foundation, two Americans who are joining hands with a small village in Burkina to bring balance to a global village that has lost its way. This is a true story of peace, water & wisdom, enacted with vision, passion and purpose. "Barka" is an African word of gratitude and blessing.
