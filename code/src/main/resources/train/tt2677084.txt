Cars, Trains, Buses and Planes: A Cross-Societal Look at Transportation
###BREAK###
Shawn Verma and Natasha Kumar, two undergraduate students at Brown University, filmed this documentary as part of an independent study course, which was commissioned by Brown University, during their Spring 2012 semester abroad in Washington D.C., USA; Sao Paulo, Brazil; Hanoi, Vietnam; and Bushbuckridge, South Africa.
