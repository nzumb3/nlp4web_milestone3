Don't Get Me Started
###BREAK###
As part of his efforts to turn over a new leaf by relocating to suburbia, former hitman Jack Lane gave up smoking a year ago minus a day. When Jerry Hoff comes to town, threatening to expose Jack's secret past, Jack's chain-smoking friend Larry must bear the brunt of Jack's paranoia. Hoff, meanwhile, is intent on selling Jack's past to primetime television, which could ruin everything. Hoff is going to have to pay the price.
