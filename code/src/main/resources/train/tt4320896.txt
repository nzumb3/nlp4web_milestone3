The Aeolians: From Russia with Love
###BREAK###
The Aeolians of Oakwood University was originally organized in 1946 by the late Dr Eva B. Dykes. SInce then, the Choir has traveled widely, touching the hearts of many with their inspirational singing. This DVD is the first documentary in the history of the world renowned Aeolicans featuring their historic visit to Moscow Russia. In this 2 1/2 hour documentary you will experience some of the sites, sounds and sensations of the entire trip, from concept to reality, sit back and ENJOY!.
