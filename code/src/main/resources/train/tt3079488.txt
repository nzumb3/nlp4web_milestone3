Logos: Change the Inevitable
###BREAK###
Jody, a marketing "spin doctor" for a multinational energy conglomerate, meets David, a homeless man with a message of peace. When she discovers her company is behind world unrest, and plans to start a war for its own profit, she turns to David for help. Her company, in turn, kidnaps David, and sends him out with their head of security to be "disposed of" and is shot, but escapes with the help of a sympathetic staff member. Before he bleeds to death, he is able to deliver his message to the world at the United Nations General Assembly.
