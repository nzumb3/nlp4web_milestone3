The Swallow Tailed Paper
###BREAK###
Calin Cassian (alias Papo) is a gypsy migrated in Italy since he was a child, now a man 65 years old, living in a countryside of Milan now marginalized, and, since the beginning survives stealing wallets at the crossroads and begging. He has adopted from a niece, two orphan kids, Rube e Natha, teaching them the art of the little burglaries in the street. Trying desperately to survive, the two kids run away from their tutor Papo looking for a life free from obligations and constraints, but while Calinn is looking anxiously for them, the two kids strive among requests of begs and bad ended burglaries till repenting and looking for Papo again.
