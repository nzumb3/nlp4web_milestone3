Antaraal
###BREAK###
Pre-marital pregnancy, a big town society dealing with it - clandestinely, the follies and foibles of a few individuals who get entwined with the lives of two women who come together for the unborn child conceived in desire but not desired. Antaraal is an intense film on human relationships. It is based on a novel by the same title written by Krishnendu Mukhopadhyay.
