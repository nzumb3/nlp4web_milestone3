Resistance/The 19th Day
###BREAK###
The honorable battle of people who gathered inside a park at the center of Istanbul. The Battle of 'Gezi Park' which was like a democracy carnival during the daylight and brought together people from all over the world, turned to the hell with the unproportional attack by the police after the evening salat and lasted in the 19th day. The result: 5 dead, hundreds of woundeds, loss of money of millions of dollars.
