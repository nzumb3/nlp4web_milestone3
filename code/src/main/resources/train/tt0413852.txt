Black-Eyed Susan
###BREAK###
Two friends plot to rob their neighbor, who has died in his apartment unbeknownst to the rest of the world. They put together a team, rob the old man's place, and get out with no one the wiser. But when the old man's strange, foreign relatives come to collect the body, things quickly go from bad, to worse, to deadly.
