The Interval
###BREAK###
"The Interval" explores four different families coming to grips with the terminal illness of a loved one and the emotional struggle of finding hope within their tragedy. All four patients reside at a hospice and while their location is the same, their stories take different paths. Although it all begins with life and ends in death, the families with the help of Haven Valley Hospice must move forward and deal with the in-between, "The Interval".
