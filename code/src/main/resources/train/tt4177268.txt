Life After Life
###BREAK###
Life After Life is a coming of age story set in the aftermath of the cradle to prison pipeline. We follow three men on their journey home, capturing their first moments on the outside after decades in prison. Each had their path to manhood interrupted by childhood trauma and violence before committing terrible acts in their adolescence. Now with the odds stacked against them they must overcome their demons, while facing the daily challenges of survival as free men.
