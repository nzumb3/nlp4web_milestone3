Yorktown
###BREAK###
"Yorktown" is a 1970's inspired thriller film about a group of eight friends that upon hearing of the haunted Yorktown Hospital the group decides to take a road trip, despite the heeded warnings from the bartender, played by actress Rebekah Kochan. What they don't know is that someone has already taken up residence in the building. When you don't pay attention to your friends, bad things happen.
