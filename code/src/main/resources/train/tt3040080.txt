Looking for Change
###BREAK###
Looking for Change, directed by Deborah Jenkins, is the second in a series of ACALATV-produced videos on housing issues affecting the people of Antigonish. This project, made possible through funding made available through the Catherine Donnelly Foundation, was a collaboration between the Antigonish County Adult Learning Association (ACALA), the Antigonish Women's Resource Centre and Sexual Assault Services Association, the Antigonish Poverty Reduction Coalition, and the Antigonish Affordable Housing Action Group.
