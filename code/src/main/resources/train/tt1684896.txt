Julian Fauth: The Blues Is Just a Feeling
###BREAK###
'Julian Fauth: The Blues Is Just A Feeling' is a stark black and white profile of Julian Fauth, Canadian Blues musician and Canadian Juno Award Winner for Best Blues Album, 2009 for his album 'Ramblin' Son'. The Film shows exciting concert footage of Julian Fauth at the Winterfolk Festival in Toronto and at the Memphis International Blues Challenge in Memphis. The film also includes some raw interview segments with Julian talking intimately about his early years, his musical influences such as Memphis Slim, and his great mentor, Mel Brown, who settled in his later years in Julian's home town of Kitchener, Ontario.
