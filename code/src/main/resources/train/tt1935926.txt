Tierra
###BREAK###
Indians at risk of extinction because many are left to starve or commit mass suicide rather than abandon their land or their traditions, others are killed by unscrupulous settlers seeking gold in the forest. Those who survive, they see their culture overwhelmed from the intrusiveness of the whites that push young people to embrace indigenous customs and traditions very different from tradition. The Brazilian constitution has finally recognized their right to maintain their social organization, traditions and original rights of the lands they occupy.
