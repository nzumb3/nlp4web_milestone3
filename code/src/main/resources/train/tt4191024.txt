Hells Playground
###BREAK###
Hell's Playground is the chilling tale of the murderous master & mistress of the Rutford mansion. In this grindhouse feature, the sadistic son & devilish daughter prey upon anyone unlucky enough to set foot on their domain. With the aid of their ravenous butler, the Rutford siblings hunt down several road-tripping college kids. This incident prompts a film team to uncover the truth behind the Rutford siblings. A dark story of fear, brutality and twisted love, Hell's Playground will leave you afraid to play games at the Rutford mansion.
