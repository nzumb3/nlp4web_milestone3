Trophy Wife
###BREAK###
Chino and Lani shared a relationship some time ago. Their relationship ended with Chino leaving Lani feeling betrayed and abandoned. Now, Chino finds that Lani is married to his brother Sammy. In the time since Chino left her, Lani's been carefully plotting her revenge. She soon draws Chino, his brother, and his girlfriend Gwen into a complex game of revenge.
