The Long Road Home
###BREAK###
One year after returning home to Northern Ontario for his parents funeral, Robert Chenaux finds himself face-to-face with a career defining promotion; find the money, close the deal, and make partner. After a major investor backs out last minute, Robert scrambles to find the financing and can see only one option, sell the last piece of family property. But, his plans get complicated when his estranged brother arrives in town.
