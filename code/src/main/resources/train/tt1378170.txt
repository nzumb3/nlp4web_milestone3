10th Gear Jettride: 3800 Miles to a Cure
###BREAK###
Jordan McSherry joined forces with the 10th Gear Youth Cyclists in taking the journey of a lifetime by riding their bicycles 3800 miles across America in the attempt to raise money in helping to save her brother Jett's life and to ride for the boys who can no longer ride their bicycles. Jett McSherry is a 12 year old boy diagnosed with this fatal disease Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy and this documentary will help shed light about the long term effects of the disease while taking these courageous teenagers from the beginning of the journey in Seaside, Or.. 3800 miles across the country and back to the birth place of America, Plymouth, MA.
