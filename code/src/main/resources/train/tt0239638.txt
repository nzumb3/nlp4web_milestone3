Millennium's End: The Fandom Menace
###BREAK###
Millennium's End: The Fandom Menace is a documentary chronicling the anticipation for Star Wars, Episode I: The Phantom Menace during the two years prior to the movie's release in May 1999. The film includes interviews with such individuals as Harry Knowles of Ain't It Cool News and the creative forces behind such websites as TheForce.net, JediNet.com and StarwarZ.com, as well as the team behind Blue Harvest magazine. Jeremy Bulloch, who played Boba Fett in The Empire Strikes Back and Return of the Jedi also appears in the film. It captures the most exciting time to be a Star Wars fan, the end of the Millennium.
