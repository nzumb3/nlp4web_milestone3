Searchdog
###BREAK###
RELIANCE a feature length documentary about Matthew Zarrella, a Rhode Island State Police Sergeant who rehabilitates 'pound dogs' and turns them into Search & Rescue K9's. We come to know Zarrella's extraordinary dogs and witness the transformation of his Search & Rescue students from across the country as he molds them into certified Search & Rescue K9 Teams.
