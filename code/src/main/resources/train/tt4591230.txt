A Moment of Love
###BREAK###
Ji Ya Qing (Cecilia Liu) was on a business trip to Yunan right before her wedding. On her stay at the hotel, she met a young man (Vic Chow) with the same intent in searching of an old lady, having in his possession were only letters his grandfather wrote before passing away. They went together on the journey in the country scenery of China, in the quest of the lady in the letters. This journey yet holds more for both of them, as they discover what love could mean and bring.
