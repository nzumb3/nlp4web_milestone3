The White Treasure and the Salt Workers from Caquena
###BREAK###
Just beside an endless white desert up in the Bolivian Andes, Daniel Lopez and his family lead traditional salt workers lifes. Since it has become known that Salar de Uyuni holds the worlds biggest amount of lithium - THE futures resource - their little village finds itself in a place where global interest reaches in.
