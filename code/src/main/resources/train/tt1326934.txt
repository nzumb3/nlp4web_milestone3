Cherry Mapping
###BREAK###
After running away from home, 17 year old Lea Bautista, thinks she can live the life of an Internet model like her Internet crush Gina- 'Pretty Toxin.' However, after arriving in Los Angeles, everything Lea has planned is taken away from her and her dreams become a diary that the FBI try to understand through the detective work of four young teenage bloggers.
