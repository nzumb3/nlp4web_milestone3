LA Sucks
###BREAK###
LA Sucks is a half-hour improv comedy about three best-friend vampires who are the opposite of the enigmatic, sexy, stealth blood-suckers depicted in all the media. When the three friends are not stalking pedophiles from sex offender websites, they spend their nights making video time capsules in an attempt to salvage memories throughout their terribly long existence.
