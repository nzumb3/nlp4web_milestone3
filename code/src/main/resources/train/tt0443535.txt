A Home for the Bullets
###BREAK###
Axel Falcon is the one man force carving his way through the crime ridden underbelly of Newcastle. With his family murdered he becomes a man with nothing to lose with only one thing on his mind, revenge. Following a series of clues left for him by an unknown assassin Falcon must fight his way to the top of the criminal empire headed by Davro, the man responsible for his family's death.
