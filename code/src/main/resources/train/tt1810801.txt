The Media: Journalism in Crisis
###BREAK###
Neal Cortell brings over thirty-five years as a television journalist, author, and enrichment lecturer, presents his latest documentary, THE MEDIA: JOURNALISM IN CRISIS. The second installment in THE MEDIA CRISIS documentary series, narrated by former CBS and ABC news anchor and correspondent, Mort Dean, features renowned journalists of the past and present, interwoven with analysts, academics, regulators and activist that ponder the current state and future of news and the internet.
