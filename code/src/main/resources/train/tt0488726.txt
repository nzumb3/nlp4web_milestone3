Bravo, My Life!
###BREAK###
(Korean with English subtitles) This subtle comedy about the quiet up-and-downs of single parent family life, tells the story of 14-year-old Gwang-ho, whose typical day involve football and brawling at his junior high, and Gwang-ho's mom, Mal-sun, a heavy-handed cosmetician who devotes everything to her teenage son.
