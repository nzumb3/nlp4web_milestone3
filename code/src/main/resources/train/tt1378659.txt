Don't Blink
###BREAK###
Follow MMA sensation Joe Heiland as he carries on his quest for domination in the exciting world of mixed martial arts. Our story begins as "Smokin" Joe wins the National Amateur Championship title in the NAAFS Welterweight (156-170 lbs.) division. Join us as we follow his everyday life, his training, and the pressure that comes with being an undefeated fighter taking his record to the next level as an aspiring professional. Don't Blink as we take you behind the scenes to see where Joe came from, and what drives him to be the best. Meet his coaches, see his training and experience the action firsthand in this one-of-a-kind documentary.
