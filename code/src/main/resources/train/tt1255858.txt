Anya (In and Out of Focus)
###BREAK###
Polish-born, American-based filmmaker Marian Marzynski's full length documentary of his daughter's first 30 years (beginning at age 5 when the family moved to the United States). Marzynski, who is perhaps best known for his work about the Holocaust, here chronicles his daughter's coming of age in America as the child of immigrant parents.
