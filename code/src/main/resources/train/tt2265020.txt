'The Journey to Myself
###BREAK###
'The Journey to Myself' is a documentary on the life of Stacey Blanchet. Fashion Designer, Author and Business Woman. She takes us through the four decades of her life with divorce, molestation, waking up and discovering who she is and where she wants to go. The documentary was shot in 'Master Class' style with the subject talking for the entire time and photos and video taking you through her life lessons.
