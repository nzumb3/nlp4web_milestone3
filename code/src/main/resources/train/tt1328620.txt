Gangotri
###BREAK###
Thakur Jaswant Singh takes over the estate of benevolent but now convicted killer, Thakur Vijay Singh, and begins a regime of oppression along with his son, Balwant. He issues instructions that no male in the region is to even look at his daughter, Gangotri, and plans to arrange her wedding with a family of equal status. He and his family's rage will know no bounds when they find out that a lowly servant, Bajrangi, has secretly married Gangotri.
