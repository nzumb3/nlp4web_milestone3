Some Kind of Drummer, the Steve Forman Story. The artist who redefined our world's understanding of percussion and opened up a universe of possibility for the future of music. Follow one man's journey of more than 50 years and encompass a body of work which includes many classic treasures of our generation's most important acts. You'll be amazed at the ever evolving and expanding realm of Forman's musical reach as it spans the musical spectrum from writing and performing to directing, composing and now, teaching a percussive experience intent on keeping the whole universe dancing!
