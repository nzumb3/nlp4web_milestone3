Meok Bang
###BREAK###
I suddenly got addicted to Meok Bang, a short form for Korean Term 'meok-neun-bang-song.' It is self-broadcasting where a person gorges oneself on large portions of food in front of camera. I just got curious why I'm an addict of it. Thus, I started off my journey to figure out what makes me keep watching it by having conversations with people around me.
