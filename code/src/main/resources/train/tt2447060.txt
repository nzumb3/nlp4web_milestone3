L'orchestra di Piazza Vittorio: I Diari del Ritorno
###BREAK###
After travelling the world with the documentary film on the multi-ethnic orchestra created in the Esquilino district of Rome (L'orchestra di Piazza Vittorio), the adventure's protagonists, Agostino Ferrente and Mario Tronco, have started to make video portraits of each individual musician in the orchestra, filmed in their country of origin. The first two profiles were made in Argentina by Alessandro Rossetto and in Tunisia by Leonardo di Costanzo.
