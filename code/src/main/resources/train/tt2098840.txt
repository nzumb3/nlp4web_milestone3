The Men of Piper Alpha
###BREAK###
'The Men Of Piper Alpha' pays tribute to the victims and survivors of one of the world's worst ever offshore oil and gas disasters where 167 men tragically lost their lives onboard the Piper Alpha platform on July 6th 1988. This film relives the catastrophic chain of events that unfolded that night before, during and after the disaster, focusing on how some of these brave men were able to survive against all odds. With compelling interviews from survivors, rescue workers, families and leading industry figures 'The Men Of Piper Alpha' ensures that the world will never be allowed to forget the horrific events of that ill-fated night.
