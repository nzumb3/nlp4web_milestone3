Road to Chernobyl
###BREAK###
Hip-Hop recording artist Crucifix and three friends venture across the globe and deep into the Radioactive Dead Zone of Chernobyl to film his latest music video. But nothing can prepare them for what awaits in the cold grip of Eastern Europe. With the Russian Mob at their heels, the threat of a lost loved one back home, and the irreversible effects of radiation growing ever more real, the group faces one question... "Is it too late to turn back?" Hearts start racing, Geiger Counters go off and a careless mistake could reap a lifetime of consequences. Follow one man's dream in this riveting, reality-style documentary that reveals the cold hard truth of Chernobyl.
