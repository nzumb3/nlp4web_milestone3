Falling Leaves
###BREAK###
Amir waits alone in Tehran for his son's return from California to help him run the traditional family business. Arash does not share his father's sense of duty and has chosen to follow his heart. Amir has no understanding or sympathy for his son's choice until he reconnects with the woman he would have married had his own father not intervened. Caught between traditional values and personal integrity, Amir chooses to rethink and come to terms with the choices he has made in his life.
