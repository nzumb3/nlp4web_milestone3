Paragon II
###BREAK###
Paragon II (sequel to 2010's multiple-award nominee PARAGON) continues the adventures of Augustus 'Paragon' Carr and his sidekick, Corliss Erman, as they search for the son of a missing millionaire. Along the way, they will learn about Augustus's murky past, as well as discovering a betrayal they never could have imagined.
