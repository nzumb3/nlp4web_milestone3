Joy Incorporated
###BREAK###
Alex, Bert and Ziya have formed a 'philosophy school' that teaches ancient Indian techniques for modern living. In order to save the school from closing down, they decide to put on a 'show' in the space, with a power point presentation, a clown, dominatrix, dancers, and other attractions, for their friends and guests. During the show, old affairs and new romantic entanglements come to the surface, and threaten to jeopardize their friendship and the whole venture.
