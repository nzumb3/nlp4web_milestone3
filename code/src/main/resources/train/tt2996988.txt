The Art of Disappearing
###BREAK###
An unknown story of a Haitian vodou priest, Amon Fremon, who visited the People's Republic of Poland in 1980. During his stay, Amon contacts Polish spirits and conducts a vodou ceremony to free Poles from evil. A metaphysical view on time of socialism through the eyes of a stranger from a different culture.
