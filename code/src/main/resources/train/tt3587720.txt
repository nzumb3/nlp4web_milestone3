Looping Earth
###BREAK###
In seven chapters this direct cinema documentary tells about the Looping Earth Tour of Georg Viktor Emmanuel. A former Austrian call-center manager with the vision to fulfill his childhood dream of becoming a famous musician. With no experience G.V.E starts his new career as a live looping street artist. He travels with his compact car through 13 European countries in seven months to make his only dream come true.
