Living Off the Manhole (and the Poetry of Real Estate)
###BREAK###
'I think if Andy Warhol were alive today, and he met Lisa, he would make her famous. But she's even better than something he would create-sort of Andy Warhol plus John Waters plus something all her own, something extremely goofy'--All set against a backdrop of a hotel empire, in the last hotel standing. What is it about living in her grandfather's hotel, across from the Waldorf Astoria in New York City, that makes it so special? Find out in this quirky documentary about a piece of New York's history.
