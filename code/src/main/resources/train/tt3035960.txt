Nack Solo
###BREAK###
Nack Solo (Nahor Solomon) is an intellect agent who works for an unofficial underground agency that is built to preserve peace and maintain balance with in the system. But his superior called J Burden has corrupted the system and it is now up to Nack Solo to restore the balance, and as he goes through his own spiritual warfare Nack Solo ultimately becomes incorruptible himself.
