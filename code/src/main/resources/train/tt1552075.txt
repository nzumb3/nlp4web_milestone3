Journeyman
###BREAK###
Journeyman follows two at-risk teenage boys from diverse backgrounds who struggle with depression and violence. Joe's father is in jail, and Mike's father committed suicide. Both boys seem determined to follow in their father's footsteps. However, the boys join a mentoring program before it's too late, where they face challenging rites of passage alongside supportive mentors, which leads them to discover their inner strength, and demonstrates the value of a community of supportive men.
