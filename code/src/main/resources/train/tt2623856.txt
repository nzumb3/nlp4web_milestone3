Blissfully Unmarried
###BREAK###
John Haw is going through a rough spell. In a bitter divorce, he has lost his wife, kids, and most of his worldly possessions. At the point where he believes that he is as low as he can go, he is robbed. While he attempts to slowly piece his miserable life back together, his luck take a dramatic turn for the best. John wins one million dollars and meets the girl of his dreams. Now that he is on cloud nine, there is only one thing that can bring him down, his ex wife. Once she finds out about his new found wealth, she goes into overdrive to get the upper hand.
