My Father's Bike
###BREAK###
The morning: seventy-year-old Vladek finds a letter, in which his wife informs him that she is leaving him. Because of this horrible news Vladek faints and ends up in hospital. The next day in hospital, Vladek is visited by his son and teenage grandson. Pavel, the son is a world famous celebrity pianist, he came from Berlin where he lives. Mack, the grandson is a student, he came from London where he lives with his mother. It turns out that both guys have not kept in touch for years. Here they are faced with shocking news that their mother/grandma fell in love and left grandpa to be with another man. The next morning the whole trio goes for the trip...
