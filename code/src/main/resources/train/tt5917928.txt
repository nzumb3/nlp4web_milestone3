Les petits pas
###BREAK###
The city of Francs-Moisins at Saint-Denis (France), during a literacy course and sewing. Meeting with Mariama, Mariam, Sougandi, Zaïna, Diarrah, and ... Françoise. They want learn writing and reading. They want to become independent, to work, to exist finally, and help their children to succeed. Small step forward imperceptible to our eyes, but huge for her. At the end of the trainee ship what then have they acquired ? They will be more confident, better equipped ?
