Daulat Ki Jung
###BREAK###
Business-rivals Bhushan Chaudhry and Agarwal's children, Rajesh and Asha are collegians who are in love with each other. When their parents find out about this romance Rajesh and Asha are restricted not to have any doings with each other prompting them to elope. Their scenario changes when they are made targets by various gangsters which not only endangers their lives but changes it forever.
