Rights & Wrongs: The Story of Women in Islam
###BREAK###
The documentary explores Muslim women's present conditions and connects this with their past, returning to Islam's beginnings to uncover women's roles in the first Muslim communities. The exploration shows how various Islamic societies have found justification for their treatment of women but uses historians and scholars to debunk the many myths and misunderstandings about women's roles in Islam.
