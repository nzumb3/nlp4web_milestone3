Hi8
###BREAK###
Isaac D. Quesada is a 15 year old boy who just got his first camcorder, a Hi8 and he records every moment of his life. The millennium has just began, a group of teenagers record on a Hi8 camera how they live this years, from the year 2000 to 2004 we see them dealing with sex, drugs, terrorist attacks and other things they encounter while they reach maturity.
