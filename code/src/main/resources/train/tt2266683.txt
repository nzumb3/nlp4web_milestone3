Brendan Benson Concert Los Angeles
###BREAK###
In the Winter of 2010, singer/songwriter Brendan Benson (Raconteurs) toured the United States backed by musicians Brad Pemberton (The Pinkhearts, Ryan Adams), and Jonathan Auer and Ken Stringfellow of The Posies/Big Star. This film documents Benson's Los Angeles show on one of the final days of the tour.
