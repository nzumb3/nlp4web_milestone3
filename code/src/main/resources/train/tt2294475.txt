Alegalu
###BREAK###
Basu's Windowed mother's plan ti marry a distant relative brings unrest in Basu's life. When Basu Fails in a class test, he forges her signature on the marks card only to be caught by her teacher! Putta unusual hobby is to curve models out rubber pieces for which he steals money from home but caught by mother and reprimanded severely. Basu's grandma's story comes as a solution The story says that whoever goes to the distant island in the sea, leaves something behind for Panjurli (a spirit!) on the island and prays, his wishes come true! Putta and Basu decide to venture into the sea in their small wooden dinghy!
