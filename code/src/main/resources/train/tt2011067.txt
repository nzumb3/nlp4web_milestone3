Industria Argentina
###BREAK###
The economic crisis caused the bankruptcy of many companies. In a metallurgical factory, workers are resist to lose their only way to survive. John is one of those employees who for months did t get paid. His pregnant wife and debts make he see a very dark future. Gradually and taking the control of their desperation, John and his colleagues are beginning to organize as a cooperative to continue operating the company abandoned by their owners. So take the hard road of building a business without business.
