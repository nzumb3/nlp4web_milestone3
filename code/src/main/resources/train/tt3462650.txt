Mill City Misfits
###BREAK###
Single mother Jezebel Johnson struggles to get her television show on the air and staying one step ahead of the mafia. Her son's father Chad and ex-con brother Dave only make matters worse. Will Jezebel and Chad be able to pay off the loan shark before he puts a hit out on them? Find out in this hilarious comedic drama Mill City Misfits.
