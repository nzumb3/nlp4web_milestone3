Delete
###BREAK###
Jurre (15) misses his father Tom. Since Tom has been diagnosed with Alzheimer's. Because of this condition his dad slowly starts to forget things. Almost as if pieces of his memory are being deleted. That's why father and son stopped doing fun things together. Deep inside the only thing Jurre wants is to spend more time with his father. Just like the good old days.
