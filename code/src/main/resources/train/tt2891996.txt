The Cuban underground hip hop movement opens a second door to the change of the socio-political situation around them. Calle Real 70 2a Puerta is a documentary of that movement with a soundtrack which includes the latest musical collection of clans and secret studies of Havana. The rhymes of Los Aldeanos, Papa Humbertico, Randy Acosta, Escuadron Patriota, Silvito El Libre, Danay Suarez, etc., lead this story that began three years ago in a village on the outskirts of Havana and came to an end in 2011.
