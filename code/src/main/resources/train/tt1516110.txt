Normal Types
###BREAK###
Normal Types is a heartfelt story about a father looking for redemption and a family that is just figuring out how to be "normal." Two half-sisters with Asperger's Syndrome struggle with sisterhood, dating, loss, and finding their place in society. One sister is determined to face the world as she is, while the other sister is so obsessed with fitting in that she doesn't see her own brilliance. Their father tries to connect with and provide for his daughters. He realizes the only way their dreams will come to fruition is if he makes major sacrifices.
