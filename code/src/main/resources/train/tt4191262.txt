Futureman
###BREAK###
'Futureman' follows 5-time Grammy winner, inventor and virtuoso Roy 'Futureman' Wooten (of Bela Fleck and the Flecktones) as he discusses and works with his various musical inventions, including a piano based on the periodic table and the newest piano he used to compose the soundtrack he has composed for his upcoming feature film about the Black Mozart titled 'Chevalier'. The film continues as we see him conducting members of the Nashville symphony as he works through the musical score on his way to record with the Simon Bolivar Orchestra in Venezuela.
