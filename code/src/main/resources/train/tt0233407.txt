Canvas: The Skateboarding Documentary
###BREAK###
Australia, 1998: A professional film crew go on tour with eight of the most diverse skaters in the sport on the Hardcore 900 Degree Tour. The result is 'Canvas', the first movie of its kind in skateboarding - a true documentary featuring skating, interviews and behind-the-scene, real life footage of some of the most talented and fascinating skaters in history.
