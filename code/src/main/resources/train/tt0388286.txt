Mullavalliyum Thenmavum
###BREAK###
Shelly and Rajasree are students at fine arts college and they fall in love during college days. Their parents oppose for their marriage and they elope and end up with Dr. Alex. Rajasree asks Shelly to wait for the marriage, as she wants to do it with their parents permission. Dr. Alex gets an assignment for Shelly, to make a house boat for the tourism department. But he cannot complete the making of the boat and another architect, Andre enters into their life.
