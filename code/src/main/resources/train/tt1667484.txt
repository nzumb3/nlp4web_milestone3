Smoke and Mirrors
###BREAK###
The untimely death of Garrett Owens (Chad Gravallese) sparks controversy throughout the iconic streets of Washington D.C. To the officials it's a case, to Connor West (Brian Jackson) it's personal. In this taut suspense thriller Connor West unveils the mystery behind Garrett's murder but to his surprise discovers secrets about his own life and identity.
