The Weight of Life
###BREAK###
A national weightlifter coach A-Fu founded a teenage weightlifting team with three aboriginal girls from Pasikau Tribe in Kaohsiung. These girls were either raised in an orphaned or single-parent home, or by their grandparents. Burdened with familial responsibility and personal goals, their challenge is not only to lift the heavy weight plates, but defy the odds in their arduous lives.
