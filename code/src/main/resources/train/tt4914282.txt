He Sends Rain
###BREAK###
He Sends Rain is about a young man whose perspective is challenged when circumstances he is faced with lead him to confront old wounds. David Conrad, a man who trusts in his intellect, and his justified anger about a family tragedy, now with deep rooted bitterness visits his father's house along with his new wife, Julia, who notices changes in David's behavior, and attempts to uncover the mystery of his past.
