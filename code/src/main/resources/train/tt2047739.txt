Good Luck on the Road
###BREAK###
GOOD LUCK ON THE ROAD is a road trip on the footsteps of the famous photographer Robert Frank. It's a journey from Paris to Mabou, Nova Scotia, through New York. New-York / Mabou where the artist is leaving and working. The film is the story about these two worlds. A trip in which will appear the face of a man and the legacy of his work.
