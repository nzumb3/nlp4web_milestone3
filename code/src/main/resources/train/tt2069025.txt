Frost Winterhawk
###BREAK###
Set in a world of chaos, we follow the relationship between a young woman, Gracie, and her social worker, Linda. They bond as Gracie is transferred to a facility for troubled youth, on the verge of her 21st birthday. What no one knows is that Gracie has a mysterious past and she is on a mission that will change the course of history.
