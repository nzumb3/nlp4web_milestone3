Courtney: The Story of Molly Flash
###BREAK###
In late 2015, a camera crew interviews performance artist Courtney Woodberry, better known as Molly Flash of Greenville, SC based troupe: The Secret Cirkus. Two weeks after the interview, Courtney passed away from Hodgkin's Lymphoma. Through a series of candid interviews, Courtney's friends, family, and fellow performers share their stories of her life and legacy.
