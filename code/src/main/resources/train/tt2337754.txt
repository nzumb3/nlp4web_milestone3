The Snow Collective
###BREAK###
The Snow Collective is a documentary on the legendary World Heli Challenge ski and snowboard event held annually in Wanaka New Zealand, the athletes at its core and their free riding journey. Filmed in Wanaka, New Zealand, Chamonix France, Jackson Hole Wyoming, Japan and Points North Heli Cordova Alaska. This is a 100% Big Mountain Freeride film
