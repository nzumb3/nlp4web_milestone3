Tramvayat - sto godini ot zhivota na Sofia
###BREAK###
On January 15, 2001, the 100 anniversary of the first ride of a streetcar in Sofia was celebrated. An old Tram tells its story and the life of the capital city during these 100 years. Events, streets, people and fates pass before the eyes of the Tram. It came from Europe bringing European breath into the city. It survives during the wars, bombs, revolutions, socialist reconstruction, trod along the road of democracy and keep traveling towards the world.
