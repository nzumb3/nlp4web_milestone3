One Line of Reality
###BREAK###
The career of a journalist couple, Kasra and Forough is endangered in the aftermath of political and social changes, which have led to the closure of many Iranians journals. They publish a cultural-literary weekly magazine titled Farhang-e Fakhteh, however they are facing several problems which discourage them to carry on the job. Shortly after wards, such difficulties put them on the verge of bankruptcy, but a proposal made by a foreign institute transforms their life.
