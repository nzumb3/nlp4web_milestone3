From Dust
###BREAK###
From Dust is a documentary about one man's transformation and renewal after attending his first Burning Man. Visually hypnotizing, From Dust tracks the journey from the road to Black Rock City to a magical place where anything can happen. By removing himself from the default world, Jeremiasz comes to believe he can transcend his failures and shortcomings and start a new life.
