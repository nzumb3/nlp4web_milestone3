Hornsleth: Deep Storage Project
###BREAK###
The provocative Danish artist Kristian von Hornsleth had an idea a few years ago, which proved to be easier said than done. Blood samples from five thousand people are dumped in a metal container eleven kilometres deep in the ground, where they remain untouched for thousands of years as a kind of DNA museum of mankind. However, the monumental 'Deep Storage Project' encounter all sorts of hardships along the way.
