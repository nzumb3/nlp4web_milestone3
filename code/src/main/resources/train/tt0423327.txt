Taxi ni Pilo
###BREAK###
A taxi driver named Pilo is tasked to bring dead people to a movie theater that leads to the next life. One of his passengers reveals that he is dead as well. With this knowledge, Pilo's desire to see his love one, Galina, intensifies. He is accompanied by cousins James and Anna who also recently died. In the process, the cousins realize that their friend Teo is in danger and with the help of Pilo they rescue him. In the end a family reunites by fate and couple relives their love.
###BREAK###
