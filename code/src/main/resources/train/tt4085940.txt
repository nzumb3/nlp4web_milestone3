Way Up North: An Arctic Symphony
###BREAK###
Over the period of one year, 100 kids from six remote northern communities all across the Northwest Territories join hands with world-class musicians and local singer-songwriters in one of the largest arts/youth outreach projects in the Northwest Territories, Canada. This is a coming-of-age story, a story about adventure, and a story about the north.
