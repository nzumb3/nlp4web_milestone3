The Gospel According to Bart
###BREAK###
Nathaniel's boyfriend kills himself after a homophobic beating. Brokenhearted, Nathaniel hides behind the skirt of Ava, his best friend since high school, and contemplates asking her to marry him. The starring role she ropes him into in a church play an openly gay apostle named Bart helps him to find the courage to accept himself.
