Almost
###BREAK###
Armed with sexy starlet Trixie Hhart and the biggest budget in Australian history, director Zack Masters and producer Rocky Maldini set out to make their genre-busting Rock opera/ Action/ Adventure feature film... Heavy Metal. As Rocky says " It's got a car chase, a boat chase, a helicopter chase...lots of shots of the Harbour Bridge... Groundbreaking" Things don't go quite as planned. The luxury yacht they are filming on blows up taking out their entire budget and with it, Trip Wolf, their LA-based action man/rock star lead. Two options remain: crawl away and die Or find the creativity that has eluded them.
