Life on Wheels
###BREAK###
'Life on Wheels' features the life of Katie Condo, a 24 year old young women living with Cerebral Palsy. We learn what the disabilities means for her, see the struggles that she encounters on a daily basis while also exemplifying great achievements. Through out we learn of her incredible family who made her the women she is today, while paying homage to her father who passed away at a young age, greatly advance the struggle she already faces as a handicap individual. Katie is an incredible individual and her story will change the world.
