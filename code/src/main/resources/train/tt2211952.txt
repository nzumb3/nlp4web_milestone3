Los Boys
###BREAK###
Los Boys tells the history of a break-dance crew from a small rural town in Argentina. Their members are teenagers from a very complicated social environment. In 2010, after passing several tests and castings and against all odds, the reached the final of a famous Argentinian TV Talent show. They recorded the entire process with a small video camera, without any prior knowledge of the image, but with all sincerity, energy and spontaneity of their age. Today, back in town after the big experience, the continue doing what they do better: being young, naive and wild.
