Verticalmente al Sud
###BREAK###
The documentary tells about climbing in southern Italy. In an area without mountains, but with beautiful rock walls, under a sun that is always shining, the climbers have fun in the game of climbing. In recent years, they have formed a beautiful community. Within this group there are great athletes: their performance is shown by the sequence of the documentary. The shots were taken in outdoor and during races in k2 Indoor gym, in Putignano.
