For the Love of the City
###BREAK###
For the Love of the City is a music documentary which follows the rise, fall, eventual restoration of the New Orleans Jazz & Heritage Festival. In 2004 the once massively successful festival suddenly recedes into the flood waters of Katrina. The aftermath leaves the city and her Musicians searching for work, health care and a life of stability. The feature documentary features such musicians as Widespread Panic, Michael Franti and Spearhead, Galactic, Mickey Hart from the Greatful Dead, and more.
