The Uprising
###BREAK###
Days after breaking up with his girlfriend, Amar Hernandez goes on a date with his classmate, Riley Smith, a beautiful college girl. After a wild night of drinking, drugs and dub step music , Riley never makes it home. When news breaks that she is missing, Amar panics because he was the last person seen with Riley. When Ron "Murder" Manson, a racist LAPD cop is assigned to the case and discovers the body of a young white female, a man hunt ensues and Amar becomes the #1 suspect in the Riley Smith case. Desperate to escape his ill gotten fate, Amar has a choice, disappear off the face of the earth or uncover what really happened to Riley Smith.
