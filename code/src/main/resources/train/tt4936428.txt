The Unfortunate Faces of Portia Dupree
###BREAK###
A notable business man's daughter is found dead in their home. A homicide detective named Mark Spicer has three woman that have more than enough motive to make his case solid bringing a close to this unfortunate situation. However, the deeper he delves into the testimonies from these women he not only realizes that he has accused the innocent of this unspeakable crime but that the guilty party may very well be the VICTIM herself!
