Mr. Bond
###BREAK###
Mr. Bond, a police officer by profession has a record of bringing some of the toughest criminals to justice. Notorious criminal Dragon and his look-alike brother Daga begin abducting children and hold them as hostages so that he can demand huge sums of money from their parents for release. Mr. Bond takes on the case of apprehending Dragon and rescuing the hostages.
