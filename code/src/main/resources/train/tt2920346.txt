A Place to Call His Own
###BREAK###
Filmmaker Mike Krumlauf takes us on an experience like no other. Matt McMahon was your everyday guy, trying to find his place. When one choice changes the fate of his life, his best friend is left with overwhelming emotion and unanswered questions. Blurring the line between fact & fantasy, A Place To Call His Own takes us on a journey of life, death, & everything in between.
