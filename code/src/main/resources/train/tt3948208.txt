Double DI Trouble
###BREAK###
Double Di Trouble is a Punjabi movie directed by Smeep Kang and is based on Comedy of Errors, Shakespeare's well-known play, takes a Desi avatar when father-son pair discover their mirror images in a different city. The riot of laughter begins when the people around this duplicate duo keep confusing between the two! Starring the legendary Dharmendra alongside the reigning superstar, Gippy Grewal.
