The Camp
###BREAK###
Mumbai-based Sonali Mante along with bus driver, Jarnail Singh, and an assistant, Bhagwanlal, pick up several children for a camping trip near Karjat. After a misadventure with a live snake on board the bus, the group is instructed to turn over their cell-phones and jewelry items. Upon arrival on the camping grounds, they pitch their tents and get ready for the adventure. Soon they will find themselves in nightmarish conditions after they find out that they cell-phones are missing, and a wealthy child, Neil, is missing - believed to be kidnapped.
