Cat Fight
###BREAK###
Cat Fight follows Carbine Kitty, a media shy, all female rock band from the suburbs of northern Virginia; as they embark on their first multi-state tour. Cameras are allowed to roll 24/7, giving viewers a look at the band on the road as well as rehearsals, live stage performances and everything behind the scenes; for the first time ever! Unlimited access to the six women that make up Carbine Kitty helps delve into what makes them strong as individuals and as a unit, and documents their struggle as they try to maintain the balance between the privacy of their personal lives and the growing popularity of the band.
