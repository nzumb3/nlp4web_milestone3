My Blues and Greens
###BREAK###
My Blues and Greens is poetic portrait of the life and art of the best academy-trained Czech painter of his generation Stanislav Stanek. Employing an unconventional technique to biography, film captures the spirit of his work and the philosophy by which he lives his life through a series of stories and anecdotes in the artist's own words. The film marries image, music, and meaning to immerse the viewer In the mind of the artist, providing a poetic insight into artist;s own experience as he goes through the process of creating his work. Stanek is a victim of the dark communist era who triumphed over the politics of his time through his unstoppable love for the beauty of life.
