Salamandras y Salamandros
###BREAK###
A film painted on a canvas and a painting exhibition filmed. There are 60 artists from 14 countries, aged between 4 and 82 years. All have been trained in the workshop of the Salamandra, prestigious art school in Madrid. During one year, 240 portraits have been painted each other and 50 salamanders. In this film expose their work and reflect on Art and about themselves.
