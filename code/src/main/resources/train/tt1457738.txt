Building 173
###BREAK###
Building 173 takes viewers behind closed doors and into the rooms of 1 apartment building in downtown Shanghai to watch the stories of 7 families unfold over 3 generations. Our story follows the apartment building?s infamous owner and six of his tenants. Their stories of life, death and survival under one roof shed new light on China?s turbulent recent history.
