Winter in Peru: A Visual Ode to the Waves of Peru
###BREAK###
"WINTER IN PERU - A Visual Ode to the Waves of PERU" A surf documentary that explores some of the best surf breaks of Perú. This surfing experience took place during a 30 day trip to Perú, in the winter months of June and July of 2006. Along this film each surf break is presented with maps and information such as break type, wave heights, best swell-direction as well as actual surfing footage. This presentation showcases local talent on their local breaks, as well as the Ocean Riders team, towing and taming the huge waves of Pico Alto.
