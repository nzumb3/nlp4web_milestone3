Savannah Rose
###BREAK###
Civil rights activist Edna Jackson campaigns to be the first African-American Female mayor of the alluring southern city of Savannah, Georgia. This sassy, southern lady in fire-engine-red lipstick is not your average politician. More than an election story, the film is a manifesto on the power of positive thinking, consensus building and working as a collective to accomplish change.
