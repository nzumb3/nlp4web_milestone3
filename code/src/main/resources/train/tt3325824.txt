Third Reel
###BREAK###
Through a chance encounter Taylor, a former film critic, meets Shelly, a young woman 20yrs his junior, who shares his love of old movies and movie lines. They quickly form a connection through this love of the film medium, but this connection is tested when those around them begin to question this unusual friendship. Taylor has a choice, follow his heart or follow conventional thinking.
