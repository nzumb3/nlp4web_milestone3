9 Summers 10 Autumns
###BREAK###
When we were at the peak of success, is thought to go back home and leave all that has been achieved? Appointed on the true story, tells the story of 9 Summers 10 Autumns child transportation drivers in Batu, East Java, who live with economic limitations, but is able to transcend the dream and went on to become director of a multinational company in New York, United States. It all can be achieved thanks to the persistence, love and family togetherness. 9 Summers 10 Autumns is the inspiring story of courage through the boundaries of fear, for family, for love.
