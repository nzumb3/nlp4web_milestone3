Los Quijotes de la Marcha
###BREAK###
A social worker, a political activist and unemployed join the 15M Movement to march 500 km from Barcelona to Madrid on foot as repudiation of the socio-political status quo. Even though the 15M would eventually inspire several groups, such as Occupy Wall Street, those individuals who marched quickly learned about the difficulties inherent in changing the world while a unlikely fictional character follows them.
