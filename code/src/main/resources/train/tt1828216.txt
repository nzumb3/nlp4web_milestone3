Journey of Hope
###BREAK###
The story of a man with a huge and generous heart named Joseph Leone Introna, a New Jersey business man who dedicated a year of his life to helping and aiding the victims of the devastating and deadly earthquake in L'Aquila, Italy. Joe traveled throughout the United States raising funds, and made three trips to L'Aquila to purchase supplies desperately needed on a day-to-day basis by the over 40,000 earthquake victims living in tents. He is currently helping rebuild La Casa Famiglia Orphanage, which was destroyed in the quake.
