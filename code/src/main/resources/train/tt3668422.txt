I ljus och mörker Live
###BREAK###
"I Ljus & Mörker" is the biggest Swedish musical of 2012. The story deals with the darker side of humanity and draws parallels between the witch trials of the past and the cyber bullying of today. West End musical sensation Nina Söderquist, and film star Joakim Nätterqvist stars. The film is bookended by live performances.
