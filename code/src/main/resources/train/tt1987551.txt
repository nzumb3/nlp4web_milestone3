Deaf Me
###BREAK###
Deaf Me is a documentary film that examines the life of a deaf businessman who truly embodies the indomitable human spirit. He has seen the painful side of humanity, discrimination, violence, cruelty, humiliation, while trying to fit in with a hearing society that has given him every reason to give up. He still cares and gives back to the community. This is a documentary that gives the viewer a brief look into living life as a deaf person and the challenges many face because of communication barriers which will inspire the audience to think selflessly and appreciate his drive to reach his goals.
