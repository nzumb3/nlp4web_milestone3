El Desliz
###BREAK###
Martin is that typical guy who doesn't believe that anything special could happen in his life. Always someone else is having lucky success. His best friend Alejandro is an example of a lucky guy, with the perfect girlfriend and the best grades. Fifteen years of friendship seems to indicate that the situation will remain the same, however, Martin's life give an unexpected turn with one big choice: get involved more than necessary with Beatriz, Alejandro's mother.
