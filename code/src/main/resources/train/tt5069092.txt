Yasuni Man
###BREAK###
In the heart of Ecuador's Amazon a conflict rages. The world most biodiverse forest in the world is under threat by extractive industries and its people are caught between two world, where the new is erasing the old. Director and Producer, Ryan P. Killackey, takes you on an unforgettable expedition through Yasuni and reveals a cautionary tale about the impacts of human dependence on fossil fuels and it's impacts on our own human survival. How far would you go to save it all?
