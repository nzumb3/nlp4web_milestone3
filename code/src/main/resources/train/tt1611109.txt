Lost Tango
###BREAK###
Jota is a Brazilian living in London with Marianne, an enigmatic French woman. They live their lives independently of one another and rarely meet despite sharing the same flat. That is until Tereza, an old friend of Jota, returns to London and discover Jota's video diary. Marianne secretly accesses the video diary and embarks on a voyeuristic journey.
