Living with the Ingles
###BREAK###
Think your life can get hectic at times? Meet the Ingles, a family from Cincinnati, Ohio, with 14 children and a 15th on her way. With most of the children adopted from all over the world and having some form of physical or cognitive disability, the complexity and diversity of the Ingles bring new meaning to a truly 'modern family.' Watch as they face adversity and joy and ultimately become stronger as a family unit.
