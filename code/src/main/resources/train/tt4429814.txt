El Buquinista
###BREAK###
Lucien, an old books seller, bitter and antisocial, spends his days inside his bookstore, by which strange characters parade, along with his assistant, Casildo the dwarf. After few years, he had not recovered from the death of his wife and he is convinced that he can bring her back to life through the mathematic algorithms from a mysterious book he has found.
