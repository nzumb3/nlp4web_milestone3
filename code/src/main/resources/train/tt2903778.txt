Dracula 2012
###BREAK###
The film is a horror film which is based on Bram Stoker's famous novel Dracula. Set in South India, the film shows the arrival of dracula and the series of deaths thereafter. When the secret of dracula opens to a few Mantriks, they set out to search and destroy him but do they succeed in this endeavor forms the story of the film.
