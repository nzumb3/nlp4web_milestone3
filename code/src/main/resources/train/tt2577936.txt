Aziz Ayse
###BREAK###
A transvestite garbage picker, nicknamed Ayse, donates her earnings from waste-picking to charity, to some mosques and Turkish Military Foundation, in the expense of facing starvation. Ayse becomes part of a couple- Elif and Murat's life and triggers a personal change and development for both of them. Elif, a woman, learns to be herself from a man who is more of a woman than her.
