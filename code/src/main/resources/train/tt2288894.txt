Depression: The Movie
###BREAK###
Four characters have to wade through the disaster that is their lives to accomplish a greater goal and get past their biggest obstacle... Themselves... with hilarious results! Lenny is in love, with just about every woman he's met and he isn't shy about telling you. Bruce's father is destroying his life by doing nothing, is it possible to make someone stop doing nothing? Bill wants to figure out how to love after his divorce, but he's completely lost. George wants to prove she isn't a giant screw-up by babysitting for her successful older sister.
