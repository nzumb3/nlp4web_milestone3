In Your Orbit
###BREAK###
'In Your Orbit' is a film about friendship, forgiveness and life choices that includes animation and documentary elements as well as music, dance and visual art developed through a collaborative process. The story takes place in a triangle of connection and conflict between three friends arriving at the imperceptible moment when youth ends and adulthood takes hold, with all its inherent decisions and responsibilities. In the shifting ground of the relationships between the three friends, the main character Tabetha wants it all, but must come to understand what she can expect from the new circumstances, and where life is leading her.
