The Night of the Sunflowers
###BREAK###
After the discovery of the body of a sexually assaulted young girl hidden in a sunflower field, the horrible event is all over the news. The inhabitants of Cerredo, a small village in Spain, were living a simple lifestyle, until the unexpected discovery of an entrance to a cave, filled their hearts with expectation and dreams. While a crew from the Geology Department sent to investigate was inside the cave, an act of unprecedented violence was taking place. This will mark the beginning of a vicious cycle of violence, deception and greed, intertwining the stories as well as the lives of everyone involved.
