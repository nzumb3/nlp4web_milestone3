Life Locked in a Suitcase
###BREAK###
Despite her severe physical disability and discriminatory perception of many people, Olesia Kornienko, an award winning writer, poet and salsa dancer, strives to live her life to the fullest and dreams to become independent. But even with Olesia's zest for life, none of her dreams would come true without her guardian angle and a lodestar - her mom Tatiana.
