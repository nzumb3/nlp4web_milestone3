How I Became That Jewish Guy
###BREAK###
Struggling actor Jason Weinstein gets one last shot at fame as he aims for a spot on the hit reality show "Real Cultures." It's the audition of his career as he fights the inevitable fear of becoming just another faceless extra by recruiting his dysfunctional, reformed Jewish family and is challenged with the task of turning them into America's sweethearts. Will his family come through for him and give him the portrayal of a lifetime, or will Jason realize that the accolades he seeks lies within himself? It's a tale of acceptance, culture and chicken balls as Jason strives to become "That Jewish Guy."
