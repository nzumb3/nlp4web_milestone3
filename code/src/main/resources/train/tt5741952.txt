The New Doctrine
###BREAK###
'The New Doctrine' follows Adela Zhao, a college freshman and party girl who finds an unexpected roommate in Miriam Yoder. But in a world in which climate change looms and hallucinatory visions affect all members of society, Adela and Miriam are forced to act. 'The New Doctrine' sends a powerful message about friendship, faith, and morality.
