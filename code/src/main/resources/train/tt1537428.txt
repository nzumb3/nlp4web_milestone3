Capital: Impressions of Early Empire
###BREAK###
Capital: Impressions of Early Empire tracks film director, Leigh Morfoot, as she injects her cast and crew into the live setting of a heated, economic protest. Seven actors inhabiting the characters of a cross- section of Washington D.C. twenty-somethings improvise their actions and reactions as they navigate a city under siege. Capital raises questions concerning the impact of "Globalization".
