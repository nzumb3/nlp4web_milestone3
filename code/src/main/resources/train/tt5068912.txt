Vic Chesnutt: Everything I Say
###BREAK###
A former class president and all around funny man, Vic Chesnutt suffered partial paralysis after a 1983 drunk driving accident. Having started a video project in the mid 2000's,Scott Stuckey uses over 6 years of behind the scenes footage as well as interviews with friends, family, and associates to deconstruct the man that was to offer a new look into the life and times of Vic Chesnutt. Revered as a song-writer and musician by his peers (REM, Garbarge, Live, The Smashing Pumpkins, Madonna etc.) , events left him unknown to vast swaths of the music listening public. Follow this documentary is it takes you into the life of a complex and interesting character.
