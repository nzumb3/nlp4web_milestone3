Teen Kanya
###BREAK###
The story of the film revolves around the lives of three women - Aparna, Damini and Nancy. Aparna is a journalist. She suspects that her husband is having an affair with Damini, an IPS officer. Aparna's husband gets kidnapped. Aparna goes to meet Damini and a relationship between Aparna and Damini begins. Nancy is a call-girl who gets raped. She lodges a complaint to the local authority but it goes in vain. She then approaches a news channel where Aparna is a journalist, Aparna helps her to get justice.
