A Summer Prayer
###BREAK###
The documentary observes people living in Genbaku Home,which is a nursing home for aged Atomic-bomb victims.They put on re-enactment stages of the day of August 9,1945 in Nagasaki, to hand on their memories and prayers to peace not to be forgotten. The center of atomic-bombed in Nagasaki City was a Catholic church crowded area,and many Christians live there. They faced a tragic experience of Atomic-bomb but have overcome with prayers. Many bomb-victims are still suffering from aftereffect and dying day by day, but generation by generation the Christians come to churches to worship the face-only (other parts were burnt out) Ave Maria.
