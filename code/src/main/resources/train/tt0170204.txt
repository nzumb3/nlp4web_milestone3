Maisy's Garden
###BREAK###
Barry has everything a man could want: a lucrative law career, a loving wife, and a beautiful young daughter. But a strange encounter with a drifter changes his life forever. Suddenly, Barry can't leave his house. He stops showering, wears the same shirt every day, and cuts himself off from what used to be his world. Despite the best efforts of friends including his super-aggressive law partner, Barry reprioritizes his life, and in the process, loses everything he used to covet.
