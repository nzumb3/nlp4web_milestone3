Welcome Matt: An Unfortunately True Story
###BREAK###
On August 27, 2010 three violent attackers known as the Saint Agnes Brothers (Matt Sheppard, Kevin Chappell and Faith Buerger) went on a wild spree that included home invasion, murder and kidnapping. In less than 6 hours, the trio was responsible for nearly a dozen deaths in 3 cities across Central Kentucky in what many have come to call The Facebook Murders. In "WELCOME MATT", you see WHAT THEY DID and WHAT HAPPENED TO THEM. You also find out why... SOMETIMES YOU DON'T WANT TO KNOW WHO IS AT THE DOOR!!!
