Ups and Downs
###BREAK###
Everything looks to be great for Zach. It's his four year anniversary with his girlfriend Rachel and things could not be better. But one shocking surprise on their anniversary turns everything upside down for Zach and Rachel after Rachel cheats on Zach with neighborhood boy Tim Young. What follows is an adolescent journey through love, second chances and all of the ups and downs that life brings.
