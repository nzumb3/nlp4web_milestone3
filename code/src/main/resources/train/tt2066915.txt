Lost to Love
###BREAK###
Two former lovers--a 50-year-old man who lives in a run-down mobile home, and a 29-year-old housewife beleaguered by her middle-class Shangri-La--reunite after ten years apart. Both try desperately to change their present lives for the better by revisiting their past relationship and its enormous impact on them.
