Lagos_liv: Total Art
###BREAK###
Five artists, one year and a vibrant and crazy African mega city: 'Lagos Live' tells the story of Mudi, Sahar, Dennis, Jude and Segun. It's a demonstration of how powerful art can be in bringing people together and connecting continents. And it's an homage to the beautiful craziness of Africa's second biggest city.
