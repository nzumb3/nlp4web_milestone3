Bali: 1000 Faces of an Island
###BREAK###
This documentary leads you through the captivating heights of Gunung Batur to the hypnotic depths of some of the world's most selected dive locations. Visit truly quirky cites that could only belong to amazing Bali, witness the fascinations of time-honoured religious ceremonies and be enthralled by the many temple dances that form part of everyday Bali. A magical Island where vibrant life pulsates in every corner: Bali is now yours in a completely new form and unique quality.
