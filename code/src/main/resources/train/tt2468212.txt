The Healing Wisdom of Jamaica
###BREAK###
Everything you know about Jamaica is wrong. From the media stereotypes that Jamaicans just smoke weed and are criminals. This movie delves into the oral tradition of how it really is in Jamaica. From being vegan, resilient proud black people. Knowing ones routes and being independent. This documentary comes to life through stories told by elders of how they have survived and putting a positive spin on what it means to be Jamaican.
