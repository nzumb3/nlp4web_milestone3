Füße im Mund
###BREAK###
Katrin is a shining porn star. Directors go mad for her, she will do anything for the camera. Pushed from set to set by her manager, her only way of escape is by taking drugs. A chance encounter with her neighbor Bert sparks a friendship, as this inconspicuous character becomes a part of her life. Step by step, Katrin faces her limits in the ruthless porn industry. But where does she belong? What other life could she lead, since the porn circus is the only life she knows and the only family she has?
