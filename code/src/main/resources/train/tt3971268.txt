Man with a Thousand Voices: Rue Davis
###BREAK###
He has been called the 'man of a thousand voices' but the life of Rue Davis is not a Cinderella happy ending kind of story. Take a look at a most improbable music career that has spanned several years at Rue Davis has made one step forward with two steps back as we hear from those who knows him best. We take a look into the world of blues and R&B and the struggles Rue Davis has experienced. In the end, we see the raw talent to comes across and wonder why.
