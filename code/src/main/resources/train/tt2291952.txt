Black Power Revolution: Trinidad and Tobago 1970 - Let the Truth be Told
###BREAK###
This documentary is an account of the Black Power Revolution that took place in Trinidad and Tobago. The documentary, which is divided into two parts, seeks to document the lead up to the revolution, the revolution itself and the aftermath. In Part One the documentary will show the time line of this revolution starting from colonialism and emancipation to The February 26th Uprising.
