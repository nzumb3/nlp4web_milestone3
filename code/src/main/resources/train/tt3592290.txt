Can't Go Back
###BREAK###
Can't Go Back is a documentary about the exceptionally talented singer/songwriter Dan Raza. Dan has been on the cusp of stardom before. He has supported Bellowhead, been endorsed by Neil Young and even shared the stage with Badly Drawn Boy. However Dan is the 'nearly man' who delivers leaflets for a living, to fuel his passion for music. This film follows Dan's fascinating journey, examines the music industry and showcases his undiscovered talent.
