The Surfing Messiah
###BREAK###
A portrait of George, a man convinced, as many millions are, that the world as we know it will come to an end on 21 December 2012. But unlike the legions of other believers, George is convinced that he has been chosen to survive the apocalypse. That he has been given a mission to ensure the survival of the human race. The filmmakers' initial goal was to portray George's preparations for the coming catastrophe. They didn't expect him to attempt to make them a part of his mission. And this changes the course of the story...
