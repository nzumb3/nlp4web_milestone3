Draga gospa ilacka
###BREAK###
The film epitomizes the metaphysics and mystics of the sacral, and man's earthly course in search for the meaning of life. The theme of pilgrimage reveals the history of the Croatian region, the appearance of the sacred water through the Blessed Virgin Mary, and links this shrine to Lourdes. Filmed on authentic locations it records the statements of eyewitnesses, documentary material, and ambient beauty of this area of Croatia. The score is based on original traditional instruments and melody.
