Faerie!

A Faerie has been assigned the mission to rescue Charles and Debbie from their mental turmoil here on earth. And it couldn't have come at a better time! Through the faerie's whimsical teachings, both will embark on a spiritual journey that will gain them the realization that they can attract the greatest joys and opportunities into their life as long as they are in control of their minds.
