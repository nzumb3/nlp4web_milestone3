The Kapu Kai
###BREAK###
The Kapu Kai restaurant, night club and bowling alley once thrived on historic Route 66 in Cucamonga California. It lived and died in the swinging sixties only to be reborn decades later on the Internet and in peoples memories. This is a nostalgic look back to a simpler time, and how a simple search on the Internet led to a re-discovery of our family history.
