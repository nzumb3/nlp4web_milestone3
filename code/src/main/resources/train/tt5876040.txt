Cataract Gold
###BREAK###
With the help of Zachariah Jones, Beth and Katie Clark set out to find a secret their father took to his grave. In a fight through the rugged badlands of the wild west they set off on a journey with the only map in existence the one map their father Jeremiah Clark made and hid while on a government expedition carrying a load of Government gold and mapping out the Green and Colorado rivers in 1869. Will they make the 40 day journey or will they fall victims to the Wilcox gang who are tracking their every move.
