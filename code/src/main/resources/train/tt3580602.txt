Saute qui peut!
###BREAK###
Charles Lacombe, private detective, is about to conclude an investigation that will put some of Montreal's most important criminals behind bars. However,he is also planning to end his own life as a way to get back at his wife who just left him. While meticulously preparing his suicide, he is suddenly surprised and interrupted by a very special gift delivery woman who is also about to ruin the plans of several other dark characters involved in this story.
