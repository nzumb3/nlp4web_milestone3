Lee Rock III
###BREAK###
The final part to the Lee Rock trilogy. Lee Rock's rise to power with the corruption of the Hong Kong Police is about to crumble beneath him. With the discovery of him being a father of a uncorruptable cop and his dying first love, can Lee Rock manage to keep from being swallowed by the new Police Officials, save his former flame and become a father to his long forgotten son?
