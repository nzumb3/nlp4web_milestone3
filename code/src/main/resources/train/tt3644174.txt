Pokhabovsk. Obratnaya storona Sibiri
###BREAK###
The story revolves around a team of professionals in the field of public relations and advertising. The client of the team is one of the most influential businessmen in Pohabovsk. He gives them a very hard assignment, and the odds of the team's success are pretty much 'Mission: Impossible'. However, tough challenges are the ones that are most interesting - especially to the audience.
