The Chaos Within
###BREAK###
My wish is to investigate the intricate character that is my mother; An orphan who had experienced on her own flesh the horrors of the Holocaust and now is devoted solely to saving humanity from another world war through spreading kabbalah. And on the other hand, I, her son, am trying to collects the shattered pieces of my past and save my own personal world. Through viewing mine and Mothers' lives I discover an impossible story that has to be told. The film is put together from shards of my life. Fragments of chaos, kabbalah, art and redemption. That meld together to create the impossible: a Home.
