Am I Don Quixote?
###BREAK###
When his wife and partner of 30 years leaves him and their circus life, legendary horseman Carlos Svenson's life and career unravel. Seizing one last opportunity, Carlos embarks on a fantastical quest to win the part of the fabled knight Don Quixote. Set in the stunning world of old-tradition European circus, Carlos' journey is an exploration of love and loss - and a lyrical meditation on the subjective nature of reality.
