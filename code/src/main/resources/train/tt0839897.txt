The Main Character
###BREAK###
A man has a crush on a woman. Only problem? He is a third-class, good-for-nothing human being. One day he is given an opportunity of his life time: Give up his body, and he can be with her forever. However, that opportunity turns out to be a tragic manipulation. When he finds out the truth, he has to make another decision. Will he compromise with the world, or will he be able to save the love of his life?
