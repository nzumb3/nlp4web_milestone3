True Story
###BREAK###
Mark has no job, lives with his mom and has a psycho girlfriend and she just got pregnant. He wants to leave her, but she won't let him. Jimmy is a cop who has marriage problems and breaks the law occasionally. Jerry is a whipped husband who seems to always have a lot of money even though he fixes air conditioners. Junior is a part time worker who pursues single moms. Johnny is a married man who seems to be the only one who has his stuff together. Put them altogether and you have one damn good true story.
