A Place Like This
###BREAK###
When Roy Faamatala is declared bankrupt and struck with a heart condition, his world turns round overnight. Going from the 5 star world of Tokyo elite, he finds himself on the streets in the heart of Sydney's homeless community. Staying at one of the shelters, he follows the stories of several unique individuals, including Gabriel the reformed drug dealer, Maria the Chilean Immigrant and Krystal the daughter of an Ice Addict. Throughout Roy's investigation, the Matthew Talbot Homeless Services are the catalyst to each of the homeless clients in giving a voice and validating their stories. Roy recruits these characters and listens to their stories as he starts to uncover the many faces of homelessness...
