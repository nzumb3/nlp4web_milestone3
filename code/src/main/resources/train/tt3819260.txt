Unwinding
###BREAK###
Glenda Powell won the 2006 World Championship for Distance Casting. All the rivers that she has fished since childhood are etched into her memory. She recalls her late father and finding solace on the river after he died. Now she prefers to teach on the Blackwater River in Ireland rather than compete. Life on the water and the beautiful places she has fished have brought contentment.
