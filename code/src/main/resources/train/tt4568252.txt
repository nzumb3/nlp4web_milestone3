Ivan Maryadaraman
###BREAK###
The lead character Raman, enacted by Dileep, is an employee in a rice mill in Pune. He used to carry the rice to the mill for processing on a bicycle. Later he decides to buy an auto rickshawand goes to his village to borrow money from his mother to buy his dream vehicle. But once he reaches his village he is shocked to find a gang of people waiting there to kill him.The rest of the event forms the crux of the plot.
