This film is about the varied and extraordinary life of a banker descended from a family of bankers -Boris Skarlatov, in the years 1912 -1944. It includes rise and fall; shady and suspicious deals in capitalism's initial stages. A story is pervaded by love and hatred, dominated by strong men and beautiful women, intrigues and adventures.
