Mamma's Trilogy
###BREAK###
Mamma's Trilogy is a film made up of three continuous short stories. Mamma's Heart, Mamma's Respect & Mamma's Boy Mamma's Trilogy is about a character that is tormented by his mother's ways. Follow his footsteps as he makes his family and friends victims of his twisted killings, taking his bizarre situations to another level and dimension. Expect the unexpected!
