Soul of the Earth
###BREAK###
Four friends reach the darkest point in their lives some in the crux of disease, misery or are near suicide. Instead of taking the easy way out, they make a pact to face death, head on, by abandoning themselves in a wilderness of their choice somewhere on the Earth for 30 days. The test is to face the climate and soul search to replenish their broken spirits.
