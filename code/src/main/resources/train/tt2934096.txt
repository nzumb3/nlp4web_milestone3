Behind the Gate
###BREAK###
This film looks at the challenge of examining the changes in the Horse Racing Industry. It takes a long look at the power and beauty behind the greatest athletes in the world. Ultimately we discover many secrets of both the dynasty and the state of the Sport of Kings. With narration and overview from Academy Award Winning Actor, Joe Pesci, along with superstars of the sport, including Oscar De La Hoya, Bob Baffert, Doug O'Neill, Laffit Pincay, Gary Stevens, Ron McAnally and many others; the excitement and passion of the sport are showcased in a way never before caught on film.
