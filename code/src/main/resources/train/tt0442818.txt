Meet the Parrot
###BREAK###
Majid (Mohammad Reza Forutan) and Nasim (Mahtab Karamati) seem to be a very good and happy couple. Majid Works for his father in law but after a while he becomes suspected to his strange behavior. As Nasim's father finds out that Majid (Mohammad Reza Forutan) is suspected to his illegal activities, he makes a plot to put Majid down. In a set up Majid becomes involved in a murder case and eventually he becomes the prime suspect, trailed and convicted to death. In the mean time Nasim, does whatever she can to save Majid and stop the execution order. Finally after she looses hope to save Majid through the Law, she recruits 3 females criminal and make a plan to hijack his convicted husband's convoy which suppose to take him from jail to the execution place.
