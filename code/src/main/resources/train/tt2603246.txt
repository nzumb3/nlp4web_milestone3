No Trespassing
###BREAK###
A true life 'Winter's Bone' documentary capturing life as it is today in the Ozarks, this captivating film draws you into the cycle of poverty, abuse and addiction in the exact same region in which 'Winter's Bone' was filmed. See into the real life stories of people trapped in a cycle from which there is little hope of escape. Documentary - reality based film with original, compelling footage.
