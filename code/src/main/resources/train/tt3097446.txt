The French Dip: A tale from Los Angeles
###BREAK###
Two restaurants in Los Angeles claim to have originated one of the most famous deli sandwiches in the United States. Up until this day both restaurants hold on to the title originator of the French Dip. This short doc explores who is the originator of the French Dip. A short doc.
