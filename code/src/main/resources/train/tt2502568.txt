Cheaters

While he maintains a sexual relationship with Ji-yeon (Da-mi Ko), she has been involved with his colleague Young-soo for quite a long time. All however have feelings for others and secretly pursue other interests. Unaware of their indirect interrelations, all involved are invited to a concert...
