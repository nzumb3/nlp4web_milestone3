Novak
###BREAK###
Lucia arrives to Buenos Aires with a mission, take care of a Hungarian filmmaker who's invited to present his film at the Buenos Aires Independent Film Festival. Lucia meets Pablo, a guy who introduces himself as the angel of famous Hungarian filmmaker, Bela Tarr. Lucia and Pablo will join Laszlo Novak in a tragic adventure, moving heaven and earth to find his lost film.
