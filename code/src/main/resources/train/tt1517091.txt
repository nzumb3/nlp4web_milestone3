Havana Kidz 2
###BREAK###
Enter the lives of a teenage band in Havana, Cuba. The intricate lives of these teenagers and the complexities they have to endure will grip you to the screen. You will experience their hopes and career goals for their future while you share their perspective on what lies ahead. Scenes from their rehearsals, pep talks, and discussions provide a very up-close and personal look into the lives of these musicians. While the teens provide a unique insight into Havana's soul and cultural diversity, the city's backdrop and musical sounds play an important part in the film and sets the tone in every scene. You will find that what Cuba lacks economically, it makes up in culture, color and zest for life when viewed through the eyes of these charismatic, confident and savvy teens.
