Everybody Cheats
###BREAK###
Everybody Cheats is about human imperfection as it reveals temptation s of everyday fact of life. In the opening scene we get a glimpse of infidelity as one of the main characters catches his fiancee in the act with another man, which changes his views on relationship thereafter. Meanwhile, Shirley and Ricky cannot have a stable relationship because of suspicion of each other. Is it possible for anyone to live in a world where the order of the day is to cut corners, fudge number on taxes, witness infidelity, and still remain true to one another? The challenges abound. At the end we can ask ourselves, 'Does everybody cheat? Therein lies the mystery.
