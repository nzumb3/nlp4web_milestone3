Gypsy Boys
###BREAK###
A look at the intertwined lives of a group of young gay men in San Francisco and their search for love. It focuses on two couples: Steven and Blair, and Manny and Aaron. Steven and Blair are a couple, but can't agree on how much of a relationship they want. Manny and Aaron are just good friends. Manny wants more, but Aaron is staying true to a long-distance relationship.
