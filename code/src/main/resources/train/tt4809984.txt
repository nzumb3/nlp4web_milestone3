Hot Sand
###BREAK###
Being effected by ambition of Tuyet who is looking for a location to build a dock to modernize her resort, the peaceful life of Cat's sisters in a poetic sandy land are broken. When Cat's house which is also the shelter of Dongs - a kind of reptile living in the sand, are going to be burned, the Dong are under the threat of extinction, they begins a revenge.
