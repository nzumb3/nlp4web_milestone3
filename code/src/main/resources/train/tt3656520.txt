Oasis Refinado
###BREAK###
In 2001 starts the Restoration of the Primera de Palos Lagoon (Huelva, Spain). A Refinery converted this wetland into a declared Natural Area, a true oasis for the flora and especially for its birdlife. His Biologist, Javier Camacho, explains how he has performed this actions and the challenges ahead.
