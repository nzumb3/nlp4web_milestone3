###BREAK###
Raymond White-Sanchez is an ambitious musician bent on living out the rest of his life as a professional trumpet player. Full of enthusiasm, he moves to the city in pursuit of his dream, but when he discovers that this life wasn't as easy as he thought, the enthusiasm turns to frustration. His passion turns to destruction. Raymond eventually becomes a homeless bum begging for pocket change, searching through dumpsters, and raging through the streets.
