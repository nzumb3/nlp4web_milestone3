Lianmanting
###BREAK###
Yang Shufen who works as a Chinese traditional costume designer suffers from the articular inflammation because of her career. One day she receives a parcel from her daughter which contains a tablet PC with Falun Gong (also called Falun Dafa) materials and the documentary 'Nine Commentaries on the Communist Party'. She recovers from her illness after practicing Falun Gong. While she watches the documentary 'Nine Commentaries on the Communist Party', she realizes the evil persecution of the Chinese Communist party.
