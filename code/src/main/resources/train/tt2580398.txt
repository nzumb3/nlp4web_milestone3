From Queens to Cairo
###BREAK###
Egyptian American filmmaker Sherif Sadek goes to his native Cairo with his family one year after the start of the Egyptian Revolution. With tons of misinformation flying around in the media, Sadek is determined to see for himself how Egyptians will navigate the bumpy road to democracy, especially after decades of authoritarianism. His journey takes him from famed Tahrir Square to the inside of taxi cabs to rallies to slums as he searches for perspectives from all walks of life.
