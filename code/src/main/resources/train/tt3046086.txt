The Love Stairs
###BREAK###
A true story, with true people. A touching and revealing road movie, 'The Love Stairs' tells the amazing story of this devoted man who carved 6,000 steps through the rocks with his own hands over 50 years, only to have his beloved wife reaching home safely. The film follows Hong Kong pop band C AllStar through the mountains of South China, in search of the legendary craft of a man that shows the true value of romance. Entertaining, touching and meaningful.
