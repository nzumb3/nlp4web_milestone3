Borderlands
###BREAK###
This is the story of Cahuilla artist Gerald Clarke's return to the Cahuilla reservation in Southern,CA. Gerald's performance art, satirizing television shows (e.g. Antiques Road Show and Extreme Makeover) highlight the dilemmas and incongruities of Native American life today is prominatly featured in the film, along with interviews of Cahuilla tribal members, including elders, that center on the themes of mixed blood, adaptation to reservation life, and cultural identity.
