###BREAK###
"Goals for Girls" follows the struggle of a group of girls from the infamous Villa 31 shantytown in Buenos Aires who want to play a sport that is off limits to women in Argentina: soccer. In their efforts to forge a real team, these young woman ages 13 - 16 battle constant, deep-rooted sexism from their own families, confront the boys who don't respect their rights to use the field, navigate the prejudice of the society at large that sees them as merely slum dwellers, and learn responsibility and the value of teamwork.
