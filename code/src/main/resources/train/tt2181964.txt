Troubled Rivers
###BREAK###
A story of friendship and revenge. Two friends, opposite poles, which will ultimately faced in a wild and bleak context. In a post-apocalyptic future world, the silent "Largo" and talking "Candolini" wandering aimlessly through the deserted roads of the cities destroyed. They have future ambitions, much less objective. But an unexpected event will shift to their lives and their relationship become a cat and mouse game with unpredictable consequences.
