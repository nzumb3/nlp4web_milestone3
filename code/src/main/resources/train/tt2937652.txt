Something Wicked This Way Comes
###BREAK###
SOMETHING WICKED THIS WAY COMES is an exploration into New England horror. The film dissects popular conventions within the genre and identifies how they're driven by the history, eerie settings, and social issues of the area. And through the stories of local actors and filmmakers, as well as discussions with genre luminaries, horror fans, and New England natives, it will also help us to discover if the area's history and passion are strong enough to help grow an independent film industry.
