Nightmare
###BREAK###
Our young protagonist, Henry Sorin, undergoes lucid nightmares relating not only to his abusive past but a strange cloaked deity within an abandoned house. The story is told through the dark realm of Henry's nightmares and his cold, bleak life in reality; as the disturbances become too much for him to take, the truth is slowly exposed, and Henry can do naught but come to terms with the horror of reality. Reality protects you. But not for long. Nightmare will scare audiences of all ages with its expert use of tension and power of suggestion, and is backed by a strong, character driven story that will continuously engage and surprises audiences.
