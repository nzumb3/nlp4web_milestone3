Love, Love: The Stories of Han Hie-jak
###BREAK###
An omnibus featuring the short stories of Han Hie-hak. Story 1: Michineun dalbeom--A woman with a wild past tries to pass herself off as a virgin to marry a wealthy man but she finds herself being tempted to accept the offers of a would-be seducer offering expensive jewels for her attentions. Story 2: Dalkomhan bulsangsa--A man who has loved a woman from afar finally gets his chance to be with her but the experience is not at all like his fantasies. Story 3: Jaesu eobtneun nal--a feminist turns herself in to the police for raping a man in order to draw attention to the women's rights movement
