Raja Kumarudu
###BREAK###
After the passing away of his parents, Raja lives with his uncle, Dhanraj, in Khandala, Maharashtra, India, where Dhanraj manages a hotel, while Raja is a model. While in Khandala, he meets with Rani, a guest from Punjab, and both fall in love with each other. Raja informs Dhanraj, who does not approve of Rani, and asks Raja to forget about her and marry his daughter instead. Will Raja do his uncle's bidding?
