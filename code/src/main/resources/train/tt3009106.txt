New Project 'Zengin Sinifin Dizi Dibinde'
###BREAK###
Spring of 2013, Istanbul in the midst of youth upheavals, Iskender, living with his daughter after the departure of his wife, earns a living by translating books. Metin Arik, a friend from his high-school years, is a nuclear physicist and a university instructor on cosmology. Iskender is attracted to a woman of fifty, managing a stationery shop. She takes lessons on yoga, looks after his son of 10, and tries to evade her ex-husband whom she has divorced but who is still after her. Metin's son disappears in Istanbul and he is left with no peace of mind to follow his studies and tries to find his son among the young street musicians and Gezi Park.
