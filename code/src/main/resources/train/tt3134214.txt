Kadal Kadannu Oru Maathukutty
###BREAK###
Maathukutty (Mammootty), an unemployed Keralite settled in Germany, is given a chance to organize their association's annual event. The association sends him to India to invite superstars to their event. Maathukutty gets in trouble as the stars refuses the invitation due to some reasons and when his friend gets into a criminal case, unfortunately. How can he escape from these troubles?
