Where Time Stops
###BREAK###
They escaped the hustle and bustle of civilization and the treadmill of capitalism. In a village in the French Pyrenees, cut off from the world, Jens and Micky from Germany found what they desired the most: freedom, peace and close contact with nature. Their idyll ended a few years ago when tourists fascinated with esoterism began to frequent the area. The aim of their pilgrimages was the holy mountain of Pic de Bugarach. In December 2012, before the announced end of the world, thousands of pilgrims came to visit the place.
