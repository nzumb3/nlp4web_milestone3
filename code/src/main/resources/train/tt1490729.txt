King of the Hamptons
###BREAK###
When mid-life hits ordinary guy Dennis Lynch, he decides to take an adventure into the Hamptons and make a documentary about it. Leaving his three kids and pregnant wife behind, Dennis spends his weekends with A-list Hamptonite, Dan Rattiner, Founder of the Hamptons largest magazine, Dan's Papers. Rattiner acts as chaperone by helping guide Lynch through the various doors, big and small, that make up the very diverse and fascinating community.
