Gatão de Meia Idade
###BREAK###
In Rio de Janeiro, the forty years old middle-class divorced designer Cláudio spends his leisure time with his teenage daughter Duda or having middle-age crisis, affair with women, chatting with his friends or visiting his mother. When his former wife Beth dates the wealthy Aurismar that is moving to Miami, Cláudio tells her that the guy might be a drug or arm dealer, and tries to convince Duda to stay with him in Rio. Meanwhile, he recalls his former girlfriends and has new love relationships with the teenager Patricinha, with the married executive Marisa Delmar and with his former girlfriend Sandrão.
