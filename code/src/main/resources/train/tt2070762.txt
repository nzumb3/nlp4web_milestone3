One June Afternoon
###BREAK###
Grace is a young woman struggling with grief after a series of dramatic events. While attempting to move forward and re-connect with friends, she finds herself drawn towards a haunted young man. The two strangers find comfort in their new found friendship as they finally learn to let go of their pain and quickly embrace the special bond that is growing between them. Complications arise however and relationships are put to the test when acts of deceit, painful secrets and true feelings are finally revealed.
