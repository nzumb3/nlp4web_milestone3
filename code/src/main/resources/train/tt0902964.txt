Three Fellas
###BREAK###
Back in the day, Jung Kwon and his two buddies Sung Hyun and Kyung Ro were renowned around the riverbank behind school as the "No Touch Gang". Kyung's mouth was scarier than his fists and Sung Hyun lacked experience in fighting despite trying really hard, but Jung Kwon had the charisma and superb fighting skills. As the years pass, things change for them all. Work, family, and life get in the way, and even the riverbank is changing. Now it's not just fights between students anymore; organized crime is starting to make a mark in the area. Will the trio be able to go back to their glory days of youth?
