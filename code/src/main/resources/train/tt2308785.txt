Just(ice) in Italy
###BREAK###
Three journalists have entered 8 different prisons to collect eyewitness reports and images and to interview prisoners, governors, therapists, psychologists, chaplains and other prison officers, all members of a suffering prison system that collapsed long ago. A journey that explores a remote land where no camera ha s been before and where thousands of people are crammed beyond the limits of the law.
