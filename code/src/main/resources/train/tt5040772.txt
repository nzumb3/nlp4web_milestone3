Kaamdev to Karrmyogi
###BREAK###
Ranjeet Chaddha, middle aged, is a 'C' grade film maker. He works on the principal of 'Give & Take'. He believes that everything has a price, and therefore, gives roles to males only after taking money from them, and to females, after having sex with them. He also fantasizes with any and every women he comes across in his daily life. During auditions for one of his movie, he meets a young girl called Mini who refuses to compromise and have sex with him, but she somehow manages to get a major role in his movie. An untoward incident happens during shoot of his movie, which changes his life. What happens to Mini and how does he manage his future course of life? It's the life journey of a sex maniac which you need to be watch to know.
