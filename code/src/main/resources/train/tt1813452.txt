Leave It on the Stage
###BREAK###
Winning the top award at a national competition might seem like the highest possible achievement for the Amazing Technicolor Show Choir. As the next year unfolds, though, this Omaha show choir continues to pursue excellence in singing and dancing. They start out the year as a group of talented individuals, but they have quite a distance to go to be their best as a group. From auditions through the competition season, the 46 students of ATSC push the limits of performance as they figure out what it means to leave it on the stage.
