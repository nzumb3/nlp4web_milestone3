Sexually Frank
###BREAK###
A young nurse is pressured to get married and have children. Her first and only boyfriend of ten years makes a hilariously inappropriate Internet video. A gay friend gets dumped for throwing his cat off the bed. Their best friend is a balding, 24-year-old virgin, and quickly becoming a 'creepy old man.' Sex is the theme in this ensemble piece, in which stigmas are challenged and a group of friends bare all.
